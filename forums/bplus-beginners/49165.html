CS_TBL(Posted 2005) [#2]
timer=CreateTimer(60)

Repeat
  WaitEvent()
  If EventId()=$803 quit=true

  If EventID()=$4001 ; timer-event
  ; your stuff here
  EndIf
Until quit

in case you have more timers:

If EventID()=$4001 ; timer-event
  If EventSource()=timer
  ; your stuff here
  EndIf
EndIf
