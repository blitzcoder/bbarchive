Kevin_(Posted 2005) [#2]
Two possibilities....

1. Create your window off screen with a Y coordinate bigger then the height of your desktop. Then move it into place when your gadgets have been created.

2. Use hidegadget after opening your window. Build the rest of your gadgets then use showgadget.

Hope that helps.
