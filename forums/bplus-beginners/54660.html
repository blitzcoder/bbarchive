CGV(Posted 2005) [#2]
The problem is you've misunderstood the use of while/wend.

Replace this:
While KeyDown(205)
charx=charx +1
Wend


with this:
If KeyDown(205) Then
charx=charx +1
Else If KeyDown(203) Then
charx=charx -1
EndIf


and that should solve your problem.
