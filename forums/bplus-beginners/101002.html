For the 2nd part, as long as it runs on the version of Windows installed (can not verify compatibility with 7 or 8 and beyond) it matters not if it is a CRT an LCD, OLED, a HDTV or VGA overhead projector for those who stumped up the ridiculous amount for one, as long as its a valid screen resolution.

Same applies to the cable between pc and monitor (VGA DVA HDMI), as long as both the monitor and PC work and are talking to each other anything in the monitors and video cards display range should work no issues.
