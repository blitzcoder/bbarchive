Hello everyone!

Please, check out my new game!
It's a tapper/clicker RPG. It's fun and (I believe) addictive.

It's still in dev-on-the-run mode =), which means it's published, but I still have work to do, and I will release updates often as they go.

I would like to share the current version of the game with you.
BTW, this pre-release is only Monkey community exclusive ;) I trust you guys to see the beginning of my work, and I'll appreciate to see your first impression.
I have my little server to gather simple statistics, so I hope I'll see how you're going through the game.

What is done:
-Few first bosses give you some abilities.
-3 potions to craft
-5 swords, 5 amulets, 5 rings to wear
-5 locations with its own monsters

It's about 3 hours of gameplay until you bored (at least I was bored after that time), but I will add more!

What will be done in nearest future
-More everything (locations, bosses, abilities, potions, items to wear)
-Leaderboard
-Ads (video, to receive free diamonds)
-IAPs (of course)

WARNING!!!:
There's a cheat that will reset the game to its start.
Hold your finger in the right corner and touch the left corner, and the game will reset.

Hold your finger in the left upper corner to see some hidden data. (The number is the enemies' health)

Thank you for the interest and happy playing!
-Alex
