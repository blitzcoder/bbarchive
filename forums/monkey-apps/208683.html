Gerry Quinn(Posted 2014) [#7]
There are ads and ads. The AdMob issue as reported was different, but it could be that Google's attitude was affected by the way ads were presented, I don't know.

I think probably nobody really minds banner ads. Static click-away interstitial ads are probably okay IF not too often - they are a bit annoying but that can be a selling point for the pro version. Also, banner ads are a poor fit for arcade-style games - too easy to hit by accident (which AdMob obviously object to), and you can't stop in the middle of jumping around to click on one. So interstitial is probably necessary for those.

Video ads, though... now you're verging towards 'enraged delete' territory...
