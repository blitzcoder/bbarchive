muddy_shoes(Posted 2011) [#2]
Eh?

* The Box2D site hosts a manual that even has a section with a "Hello World" example.

* The MonkeyBox2D project includes a demo folder that contains examples of pretty much everything Box2D does.

* If you just Google "box2d tutorial", you'll find dozens of tutorials across the web.

As OS projects go, box2d is among the best in terms of being documented and supplied with tutorials. There's very little difference between the Monkey port and the other versions, particularly its direct Flash parent, so there's nothing stopping you using all the resources out there.
