No experience with these particular services but $10/month seems pretty good to me for handling all the backup/redundancy/connectivity etc. issues. The question is always just how reliable they'll be and what sort of limits and price ramping are involved if you have the fortunate problem of getting a lot of users. I can't see anything that states what Kumulos considers to be "fair use" for example or pricing options that allow for heavier use than their base deal.

I'd just be sure that they allow you to get your data back (and preferably any server-side code specific to your app too) so that you have the option to migrate to another service or to your own solution.
