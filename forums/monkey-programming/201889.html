benmc(Posted 2011) [#2]
Make sure your image is originally an even number in width and height, like 512x128 and see if that helps. Not 512x123 (for example)

I'm not sure which target you are using, but if you are using Floats to position and resize your image, you may want to cast the final position/size as Int() in your OnRender to see if that helps as well.

I don't know why, but I also experienced blurriness in some images and this helped me. Otherwise, I'm not sure :(


benmc(Posted 2011) [#3]
Oh, and whenever you "enlarge" an image anyway, it will probably blur because it has to make certain pixels larger - unless maybe you are starting with a much larger image, displaying it smaller, and then resizing it larger on the screen - tho it is still smaller than the original. If you are doing this, you would probably want all your resizes to resize from the original instead of the previously shrunken image.
