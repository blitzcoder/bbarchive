Greetings! My App MonKonfig is in Beta. I am posting to let you guys know about it.

MonKonfig allows you to easily configure the CONFIG.TXT files in your Targets folders. Editing manually can be a pain especially when changing projects and this is where MonKonfig comes in to play.

Beta Demo Downloads for Mac, Windows, Linux can be found at the links below.

All targets are implemented except for stdcpp atm. In these beta demo's you can only export and select from html5 and flash.

Screenies and download links below:
