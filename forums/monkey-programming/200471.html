What I do is just churn the CPU doing a lot of calculations for a bit, and then take Millisecs():

Class MyApp extends App
  Method OnCreate()
    For Local c=1 to 100000000
      Sqrt(c)
    Next
    Seed = Millisecs()
  End
End
