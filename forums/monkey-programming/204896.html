You really just gotta tweak. I have a friction variable in my EndlessScroller class, which is applied to a momentum that's carefully controlled and low-end clamped so the scroller doesn't feel sluggish or overly slippery. The code's part of a framework, but it's mostly modular, if you want a copy and are willing to figure it out for yourself. Here's a demo to see if you're interested: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FJ7gPRSHMJg

As for iOS scrollers, I always found them a little too "sticky" compared to Android's, so my class might feel a little "slippery" to you (that being said, it stops on a dime when you tap!)
