jhans0n(Posted 2011) [#2]
Yes, my game Senet, which is a BlitzMax game, is on the Mac App Store.

Besides the Intel Mac w/the latest xcode, you need to join Apple's development program for $99. After that, just make sure your app follows their guidelines, use their manual packaging instructions, and you should be good to go.

The developer site is developer.apple.com, by the way.
