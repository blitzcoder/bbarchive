Having written a game in javascript/php/html earlier this year (see http://mattiesgames.com/gorgoth/) I've found there are a few things you have to be careful of when writing for online.

Firstly, putting all your graphics into a single image is vital if you have lots otherwise the pc will spend all its time downloading each image.

Secondly, music should be streamed if possible.
