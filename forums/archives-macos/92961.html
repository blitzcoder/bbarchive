xlsior(Posted 2010) [#4]
When dealing with a stuck key, just hit every key on the keyboard a few times, and it may unstick it.
Depending on the cause of the problem, it may or may not fix it, and the issue may come back.

It could literally be a sticky key that's stuck halfway down making a connection, or it could be something more low-level like a short-out in a circuit board that makes it look like a key is pressed down.
