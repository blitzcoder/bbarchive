Also, to rebuild modules in Blitzmax 1.50, in fl_draw.cxx (fltkmaxgui.mod/src folder), you have to move this line:

#define min(a,b) ((a)<(b)?(a):(b))


to beneath:

#include <math.h>


in order to compile the fltkmaxgui module successfully on recent linux distros.
