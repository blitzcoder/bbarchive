Maybe it's bad form to reply to your own posts? Anyhow, the following blog post fixed it for me - the key bit is

"My problem was solved when I went to the project properties, under Linker then Manifest File, change Generate Manifest to No."

Recompiling then worked..

http://www.mikazo.com/2012/03/solve-mtexe-errors-in-visual-studio.html

Hope this helps someone someday..
