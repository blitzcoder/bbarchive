TournamentBlitz.com Event Managment System SDK for Blitz3D by Techlord2004
What is the TournamentBlitz Event Management System Software Development Kit?
The TBEMS SDK is a Blitz3D Code library that adds TournamentBlitz.com Services
to Games developed with Blitz3D, BlitzPlus & BlitzMax (coming soon). Any Game with a Score

System can take advantage off the following services:

* Online Scoreboard
* Prize-Based Skill Game Tournaments
* Cash-Based Skill Game Tournaments
* Pay2Play
* In-Game Commerce
* In-Game Advertisement Coop
* TBNet Server Locator

The advantage: Use Free Services to draw new and seasoned players to your website. Use cash-based services to generate additional profit streams from your software.

Heres a brief overview of each service.

With the Online Scoreboard you can display Player Top Scores on your website. Freely stored on our servers courtesy of TournamentBlitz.com.

With Skill Game Tournament Service your players can compete in both single player and multiplayer games via the Internet. Compete for TBPoints and redeem them for prizes. Compete

for Cash and take home the JackPot! With your Cash tournaments you earn a 25% of every entry.

With Pay2Play Service you can give your Players payment options to use your software on time-limited basis. Its ideal for the following payment options: pay/use, pay/features, rental, and subscriptions. The service can be easily integrated into both games and applications.

With In-Game Commerce Management you can give your players option to purchase in-game unique items and upgrades for cash or tbpoints. This method of commerce has become very popular with MMOPRG and is destined to expand to other genres.

Join a In-Game advertising Co-Op and advertise your games and application software in others.

An enhanced version of the popular GNet, TBNet Server Locator provides full tournament and game

Server tracking hosted free of charge courtesy of tournamentBlitz.com.

The TBEMS SDK is designed to be simple and easy to integrate with your user interface and game server.
