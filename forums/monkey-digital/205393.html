benmc(Posted 2013) [#13]
This is only my opinion, but I think $2 is WAY too little.

First, the people who may need this are not the "good" developers. The good developers can do this really easily all on their own. It's not even an issue. It can take some time, sure, but we certainly aren't out looking for a software program to do it for us - I don't think.

The people buying this are struggling with it, so they are probably struggling with a lot of things. The value of a piece of software like this will be MUCH higher.

They are probably even using a simpler WYSIWYG game making tool other than Monkey. They may barely know how to use the graphics tools they are using to make their game.

That being said, this problem for your target audience is bigger than a $2 problem most likely, so maybe even $20 would make more sense - but it has to be a 1 or 2 click deal IMO. Add 1 image, make icons or screenshots. Done.

My opinion of what you've mocked-up, and looking at your interface... it seems like you may have made the problem appear even more complicated than it is, and learning the software is going to take me even more time than it would to resize a few of my images to make icons and screenshots. You may be defeating yourself a bit.

What I expected: 1 button to load 1 big screenshot that I've taken, button 2, resize the screenshot for all the device resolutions. Maybe an Advanced button for all the other stuff.

Anyway, just my opinions, first impressions, etc.
