xlsior(Posted 2012) [#5]
"Making her site appear in the top 30 results" can work, but for very specific keywords only.

For example "computer repair in smalltown, anystate" is not that big a deal. But trying to get in the first ten thousand results for "computer repair" is going to be near impossible since you'll be competing with the other 10 million companies around the works that are in the same line of business, many of which already do their own SEO stuff.

Like Aman mentions above, it's a dangerous procedure too: Google also has a bunch of algorithms that try to detect overzealous SEO, and will actually punish sites that use it too much... Which could result in you getting a much lower ranking, or being dropped altogether.

Any remember: It's very common to see spam from SEO companies -claiming- they can get you in the 10/25/50/whatever of Google, but that's a complete scam since they are promising things that are impossible to deliver.
