My opinion.
1) Good communication skills.
2) Know the strengths and weakness of other team members so as not to give them tasks beyond their abilities.
3) Being able to plan head, organise and adapt to solve problems when things don't quite work out.
4) The patience of a Saint and not to get angry or stressed out.
