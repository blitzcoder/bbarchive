Hey folks,

As a way of testing a proof-of-concept for (hopefully) a later standalone game (made in Monkey, of course), I've created a card game using the product Tabletop Simulator. If any of you happen to own this, the mod I've created is free, and I'd love to get more people playing it and letting me know what they think about the gameplay itself.

It has quite a number of UI "bugs" due to the way I had to set it up (and TTS being 'rough around the edges' for a few types of games depending on what you want to try doing with it), but it is perfectly playable and I gave it a few days worth of playtesting with the maximum amount of players. I don't know too many other people who own TTS, though, so I want to get the word out and try playing it with some new people!

Thanks, and enjoy.
