SlopeOak(Posted 2014) [#6]
Thanks very much, guys! We're pretty excited to have been nominated :)

We've heavily hacked Monkey to make it more viable for our purposes, FYI. We're using DirectX on Windows (there are just too many driver issues with OpenGL on Windows, as we mentioned previously -- hopefully Mark is listening), and FMOD for audio (OpenAL just has too many quirks and flaws). It has been a bit of a pain, but hopefully when it comes time to port to other platforms in the future, the investment we've made in using Monkey will pay off.
