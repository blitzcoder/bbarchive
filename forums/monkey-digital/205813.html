ElectricBoogaloo(Posted 2013) [#2]
Doubtful. Avatars are, for the most part, all 64x64 pixel png/jpg links, and gravitar simply provides hosting or generation of those images.
Sigs are much more complicated, being anything from simple text, to banners and more. Different forums have different banner rules, and BBCode can vary illy from site to site.
Your best bet is to upload a banner to your website, then try to stick to the same banner code for every site you visit.
.. Or be like me, and have 100,000 varieties, ranging from plain text, to decade old links to images that no longer exist on servers that are long since dead... Seems to work just fine!
