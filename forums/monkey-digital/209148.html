I would seek legal advice on this as you will end up getting half a dozen posts that may give incorrect advice. The current version of this license is 4.0 that includes legalities for international use; previous versions only had the U.S. legal system in mind. So in theory an author releasing content under the the earlier versions of this license would have trouble getting legal enforcement outside of the U.S. There have also been a few legal cases where images where released under this license that had actual people whose consent was not give to the author and subsequently used by a third party who end up being sued by those persons in the images.

You should read the wikki and the the information on the creative commons web site.
https://creativecommons.org/
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Creative_Commons_license

The safest (and if you do it your self, the cheapest) solution is to create your own content and then you can release it under any license you want.
