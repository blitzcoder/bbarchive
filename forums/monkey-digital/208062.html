This Windows 8.1 with bing is a ultra cheap version for device manufacturers that is ultra cheap, but requires them to leave Bing as the default search engine. Device users will be able to modify this later if they wish, it's just that manufacturers are not allowed to customize this by licensing therms with Microsoft. This version won't be available to retail buyers, it'll be only available to device manufacturers and the idea is to be able to provide even cheaper windows 8.1 devices. So no, no free Windows 8.1 for us, and no ads on windows, or the like. Also, notice that this version will be only available to manufacturers when they distribute devices priced less than 250$ in the retail market.
