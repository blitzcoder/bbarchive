Testing on a device is as easy as plugging in to your computer using a USB cable and letting the Android SDK install the final package to your device. (well, you'll have to enable the USB debug from your device and install the drivers for your computer).
No costs other than the actual device. The emulator can't be used to do anything as it is incredibly slow.
