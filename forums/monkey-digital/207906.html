computercoder(Posted 2014) [#7]
Hmm. Interesting to hear what others do to get started each day :)

I've always grabbed a cup of hot cocoa, and just after the first sip, I'll take a look at my notes I left myself they day before. Usually I leave myself a one to two sentence-ish note letting me know what direction I was heading prior to calling it a day. I pick it right back up where I left off. Overall, I have a basic TODO list of things that need implementing. Usually that pans itself out according to how the development of whatever I am currently working on goes. If I find something that needs overhauling, then that hits the top of the list. Otherwise it just comes as the project matures. Project deadlines and milestones keep the entire project on task.
