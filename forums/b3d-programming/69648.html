Ok,

I have *bumped* this so anyone with a similar problem can see one way around the issue.

My characters had alpha channels to reveal other textures through straps and such on their clothing. This was causing horrible z-buffer issues and all sorts of flickering.

I simply replaced the alpha channel with black and loaded the textures in game using the mask flag, the whole thing is working much better now, although I cannot have true black in the masked texture - ah well
