John Blackledge(Posted 2005) [#15]
Putting aside the problem of actually 'pointing' to a specific folder (I'd use BlitzSys for that) you need something like this:
myDir	= ReadDir(fold$)
Repeat
 f$ = NextFile(myDir)
 Print f$
Until f$ = ""
CloseDir myDir
