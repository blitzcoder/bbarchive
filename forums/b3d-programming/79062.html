Pinete(Posted 2008) [#10]
Depends on the kind of terrains you need to make..
L3DT is quite simple to control and the results are awesome..
T.Ed is a very good terrain editor, very uncomfortable interface, but the techniques both of them uses are quite different.. The first one uses a "mega-texture" while the second one uses texture splatting.
In terms of speed and simplicity, "Panorama" by Jeppe Nielsen is amazing, with much diference is the fastest to deal with and the results are quite controlables, but it's a shame it doesn't offers a great number of options or features, even with that, is quite recommendable.
You can check also the terrain generator of Gile[s] 2.0... It seems very promissing, but I've not played anough with it.
I've worked a lot to obtain good results and I can strongly recommend L3DT and Panorama, T.ed is also an option but very uncomfortable to me. All depends on your patience. ;)
