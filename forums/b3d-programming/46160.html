Anyway, this works:

If KeyHit(KEY_PAUSE) Or KeyHit(KEY_NUMlOCK)
FlushKeys
Repeat
Flip
Until KeyHit(KEY_PAUSE) Or KeyHit(KEY_NUMlOCK)
FlushKeys
Endif
