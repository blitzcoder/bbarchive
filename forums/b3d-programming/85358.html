Hi there Gorley,
you'd probably receive better feedback on the Content Creation Tools forum, specific for these kind of questions.

I personally use 3DS Max and have nothing to complain about. Not only is it great at modelling, texturing and animating, but it can also have full support for animated B3D models with the B3D Pipeline, which saves a lot of trouble, trust me.
You can even export your models with all the flags you want already ticked, just having to load it through code.

Complete packages like Maya, 3DS, XSI and others are better at animation because they are specialized in not only keyframes but on the editing of those same keyframes.
You can tweak the speed, value, scale, and lots of other parameters with such an ease that would be too tiresome to do by hand on simpler animators.
