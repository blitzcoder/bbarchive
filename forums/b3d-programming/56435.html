Jeppe Nielsen(Posted 2006) [#2]
;Vector to target:
dx#=targetx#-robotx#
dy#=targety#-roboty#
dz#=targetz#-robotz#

;smooth variable:
smooth#=0.05

AlignToVector torsoentity,dx,dy,dz,3,smooth# <-assuming the z-axis is the torso's forward axis.


Hope this helps
