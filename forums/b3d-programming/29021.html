Do some basic stuff first, such as moving the camera around with the arrow keys and turning the camera, just to get used to the simple, core commands. Maybe, display a sphere and scale it with the arrow keys. Sorta work your way up. :)

I though the tutorials are pretty good that comes with it too. Best bit about blitz3d, is it lets you work on the mechanics of the games, and not spend tons of time worring about how to get stuff to appear on the screen.
