..what would be best way to apply different detail maps on to my mesh terrain, regarding different ground textures/materials(they are all blended in to one color map)?


Dreamora(Posted 2008) [#2]
Alphamaps that show the specific area of different textures of the ground texture
Detailmap

then blend them together by stacking them in slot x and x+1 and texturealpha blend and let your detail texture be repeated

-> 3 different detail textures


if you had a multisurface mesh terrain it would be simpler, you could just put it there as second texture
