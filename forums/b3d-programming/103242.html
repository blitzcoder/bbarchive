Not familiar with titles. But your timeframe depends on whether you mean coding, doing graphics and sounds for 8 hours a day 5 days a week or simply the odd hour here or there after work/study.

I also wrote some mobile app games in as little as 12 hours from start to finish.

The more complex the game the longer it will take. The more you want to polish it the longer it will take.

For a good game that people will want to play i don't believe it is possible to make a fully polished version in a month or two....but don't let me stop you trying.
