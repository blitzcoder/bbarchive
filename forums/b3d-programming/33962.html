Rob(Posted 2004) [#6]
To work out where each vert is:

surf=getsurface(mesh,1)
For i=0 to countvertices(surf)-1
tformpoint vertexX(surf,i),vertexY(surf,i),vertexZ(surf,i)
xpos# = tformedx() : ypos# = tformedy() : zpos# = tformedz()
next
