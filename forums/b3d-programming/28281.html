Rob(Posted 2003) [#2]
You need to use AlignToVector ent,NX#,NY#,NZ#,axis

Ent is the entity to rotate.

You get nx etc by returning the collision normals. ie. CollisionNX.

Axis is 0,1,2 and represents X,Y,Z angle.
