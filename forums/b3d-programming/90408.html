you can do something like that

yoursong=LoadSound("favourite.mp3") 
LoopSound yoursong 

channel=PlaySound(yoursong) 

Repeat


	If KeyHit(2) PauseChannel channel
	If KeyHit(3) ResumeChannel channel

Until KeyHit(1)
