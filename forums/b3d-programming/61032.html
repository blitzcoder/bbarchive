Stevie G(Posted 2006) [#2]
http://www.blitzbasic.com/b3ddocs/command.php?name=FitMesh&ref=3d_a-z

So ...

Width# = 10
Height# = 2
Depth#, 15

FitMesh Mesh , -Width*.5, -Height*.5, -Depth*.5, Width, Height, Depth.
