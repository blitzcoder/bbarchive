Maybe you could use EntityDistance()?
;space
If KeyHit(57) then
  ;close to car
  If EntityDistance(camera, car) < 2.0 then
    ;get into car
  end if
end if

puki(Posted 2007) [#4]
Either that or a collision.

What I mean is - let's suppose you want to do this but only when standing by a car door. You basically check for a collision between the car door and a hidden collision mesh (hidden with EntityAlpha) that is parented in front of your character/camera - a bit like pushing an invisible wheelbarrow (If you see what I mean).

The collision method lets you be a bit more specific with relation to doors, etc.
