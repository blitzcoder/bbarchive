Eric Draven(Posted 2004) [#4]
Hi,

I found out that you can chage this code:
For k = 1 To ticks
        time = time + period
	If k = ticks
		CaptureWorld
	EndIf
		
	;Update your game
	UpdateGame()
	UpdateWorld
Next


for this:
For k = 1 To ticks
        time = time + period
	
        ;Update your game
	UpdateGame()
        
        If k = ticks
		CaptureWorld
	EndIf
	
	UpdateWorld
Next


The problem is solved.
I hope there's no side effecs for doing this.
