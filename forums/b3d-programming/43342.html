Coders Workshop WATER Competition Started!

http://www.codersworkshop.com/viewarticle.php?id=8

Hey guys, we're hosting a competition over at Coders Workshop with a very nice prize listing to boot! Write a cool game with a WATER theme to it (this could be an underwater game, a fishtank, a jet-spraying game, or anything!) then submit it to our competition before the end of May and you could win one of our top prizes!

Note: It has to be a game that you have either just started, or will have started since February 1st. Sorry, fans of "nano" and other titles, it wouldn't be fair if you entered the comp at this stage in development! ;)

Anyway, we've got plenty of stuff to give away, including:
* 3 RARE Supersoaker CPS-2100s!! (you can't even buy them anymore!)
* FruityLoops Producer Edition
* UltimateUnwrap3D for model texturing
* Pro Pixel 2D, a graphics editor
* Games, mousepads, and more!

Hope to see some very cool games in progress! All information regarding the competition is found at the link at the top of this message.

- Seth Jeffery
