Jeremy Alessi(Posted 2003) [#2]
Whichever you feel comfortable with. CShop and Maplet are specifically designed to do what you want. Maplet is limited (you need to get decorator to help it out). Check out True-Vol or Operation Chateau Pregney for examples of what it can do. The new CShop seems to be a good bet. I've experienced a little instability with it but it's still a good app for what you want. Quill also has an indoor map builder along with the ability to do other modelling. It's the most complete modelling package of the 3. You really need to just play with the demos. One other nice thing about Maplet is that is' free ;)
