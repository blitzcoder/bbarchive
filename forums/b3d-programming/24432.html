Hey team!

This may be of some use to CShop and b3D users.

I'm sure some of you see a difference between CShop renders and B3D renders.

I have found that levels look darker in B3D than in CShop. Increasing the ambient light only makes things murkier.

After a quick e-mail to Halo we have found that if you simply take the light map and increase the brightness and fiddle with the contrast a little you'll find everything looks more or less the same in B3d.

I use Photoshop Elements and found taking the lightmap into Photoshop and increasing the brightness level by 40% and tweaking the contrast sorts out the 'problem'.

Now my levels look like CShop renders - cool!


I hope this helps...

Regards,

IPete2.
