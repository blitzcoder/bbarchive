Pudding(Posted 2008) [#10]
By all means, send feature requests. However my immediate next steps are to make what's already there easier to understand:

- Start a FAQ to address common problems (e.g. my model animates in the B3d Viewer but not in game; textures don't show up in game...). The rtf shown in the export dialog was originally designed to address these kinds of issues, but it needs better definitions of core concepts (e.g. Scene Root) and more detailed step-by-step examples of common scenarios (e.g. exporting a character model and compatible animations). I might look into a wiki for this so that the community can help maintain it...

- Clean up and release the source code to the B3d Viewer. This should help people use the B3d Extensions in their own projects, or adapt the viewer to their needs.

Thanks to everyone who has donated! It will keep me in tangerines (and the scurvy at bay) through the long New England winter!

(I'll look into Plimus etc.)
