Bobysait(Posted 2012) [#5]
I think, It's a bit more complicated but using PaintEntity/EntityTexture you 'll be able to texture singles entities whatever they are instanced or not
But if you use "PaintMesh" instead or affect a mesh at its surface level, it will affect all entities.

So, you'll probably have to take care about : modifying brushes for the surfaces will modify all instances
