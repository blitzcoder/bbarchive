Rroff(Posted 2015) [#3]
Blender can export to a number of popular formats depending on what features you need some of which can be loaded natively by B3D, there are also a variety of freeware/commercial tools that can take files exported by blender and convert them to formats B3D can handle though you might be a little limited if your using animations.
