Kryzon(Posted 2011) [#2]
I take it you're talking about posting a News entry.

In this website, go to Home and then scroll all the way down to the bottom of the homepage so you see the Submit News link - It has some instructions on what is pertinent to post, size restrains etc. It's also the form you'll use to submit your News entry.

The same formatting codes implemented in these forums also work for News entries, so make sure to keep everything well-readable and interesting, without letting it go crazy. I highly recommend adding an image at the top of your post.
You can always see the other News entries as reference (note the image sizes are always 400x300 to fit flawlessly in the entry's frame).
