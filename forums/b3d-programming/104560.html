RemiD(Posted 2015) [#6]
If you know what is a vector and how to calculate and use vectors, you can probably create a convincing aircraft simulation with Blitz3d.

Some things to consider would be the "propel" force, the "move up or down" force (due to the orientation of the wings on the sides), the "turn left or right" force (due to the orientation of the wings on the back), the air resistance, the wind force, the gravity force, the aircraft velocity...
