GfK(Posted 2008) [#4]
You can do this with B3D format models by using two proxy skeletons - one for the upper body, one for the lower body (and another for the head, if you must).

Proxy skeletons are invisible. Perform your animations on these, then manually rotate the corresponding bones in your player model to match.
