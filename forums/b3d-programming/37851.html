Sir Felgar(Posted 2004) [#2]
Blitz3D only supports Q3 BSPs. Ultimate Unwrap can convert most BSPs to B3d format. BSP Factory by Jedive also converts BSPs to B3D, athough I'm not sure if it supports Q2.


Skitchy(Posted 2004) [#3]
You lose all BSP information in the conversion to .b3d though ie. the speed increases it may give no longer apply as you're using one big mesh. In such a case its probably better just to model your level in a normal 3D modeller with all the bells and whistles and not limit yourself to CSG 'brushes' that look a bit clunky by todays standards :)
