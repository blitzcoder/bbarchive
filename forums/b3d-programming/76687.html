This might help a bit... http://www.blitzbasic.com/codearcs/codearcs.php?code=2224

And... To answer your question.

Store the old mouse position and the new and draw a line between the two.

Graphics 640,480,32,2

MoveMouse 320,240
ox = 320
oy = 240
Repeat

	x = MouseX()
	y = MouseY()
	
	Line ox,oy,x,y
	
	ox = x
	oy = y

Until KeyHit(1)
