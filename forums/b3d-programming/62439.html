3D World Studio, how do you put your own textures into the world editor? The files are all called .stf's and it wont let me use my own textures.


kevin8084(Posted 2006) [#2]
save your textures in your own directory in the Materials directory (c:\program files\3d world studio\materials\myown). Make sure that the images are powers of 2, such as 128x128, 256x256, 512x512, etc...
