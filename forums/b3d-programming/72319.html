tcpstream=OpenTCPStream(BMP_ServerIP,7999) ;,7999)

Make sure the BMP_ServerIP is a String, not an integer.
BMP_ServerIP$ = "xxx.xxx.xxx.xx". I recommend adding the $ to the end of the var to make sure.
