There are codes for many other exports formats than .3ds. AFAIK .3ds Export is still kind of missing. I would suggest to use an other format instead, there's .X, .VRML (WRL), Milkshape ascii, B3D and more.
If it really has to be .3ds, all I can offer you is to download this mesh converter software, written in ansi C, including the sourcecode, including import and export of 3ds. It may or may not b easy to convert the 3ds export function to Blitz (I planed to do so, never found the time so far):
http://www.melog.ch/dl/ivcon_3dconvert_win32bin.zip
