Something like:
;Distance between planets
Dist# = 100

;position of planet 1
X1#=0
Y1#=0
Z1#=0

For A = 0 to 359
;position of planet 2
X2#=Cos(A) * Dist + X1
Z2#=Sin(A) * Dist + Z1
RenderWorld()
Flip
