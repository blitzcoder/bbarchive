KuRiX(Posted 2005) [#2]
Of course:

hdc = api_GetDC(api_GetActiveWindow())
cx = api_getdevicecaps(hdc,8)
cy = api_getdevicecaps(hdc,10)
Col = api_getdevicecaps(hdc,12)


You will need user32.decls on your userlibs. Check the codearchives.

Cheers.
