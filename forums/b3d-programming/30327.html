Interesting, I used to work in robotics myself.

I assume you want the camera to point away from the arm, at 90 degrees to the wrist joint.

If this is the case, load in your model and create a pivot which is in the direction the camera should be facing in the default position, then just create the camera and pointentity the camera to that pivot.
