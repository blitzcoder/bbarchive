Brucey(Posted 2008) [#2]
I'm slowly moving them over :-)

It's a lot of work, and some of them may not play nice under the MIT-style license, which means some may have to stay where they are now.
It takes time to sort everything out - per module. I check for updates (if there are new libs out for wrapping), test the mod to see it works properly, perhaps update the code, setup the repository, add a wiki entry, update the original page.

You'll see that some of them have moved already. But currently there are more in their original location than are at googlecode.

Sure, I could spend a weekend moving everything, but then I wouldn't get to play in Blitz :-)
