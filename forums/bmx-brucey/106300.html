Here is a small test that works on my system:

SuperStrict

Framework sdl.gl2sdlmax2d
Import brl.polledinput

SetGraphicsDriver GL2Max2DDriver()
Graphics 800, 600, 0

While Not AppTerminate()
	Cls()
	DrawText("This is a test.", 50, 50)
	Flip()
Wend

EndGraphics()
End
