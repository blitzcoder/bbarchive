Brucey(Posted 2008) [#2]
I believe they call them Sheet dialogs. Apparently they *may* be added in wxWidgets 3.0, but there are issues currently with the way dialogs work, since sheet dialogs are *window* modal, as opposed to *app* modal, and it seems that dialogs in wxWidgets are assumed to be mostly app modal.
That is, you should be able to have a sheet dialog open on one window, while still be able to work on another window.

They appear to be working to resolve it though. Fingers crossed :-)
