What kind of specs?

CPU, RAM, HD, video card?

If you're thinking about using blitzMax on it, then the biggest limiting factor is probably going to be the video adapter -- BlitzMax *really* needs a hardware accelerated 3D card, and any card over a decade old is likely going to be severely deficient... Expect excruciatingly slow screen updates from a blitzmax app on such a system.
