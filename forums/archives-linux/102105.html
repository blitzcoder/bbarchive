Brucey(Posted 2014) [#4]
1. all these modules are for Linux 1.50 ?

For the BRL and PUB modules you can download these :

https://github.com/maxmods/brl.mod/archive/master.zip
https://github.com/maxmods/pub.mod/archive/master.zip

Backup your pub.mod and brl.mod folders in your BlitzMax/mod directory.
Unzip these files, and rename the extracted directories to brl.mod and pub.mod respectively.

Put them into your BlitzMax/mod directory.

Rebuild your modules.
