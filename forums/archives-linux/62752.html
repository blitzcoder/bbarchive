Marmoth(Posted 2006) [#3]
I found where the problem was coming from:

The X-System had no other resolution than the desktop one.
You have to add new resolutions manually to you x.config file.

this is a useful link to do that:

https://help.ubuntu.com/community/FixVideoResolutionHowto

note: This should be done on any computer that would use your program. Perhaps it should be better to warn users about that or to include a resolution checker in your program.

Cheers,

Marmoth
