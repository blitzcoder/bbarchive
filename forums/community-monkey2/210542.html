I have some news regarding our future license or business model!

Pyro
You may know, currently I am working on porting Pyro to Monkey 2.
Now I can't say when 'Pyro for Monkey 2' becomes available but when it does, a new license form will be introduced.
Details are not yet exactly defined but basically we will chop Pyro in to separate modules and add new products.
Some of the modules and products will be available for free and some will be available as separate add-ons that you would need to purchase.
The current Pyro license will stay valid for existing customers and for possible new ones who purchase Pyro BEFORE the release of 'Pyro for Monkey 2'.
So to be clear, this means that everone who purchased or purchases Pyro BEFORE the release of 'Pyro for Monkey 2' will keep access to all its Pyro modules, updates, tools and editors from Playniax without having to pay extra!
AFTER the release of 'Pyro for Monkey 2' only the new license form will be available.
This means some modules and products need to be purchased separatly as an add-on making it more costly if you need all of them.
The current license will probably be renamed to 'Pyro lifetime' or 'Pyro ultimate' or whatever sounds cooler.
Basically this is our way to thank people for their early support!
So get it now!

Ignition X
Ignition X was / is a very successful product and will still be maintained and developed as long as there is a demand for it.
I also would like to thank the people who purchased Ignition X with a little present.
What this will be, I have not decided yet.
More news on this will follow...
