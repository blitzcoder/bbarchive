cheers guys. yes I do this for a living . used to hang around Blitz forums quite a lot. currently freelancing as a character artist on 'APB' for Real Time worlds.

simonh - The male guys took me months , because I was trying to build the 'perfect' base mesh, but the female has taken about 8 hours so far as I used the male base mesh to start from.

worflow is - base mesh in max mainly quads - zbrush the details - export lowest level back to max , tweak the low poly to fit the volumes of the high res as best i can - unwrap the uv 's - bake the normal map in max - paint he diffuse map in pshop.

The trousers were hand painted in zbrush, even the seams and pockets using a fairly low poly base mesh.
