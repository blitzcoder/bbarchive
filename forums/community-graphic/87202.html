Ok...sorry for the lack of response as I have been busy. And thanks for the nice comments.

Software: Voodoo Camera Tracking - Free Dload.
3SD Max9: Used to load the max script Voodoo creates. This is a bunch of points and a camera.

Max9 is also used to create TGA Image sequences from AVI footage and then loaded into Voodoo for the tracking process.

Once the Video (or image sequences are tracked) the script file is loaded into Max. I then force the video to show in the viewport and load in my 3d elements, place them accordingly and finally render out all the frames as a new AVI.

Camera Tracking 101.
