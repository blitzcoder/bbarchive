jfk EO-11110(Posted 2004) [#2]
these texturs are using a classical color set: steel+woodish. Of course there is no wood, but the greytones and browntones combination makes it look sexy. (compare eg. alien breed).
Additionally decent relief usage gives good depth. You should not use reliefs that are deeper/higher than 4 cm (in the world).

Plus if you can add a certain amount of dirt, this will also help to give the scene some live feeling.
