Gabriel(Posted 2007) [#8]
Part of the reason it looks a little odd is your window placement. Those windows are too high to be downstairs windows ( they don't start until well above the level of the top of the door, so unless people are bending in half to get through the door, they wouldn't be tall enough to see out of the windows. )

If, on the other hand, they're upstairs windows, what kind of house has windows upstairs and none downstairs?

The windows are also a little too small.

Bevel and extrude the roof a little so that it oversteps the walls at the front and on the sides.

Those few little touches and it would look pretty decent, I think.
