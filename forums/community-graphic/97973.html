In other posts I talk a lot about the technical aspects of the game I'm working on. I thought maybe I show you a few of the designs I came up with. The game is set to take place in a galaxy about 5 million light years away, and about 500 years from now.

Designing a galaxy and the races that are supposed to occupy it is not so simple. Each race has a rich back-story - but that's a topic for another time.

So here are a few designs.

Let's start off with the three races.

The Orion Federation is the governing body of all human presence in the galaxy. Since humans set foot into the galaxy in 2521, the Federation has an open door for anyone who values freedom and supports democracy. This concept is unknown to the other races, and has caused a stir in the political landscape.


Click for larger version

The Reticuli Triumvirate is arguably the oldest race amongst the galaxy's inhabitants. With technology far superior to both the other races, the real reason for their presence in the galaxy is a mystery. It is rumored that their survival as a whole is at stake, which is why they do not stop at anything to get their hands on other technology to save themselves. There's not one single leader, but three. All three have to be in agreement before a decision is made. Those three are often comprised of different walks of life. They are roughly 1.5m in size, large black distinctive eyes, and grey silver-ish skin. They piloted smaller scout vessels known as UFOs and watched Earth closely for millennia. Their interest in mankind peaked almost without limit when Earth began construction of the Orion Device, a man-made interstellar relay that is similar to the ones in the Orion galaxy. The Reticuli have engineered and built an entire star near their home world in the galaxy. It is unknown why this was done.


Click for larger version

In stark contrast to both stands the Midori Regime. These people come from a background with a lot of bloodshed in their past. A very dominant race that will stop at nothing and seems to be mainly concerned about territorial expansion and control more so than anything else. They have no specific goal other than to rule and control the galaxy. Some attempts have been made to reduce the territory of Reticuli space - without success. Reportedly some kind of super-weapon was deployed. These people have a reptilian appearance, with a green-ish reflective skin. They are often about 2-2.5m in height.


Click for larger version


The galaxy itself is a very small cluster, maybe 75 light years in diameter. It has about 2,200 inhabitable solar systems, almost all of them are connected through interstellar relays. This makes rapid travel between the planets possible.
