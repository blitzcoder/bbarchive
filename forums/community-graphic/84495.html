Project SWNG -or- "Star Wars - Era of War"
SWEoW is a Star Wars fangame based on Blitz3D.
The game has an own characteristic, but has special properties of other spacegames like Freelancer, X series, X-Wing series, and Elite.

I work since one year on this project. Sometimes more, sometimes less, but it made very huge steps in the past.

_PJ_(Posted 2009) [#21]
Looks pretty, though a little dark.

I recall a discussion previously about lighting in space.
Whilst realistically, anything viewed facing away from the nearest light source (i.e. star) would be pretty much pitch-black and be practically invisible if it passes in front of the star, but in games, this approach is rarely used, favouring a level of ambient lighting so that ships etc. can still be seen in glorious detail but perhaps with some shadows.

I think this decision allows nice textures and surface details etc. not to be lost and also for playability.
