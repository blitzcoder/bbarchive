In the spirit of giving and sharing, I've decided to share this model, which is UV-mapped and textured, ready to use.
It's 1290 triangles, comes with a 512x512 texture, and is released under the DWYWWI license. ( Do What You Want With It. ) Seriously, I don't care. If you use it in a commercial game that earns you a huge advance, you don't owe me anything.

I'm fed up of seeing good projects fail, and if everyone contributed a couple of models, a few textures, a couple of animations, a few snippets of code or whatever they can, maybe less games would fail.

So knock yourself out, I hope it helps a few people out.
