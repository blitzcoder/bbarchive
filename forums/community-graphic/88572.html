Jim Brown - Time consuming at first. Getting your head around what it can and cant do is the real issue. Most footage can be used, but it helps if the footage is purpose shot for motion tracking. As for a tutorial, there are actually plenty around. Try Youtube.

BeAnAge - Automatic tracking is the default setting of Boujou. Manual tracking is available by adding "target' tracks. This helps the process greatly. Most good 'tracks' are done with a mixture of both. Good reference points in you footage also helps.
