Dreamy, I think the plastic sound is due to the low quality ogg, I've got some nice synths that do sound good when heard at a decent quality, if memory serves it's an oboe in the background. I totally accept that it doesn't really go anyway, there are loads of subtle layers that you can't hear at this quality though which is a shame cos there are some nice little filter sweeps and stuff. The clean sounds (piano, oboe, strings) were from my Korg sound module that has superb clarity and quality. The drums I think were from the Yammy DJX.

Regret, Never intended for a game, just a track I wrote, I sang it (badly) as I mentioned above and I'm not a singer (as mentioned above). This I actually did on a multitrack just playing all the bits live. As soon as I've got a singer it'll be redone, as I said, it was a demo.

Sanxion, It's a remake, I didn't write it, and yes it was supposed to be cheesey! I'm guessing you've not heard the original as I've not changed the structure much at all.
