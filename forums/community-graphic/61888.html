The picures are rendered in Lithunwrap, so this is a plain DX7 render without lightmapping or shadows etc. This is about what you would see in Blitz.

I tried to make it as realistic as possible, within the possibilities of a low poly model. I decided to use only one side on the texture, for the sake of higher details. So the right side isn't really correct: patent and logo etc. are missing.

The model has 335 Tris and the slide is a seperate object, so it can be animated easily.
