Right now I'm going to work on my P.U.K.I game engine so
it can handle what I have in mind for Cool Kid.

Cool kid will be a fairly ordinary platforming game in most respects
except for one. There will be at least two 'planes' that the character
can switch back and forth from.

A 'plane' being defined as the area he can walk in.
For instance in this image, the road he's on would be one plane. He
can't go up or down, just forward and backward.
(By up and down, I mean walk up and down across the road. He can still jump)
If I were to put in another plane, it would be located in the background
behind the lighter colored trees.
So then he could walk forward and backward back there as well.

This would make a nice element for puzzles, with him constantly
switching back and forth between planes. To dodge enemies or obstacles,
to get around obstructions, etc.

Hope my explanation makes sense,

WERDNA
