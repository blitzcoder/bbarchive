well, the thing that's really ruining the mood is the bloom/DOF METHOD. You can see that it's placing layers of images in the render, one is sharp and normal, but then the other layers are blurred, which does a weird effect that looks unlike correct bloom. That effect is more of a scrambling, disorienting effect, while DOF merely diverts the eyes from the out-of-focus area, and creates less distractions from the point of interest.
