skn3(Posted 2004) [#11]
One thing you should never do when drawing a picture, is to draw your lines in black, then color over them.

You are left with a messy half line/half color mush. Idealy you should either show your lines in a bold color, or remove them completley

The best solution is to draw your lines on a seperate layer, with tools that support alpha. Then create your color sections on a layer underneath.
