Derron(Posted 2017) [#8]
Ok, it is very very low poly - except for the turret.

At the top you could get rid of some triangles (if you arranged them differently) and to keep polycount constant, add a loop cut to the "gun" so it looks more "round".


Even if you render it at a low resolution: every added detail adds slightly to the rendered image - it darkens a pixel here and there, so the "eye" of the player will imaginate the missing details.

So add a cube for the "reserve gas", add an cube for an "antenna", add a bigger portion at the end of the gun and so on.


bye
Ron
