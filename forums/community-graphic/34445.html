Here is my first ever 3D building model. Its a wizard tower, but could be any kind of fantasy tower. This model is free to use with any project commercial or freeware, but credit would be nice. I haven't attached the doorway in case someone wants to use it without. I am not really good at texturing, so feel free to post images of it textured as it would be nice to see how you make it look in your game.

You can get the package from here:

http://zac-interactive.com/models/wizard_tower.zip

Its 357 polys with the doorway, the tower on its own is 249 polys. The design of it was inspired by a picture of a cardboard tower I saw in a magazine published by Target Games back in 1999. I found the magazine while going through my old stuff the other day and figured it would make a nice building for a fantasy setting.
