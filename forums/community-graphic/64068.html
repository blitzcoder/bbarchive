Since the tank design is the ip of a rather big company, i dont really think its a good idea to use it in anything other then practice.

Half the reason for making this was to test how blender performs on this machine with larger polycounts. So far it is still running smooth, only delay so far occurs when switching between render modes, and its still small, around half a second. Im using a amd 1.6ghz, 512mb ram, gf4 card box.

The tank is around 34k tris now, with the tracks/wheels making up around half of that figure.
