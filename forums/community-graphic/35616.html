Been working on a couple of human figures. These are now my 4th and 5th attempts at humans.

The male is me! I got my girlfriend to take a bunch of photos of me and I created a mini-me! Looks a bit freaky actually!

And the female was created from me drawing the views, I originally planned for her to be really cartoony, but it turned out looking more and more realistic so I moved in that direction.

I'm still yet to complete the texturing on either of them, although, it's mostly done. You'll see a couple of patches on the arms of the male, and a line between the neck and head.

The models both have 2 256x256 textures, 1 of the head so you get plenty of face detail and 1 for the body.
