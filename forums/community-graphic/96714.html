As most of you will know, when you've spent years on the same project, motivation dwindles. I started this as a sideline project to 'Polymaniacs' and it's now complete. Some of you may remember a small one-switch game 'Microprix' I wrote many years ago, this is it remastered big time.

I wanted to do something which could be accessible to everyone so the whole thing can be controlled with one joypad/mouse button or key. It's basically Scalextric with alot more features.

@ OldNESJunkie,

Yes just like slot car racing. Given the size of the tracks it would be a nightmare to steer as well as the AI. For accessability reasons you can set your button to accelerate or brake.

The editor is not very user friendly but good enough that I can build a new track in 5 mins. I was planning to release new track packs but I'll think about including it eventually. The bonus is that the AI will be able to drive any track you make without setting any waypoint or speeds, so once you've built it your good to go.
