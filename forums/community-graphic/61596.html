ICECAP(Posted 2006) [#12]
What app did you use to create it?
If its one of the mainstream apps like 3ds or softimage, try useing another render engine.
The reason i say this is because then you can create stuff like caustics from the glass of the decks and generally a softer and more realistic look.
I use mentalray and vray.
They create very nice and real caustics and shadows... just helps to pull some more realism into it.

But very good work, keep it up!
