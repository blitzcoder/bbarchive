Multiplayer FPS Greedy Blastards. Characters, Guns & Ammo, Environments. Post your ideas and images here. Thanks.

Greedy Blastards is a slap-stick comedic Multplayer FPS/3PS in which Contestants enter an Arena to compete against one another. The objective is to collect/steal as many coins you can and 'bank' them for points. The only loser is the one who ends the round with 0 coins.

A variety of non-lethal weapons such Spud Launcher, Grease Gun, Glue Gun, Bubble Blaster, Freeze Ray, the Zapper will be at the players disposal. The weapons will temporarily stun players causing them to fumble some or all collected coins.

Contestants can submit a real-world mugshot of themeselves in which we generate comedic representation of their head and attach it to a body in-game.

Exaggerated Movement/Gestures/Facial Expressions and a Witty Announcer is supposed to make the game funny to watch and hear verses its simple run-of-the-mill FPS gameplay.
