Thanks for sharing man. Some very nice textures. Tho, some of the sizes a re a bit odd (eg 196*384). This will be resized by Blitz automaticly to 256*512 and will look blurry, while it's a waste of VRam. Resizing it to 128*256 at the other hand will make it very tiny. Doors for example should best be saved in 256*512 IMHO.

Now since you're a texture man, you HAVE to play with the new DXT3 masked textures! You can use all of those nice structures found on fences, armory etc, as well as plants and more. Just blur the outline of it, so the masks border will not jitter.
