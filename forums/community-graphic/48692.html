We were discussing on IRC whether or not Ghoul's models are nice or not. I had a few freebies lying around, so I knocked up a little demo in Quest3d to show one off. It should run on most hardware, but the radial blur effect probably won't show up on non-shader hardware. No idea how fast it will be, but it's very smooth at 1024x768 on my 9700 Pro. It supports a wide range of resolutions anyway, so give it a bash.

http://www.animebowlingbabes.com/ship.exe

Download is around 1.5MB
