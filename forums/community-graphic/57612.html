Dustin(Posted 2006) [#11]
Thanks everyone!

@rob I'm making a 'casual' game so the color selection will be there to allow the player to sort of customize their avatar. I might also have other characters to choose from. Since the anim is boned, I can easily swap it for another.

@Grisu 75% of the gameplay will have the chracter in profile so I decided to go with a shiny visor rather than trying to have the eyes visible behind it. Although, when you duck in a dark hiding place the character will go completely dark and you will see his eyes staring out at you. :)

BTW, I learned something about surfaces... they can change order! I hard coded the color change to the first surface it finds. Well, that breaks sometimes and the other parts suddenly start changing colors and an incorrect texture is applied. I changed it to look up the name of the texture on the surface to make sure it is mucking with the right surface.

I've uploaded a corrected version so if you have the older version and it goes whacky then you can redownload it or just toss it in the trash. :)
