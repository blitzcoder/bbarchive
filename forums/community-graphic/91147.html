Heres an abandoned model from a game concept: a Machinarium clone with a cartoon steampunk visual style. This was supposed to be the lead character.

The sketchy idea is that the great clockwork machine that runs "The City" is on the verge of breaking down. The player must travel up to the very top of "The Gear Tower", fixing things along the way in order to save the city from some sort of inconvenient catastrophe. Each level of the tower would be a puzzle that the player must solve to progress upwards - either fixing a part of the machine or foiling the attempts of a fiendish, moustache-twirling villain to stop you in your tracks.
