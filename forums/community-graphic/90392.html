I've had this one for a while so it's not truly the latest structure in Prague. However, new water animation was added. When it comes to food, corn is king in Prague. Why? Because it's the only food source. Corn is also king because you can use the corn juice as a source of water. But I won't recommend using it to wash your hands or do any kind of cleaning. So corn as a source of water is very limited in its use. There has to be a better way. I introduce to you...

THE RAINCATCHER


This structure, once built will store clean rainwater for later use whenever it rains. It's that simple. With a special add-on, the Raincatcher will even tell you how much water is in the soil so you can schedule your harvest correctly. Problem Solved!
