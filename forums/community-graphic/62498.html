jfk EO-11110(Posted 2006) [#1]
I'm currently working on a sniper mode for the engine, so I needed a rifle mesh. Took me about a night. It's a traditional bolt action rifle, a design that wasn't enhanced much since the year 1900 (for good reasons). This one has a heavy barrel that is used for precision long range rifles, plus a strong sniper sight. It isn't a hollywood sniper gun. Looks more like a hunting rifle, but infact it would be a very accurate gun for eg. the caliber .308 winchester and therefor an efficient range of 800 Meters.

I wonder where this would be required in a game with a low camerarange, but hey.















BTW: Polycount is 594. Comments are welcome.
