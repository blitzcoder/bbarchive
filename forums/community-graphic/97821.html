As some of you already know, I've started work on a point-and-click adventure game. To make it truly original, I'm not using Kickstarter. We're about half-way through developing the characters, and I've decided to start posting images of those characters.

The game is targeted at Win, Mac, iPad and consoles so the artwork requirements were pretty strict. 2,500 tris and one 2k x 2k diffuse map only.

This is the main player character. I'm going to refer to him as Kyle for now. Although for complicated reasons he is also referred to under a different name.

Gabriel(Posted 2012) [#13]
Sorry for not replying sooner. I've had a spectacularly hectic few months and have just recovered from moving home yet again.

Thank you all for the very kind comments. I'm a big fan of Tim Schafer, so for me, there really is no higher praise than "Looks like something Tim Schafer would do".

Quick roundup of answers. Why pink(purple)? Because it's the one thing he would hate the most. I will indeed be posting everything here. It's just nice on Facebook because you don't annoy people who aren't interested when you post new stuff. You may certainly answer for me, and yes, Supreme League of Patriots will be developed in Unity. BlitzMax and Monkey are both very nice, but don't really have a mature 3D engine that does what I want on all the platforms I want to target, and I don't really have the time to be writing my own low level code any more. It's not a Freedom Force style of game, but I must admit that I was immediately tempted to make such a game when I got some of the artwork back. This is more of a Day of The Tentacle type of game.

And on that note, I'll leave you with the second character, Stan. Stan is a former superhero who now owns a bar frequented by superheroes, supervillains and groupies.



Now that I'm (hopefully) settled, I should be able to get things moving and have new artwork arriving on a regular basis.
