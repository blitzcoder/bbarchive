Robert Cummings(Posted 2010) [#5]
Best tip for any sculpt app is to disclipline yourself not to use detail or small brushes until you absolutely cannot get the effect with a bigger brush.

Always go from big brush/low detail -> smaller brush / hi detail.

Big to small, and it will work and look good otherwise you get ugly bubble lip syndrome and so on.
