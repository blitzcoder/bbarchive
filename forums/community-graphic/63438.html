It's an XM-8 made by H&K. I believe the project is mostly cancelled, at the moment.

Basically it's a descendant of the H&K G-36 series designed for the US Military, but really it's just another 30 round 5.56x45mm rifle at heart. It is more modular than most such rifles, but I think eyes have turned to the FN SCAR project instead. The SCAR does have the advantage of being a multicaliber platform, currently being made in 5.56x45mm, 7.62x51mm, and even a small number of 7.62x39mm versions of the latter that can take any normal AK-47 magazines.
