@Genexi2

Yeah I thought that once too, but its surprisingly simple. By experimenting with the noise filter, and clone brush, as well as colour filters, you can come up with some pretty convincing star clusters.

For the planets, its all about painting with the brush tool using large sizes and a mode other than Normal. Again experimentation is the key. I start out with a coloured circle and work from that, shadowing and highlighting etc.

You'll find tutorials all over the net to help you get started...

And no, that is the highest res I have of that. Its the size I painted it at, so there is no anti-alias-resizing-trickery going on there.
