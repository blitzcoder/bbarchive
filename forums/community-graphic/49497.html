If you would like a valid criticism, you need to add more light. Although the image is nice as is from a finished piece perspective, your hard shadows seem like they are hiding something. Light the model with traditional key, fill, rim lights and then it will be easier to see what needs improvement.

Also, the blue background makes it very clear this is a 3d model. Place some type of background that is realistic in your renderings to give the illusion that this is actually real.
