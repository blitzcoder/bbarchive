skn3(Posted 2009) [#7]
Its good!

I does feel a little rushed in the story telling department already only after 2 "episodes".

I reckon you need more talk between characters. Now its just like "boo hoo".. "ok im leaving town"..

The screen size thing is an issue. You could look at automating the screen size by using javascript to send dimensions to the flash app? That way it would size to the users window.

Don't get lazy on some of the drawings. The quality seems to fluctuate. Especialy the backgrounds. It seems some of the backgrounds are very rushed in comparison to some of the main characters. I may be wrong here, but I recognise the rushed bits because I have done drawing like that before ;). You could so with adding another step in your drawing process. A step to refine the line work more before coloring.

It could look nice if you had some more finer details. EG dragon flies buzzing around the water, birds flapping in the distance, dust clouds going across the path and other non essential visual cues. They will greatly enhance the atmosphere.

All above meant as constructive criticism. Its a really good start.
