After spending quite a time working on a top down RPG, I decided to rewrite the whole thing using Isometric Tiles.

Of course starting on this route requires either using a third party map maker or writing you own. I have always elected to write my own because it makes creating the game that much easier due to understanding the drawing technique that much more.

The GUI is very simple of course as this isn't exactly meant for public use (yet), just something I can make maps for the game with real quick.
