Mac install guide?

Okay... here goes...

1) Double click the .ttf file Icon.
2) Click on "Install Font" button on the Font Book window.

Addendum for those that don't know how to use their computer...

1) Double click on the .zip file.
2) Double click on the new folder, "blitz-ttf". Inside you will see, as if by magic, a readme.txt file, and the font file.
3) Double click the .ttf file Icon.
4) Click on "Install Font" button on the Font Book window.
5) Close Font Book.
6) Drag the "blitz-ttf" folder to the Trash can in your Dock.
