How does one play this? I read info on the wobbles webpage but I dont get it from these screenshots :) Looks like there isn't much to do?


Jeroen(Posted 2004) [#8]
Hey Maximo,

You can shoot the other player using Red Wobbles or push the other one off the cliff. This game is not about weapons, etc, but developing your character the right way during the round.
E.g in the ice/snow level screenshot you have to collect a lot of green Wobbles in order to jump higher. With this, you can reach a platform with red and yellow Wobbles. Only those colours can be "dumped" in your base (which adds to your score).
Some other levels keep character development "open" so that you can choose whatever you see as a tactic against the other player.
