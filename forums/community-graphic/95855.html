I've been poking around my very old Blitz3D sources (back to 2005) and found a little demo showing a simple planet earth turning around with nice music I never released. Playing the last days with textures and source I created this useless little piece of eyecandy stress relief demo which I like to share with the community. Have fun and feel free to play around with the source which is included.

Interaction: SPACE stops/starts the demo and RETURN saves a screenshot. "S" shows/hides the "shader" and "C" the clouds.
