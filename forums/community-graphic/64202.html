Atomic Duck(Posted 2006) [#20]
Hi Guys

Here's my next version,

I've remade the entire model. 2000 poly's less than the previous version. Now it's around 5300 poly's.

In the first version I started with a cube and extracted geometry from that. Now, I started with a cylinder which gives better results.

D-Grafix wrote about spherical environment-mapping.
I did that and it looks great. I went one step further and applied Cubic Environment-mapping to the model.
The main texture is blended with Textureblend 3 and the cubemap has Textureblend 2. You can see the result here.





So far, it runs on a steady 30 fps , with realtime-Cubic Mapping .

More suggestions are welcome. :)

>4 rotor and 2 rotor version, probably a couple more also.

>Hazard.

The 2 rotorblade version were the original + a number of closeup/detail models. The 4 rotorblade models were remote control scale models.

>Can I ask a stoopid question? I thought Airwolf was a very
>dark green - not black?

Before shooting began on the original film, it was glossy black and white, but it was too reflective for filming and was changed to phantom grey(dark misty grey with a hint of green) and white. During filming it was often covered with dirt to reduce reflections further. Also, many scenes were shot using green filters to make up for the lack of colour in the raw film.
