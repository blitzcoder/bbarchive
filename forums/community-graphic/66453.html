Gabriel(Posted 2007) [#8]
As I understand it, the trick to having good knee and elbow deformations is to have two loops of vertices on the outside of the joint ( back of the elbow, front of the knee ) and one loop of vertices on the inside of the joint. This means that the 2 loops on the outside can stay apart, ensuring you keep a joint and the single loop on the inside can just sit in the middle, whereas double loops all around would cross over themselves on the inside.

If that all makes sense.

I can't be sure, but it looks like you've done that on the knee. Again, I can't be sure but it looks like you haven't done it on the elbow, so perhaps that might be worth doing before you rig and animate it.

But I say again, terrific work.
