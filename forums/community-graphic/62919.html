Nice. The area around the mouth could do with a bit of work; the profile view indicates that the jaw and lips jut forward too much, - resulting in a flat, pouty expression. If I were you, I would push them back and taper the line a little bit. I'd also tweak the shape of the nose, if I were you.

Here's a quick and rough manipulation of the profile image you provided to be explain what I mean:



Can you see how such minor changes might give your model a more natural appearance?

EDIT: If you do decide to modify your model in this way, then I would also suggest that you 'round' the chin more than in my example if you want him to appear a little more manly and heroic.
