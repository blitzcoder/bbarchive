Thought I'd share a WIP model that I'm making for fun and practice, modelled and rendered in Cheetah 3d.

The large vents in the hull are for cannons. Many details have yet to be added: railings, a nautical-style steering wheel, various pipes, tubes, pistons, and other bits of machinery, and a retractable ladder.

It's meant to be a bit unbalanced and quirky - hence the non-realistic proportions. Conceptually, i want it to resemble some sort of mix of pirate ship, steam engine, and Victorian pram.







Feedback would be appreciated.
