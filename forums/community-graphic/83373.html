Wolf-359 is a space trading computer game

Travel from planet to planet. Trade goods. Explore new worlds and make a living as privateer, merc or outlaw - that is the game Wolf 359 is about. The game is in the style of the legendary Elite and so a lot of content will be similar to that great game but of course it won't be an Elite II.

What's all about?

The player starts his adventure as the protagonist Max de Laudry with an old ship of the Mantis class, hunted by a secret organisation which tries to possess the data crystal of his father. Having a poor equipment and only a few credits the player has the choice to trade goods, take assignments or to become illegal immediately.



The universe of Wolf 359 offers a lot of different ships, trading posts, planets and moons.

More at: http://www.wolf-359.de/index_en.html
