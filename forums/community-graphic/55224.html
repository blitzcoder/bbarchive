Rob Farley(Posted 2006) [#12]
Flo,

You've been given a link, 2D sprites can be easily made from these 3D models with a little time.

I assume you've done plenty of searches with google and come up with very little. This is precisely what most other poeple would do for you and probably came up with the same results.

This leaves you with a few of choices.
Create the 2D images from the 3D models.
Go to a pixel art forum and ask there for help ( http://www.pixeljoint.com/ is a good starter)
Or do the art yourself.

The blitz forum is primarily for coding and this is where *most* of the forum members skills are. Use art forums for art, music forums for music and 3d modelling forums for 3d modelling. The blitz members who can do these will probably be active on some of those forums too.
