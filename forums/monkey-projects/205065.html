TCKSOFT Retro Series #4 - Duck Dash

This is a remake of my PC remake of the original Atari ST game by John Gymer of Kingfisher Software and was originally seen many many moons ago on a magazine cover disk, back when the world was a little more innocent.

It's spring so lets go egg collecting! Mr Duck has lost his eggs and must get them all back before Mrs Duck comes home and makes him sleep in the swamp rather than their comfy nest. They are scattered on the fragile tiles of doom and guarded by nasties and sticky spider webs.

Luckily the god of all ducks is feeling merciful and sends Mr Duck some power ups and extra lives from time to time to help in his thankless quest.

Touch and drag your finger to move Mr Duck.

Once you have stepped on a tile it is broken and cannot be stepped on again - don't worry about being trapped though because Mr Duck is magical and can shift a row or column of tiles to escape such a predicament (by using the button in the bottom right of the screen while moving.
