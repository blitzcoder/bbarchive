Hey guys, i've got a fairly early beta of the game uploaded. Certain things aren't fully added to the game yet and there are also a number of cosmetic changes needed in the game but it would be great if some of you can give it a run over and provide feedback that would be great.

- You can add and remove shows from the game, however you can not add TV shows yet. 3CW start with a TV show, however this doesn't make a difference to the game yet as you don't get TV ratings. PPV's also don't provide buy rates just yet.

-You can add or remove Titles, although you cannot have them defended in matches yet.

-3CW are provided with a full roster while WZW only have 2 Wrestlers and GBW have none.

-Certain features don't work fully without leveling up first. You cannot negotiate contract lengths or wages without levelling up on negotiation - although you can sign and recontract wrestlers. scouting isn't accurate till you level up and you cannot edit the card during a show until you level up road agent.

-The full list of Wrestlers that aren't in your roster don't show at the start, you need to do a scouting mission first and they show once they have been scouted.
