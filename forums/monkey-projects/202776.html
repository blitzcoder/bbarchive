Here's a tic tac toe game I made because the others I found didn't match my taste.
Features:
- Player vs. Computer (one player mode) & Player vs. Player (two player mode) possible
- The computer / artificial intelligence doesn't always behave the same way
- Background music and sound effects (can be turned off)
- Space graphics
- Options and player names can be saved
- Free, no ads, no permissions needed
