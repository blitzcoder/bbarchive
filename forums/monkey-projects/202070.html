anawiki(Posted 2012) [#11]
I'm not having spectacular success on PC/Mac, but I figured out that I don't need to. My plan was to adjust game dev budgets to "avg casual game price" and make them in genres that I can compete (which rules out all HO games). The other point in my strategy is long tail. And the last one is "it all adds up". I try to release games on as many portals as possible, though some get my games earlier, some later, sometimes they get them the same day, but due to different queues they release it on different days.

I know that this year mobile world will add significant revenue to my income, but that's due to publishing deals, not my marketing skills in that area. Still, there are titles that due to time constrains or games quality I can't/don't want to publish through 3rd party (like The Perfect Tree).
