Update: Alright, now that I've had time to refresh. This is a game about a springtail inside of a house. If you don't know what that is, here's a video: https://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_detailpage&v=OwOL-MHcQ1w#t=71s Or Wikipedia at https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Springtail. The goal of the game is to reach the other side of the room without getting stomped on by feet or run over by the stag beetle/spider things. As each stage progresses the game gets gradually harder. For most people, level 3 is as high as they get.
