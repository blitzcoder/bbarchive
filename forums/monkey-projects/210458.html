The game is almost finished. We're concerned about the setting, too. But it's completely playable.

A little spoiler.


Imagine yourself in the dungeon where different heroes are coming to. The place gets overcrowded quickly and you have to keep enough room for everyone. Suddenly you realize that two identical heroes can be merged so they don't waste space.
How many heroes can you place in your dungeon?

Features:
- An overcrowded dungeon
- Lots of different heroes (including skeletons)
- Three power ups

What else do you need miss your subway stations?
