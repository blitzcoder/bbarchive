The Texas Instruments classic game comes to Android!

TCKSOFT Retro Series #6 - Hunt The Wumpus.

Original version C 1980 Kevin Kenney, this version is C 2013 Andrew Pointon

Enter the caves of the Wumpus with your single arrow and try to track him down and fire it into his heart.

Avoid troublesome bats who will carry you away.

Try not to stumble into the lair of the Wumpus, or fall into a bottomless pits of slime.

Why are you even hunting the dreaded Wumpus? The elders say he has stalked your village in the past, but it has not been in your life time. Is it a right of passage or do they want rid of you, like all those who have been sent on this mission in the past?

You think too much and maybe they feel your are a challenge to their power and their ways? Come back victorious and maybe you can change things for your people for the better. No more blindly following in line when you know there you can improve everyone's lives.
