:) Thanks Dave, hehe the game is also for Tables :) Im updating the video.

We used the particles system of Blender ( www.blender.org ) to create the effects.

Tip: To do the exploding effect ... create a text in alpha. Apply the text as a texture in a subdivide plane and apply a disolve effect on it.

I found a video on Vimeo that could help you. It's more and less in the middle of the video http://vimeo.com/21393926
