I was working when I realized that I could rotate this one vector while bobbing another one up and down; that they together make these neat, almost fractal like, drawings. Needless to say, I got very distracted and spent 2 hours making this. I'm sure it won't impress some people, but I love patterns and these kinds of things. So I wanted to share it.

The controls are on the page. As a last note, for your own safety, you probably shouldn't enable seizure mode.
http://nrgs.org/skyknitters/gdemos/vdraw/
