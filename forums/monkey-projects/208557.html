Hello all,

Mystery Crystals is the mobile F2P version of my last desktop game: Crystals of Time ( http://rvlgames.com/crystals-of-time.html )

It will be available only for tablets. iOS for now: https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/mystery-crystals/id866019537?mt=8

I wanted first to create a game similar with the desktop one which is a simple Adventure HO Game. In January I abandoned the idea and wanted to make a small experiment. How about to create a Free 2 Play game. The player will consume energy to explore rooms and in order to recover the energy must buy it using crystals or wait some hours.

Unfortunately this version is still not as good as I wanted. I am using my time now to work on many other projects and I left this one the last. I am planning to make a lot of updates if this 1st build will have success.
