To unlock all mods and levels for testing type "dipstick" on the keyboard at anytime

Hey guys, i've been working on this game for the past good while now, i think it's just about ready for some beta testing.

ALSO THE BETA PASSWORD TO PLAY IS "boot". Just a simple password you will remember for when you come back to play, and you should be using google chrome. It's the most stable browser that i've tested this game on.

Schizo Ballz is a simple game (at first) where you guide the red balls to the red side of the level and blue balls to the blue side using nothing more then a bar with a slight gap in the middle. It gets more complex as you progress through the levels

This version includes 40 Levels, 3 Difficulties and one unlockable difficulty

10 unlockable challenge levels, 12 unlockable skins and mods

and a time attack mode.

Please report bugs/suggestions on this forum or by the email found on my website.

The website is slightly under construction still, but you can find the game at

http://www.schizohead.com

Happy Beta Testing!
