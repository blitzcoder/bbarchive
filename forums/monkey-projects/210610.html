today I want to introduce my new app: "Football Pocket Manager" or also called "Soccer Pocket Manager". Currently the game is only available for Android. iOS and Desktop version will coming soon.

Description:
"Football Pocket Manager" is an easy to learn football manager, which makes a lot of fun. It is in contrast to other games fully offline playable. You make decisions: Take care of the team formation, care about sponsors, fan articles and your stadium. First you start at the bottom of the league system in the 4th division and lead your team up to the tip of the football history!
