I think the safest option would be a non-violent theme (including the violence of imprisoning someone). Rescuing baby animals, maybe (eggs/chick onto nests, bunnies to warrens)? You could swap the interaction to leading/enticing rather than pushing (e.g. dropping food) which would work better with touch interfaces as well as being less aggressive and a step away from standard sokoban. Plenty of scope for cute saved animations and people wouldn't have a problem with colours or patterns to show which animal goes where.

My goal is create a really good FREE sokoban games for android, there are lots of them but they are lazy ports with awful controls. I am using swip and hold controls to move around the level and that works really really well, no need for on screen direction keys etc.

Shortly after I posted this thread and read what muddy said I had an epiphany, violence is not good, bombs, criminals etc. but what if you played as "Demolition Dave"? His job is to rig old buildings floor by floor with explosives. This solved my other issue of how to present the levels to players, I'll now have a city map and players choose a building (different difficulty on each) and then select a floor of that building (one level per floor), one you complete every floor you're treated to the building crumbling into dust.

The in game graphics style I am thinking of Rick Dangerous.
