Funny story.

Since neither my buddy Matt or I are any good with graphics, we're creating a game for all devices that looks like it's been hand drawn on paper with pens and is being presented like a flip book animation.

Our first game is an Asteroids remake.
(this is still a WIP but you can play around with it)

I'm still ironing out a couple issues when asteroids collided, but so far this is how it looks.

http://www.pishlo.net/flickertest-flash/

(Ignore the writing on the paper)
Click on the piece of paper, then press the space bar to begin. Use the arrows to rotate left and right, and the up arrow for thrust.
If your ship gets blown up just reload the page.
