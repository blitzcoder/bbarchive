Trey and the gang return in this exciting continuation of the Tribloos story (but don't worry if you didn't play the first one, everything is explained!)

Terrible storms are still coming and Matilda, Trey’s Dragon friend, is getting very tired of blowing them away each time. Trey and his friends Brainy and Tuff decide to do something about it and go on a journey to find out who’s behind these storms once and for all.
