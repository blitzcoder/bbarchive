Hi,
I'm trying to make a little sheep sim game made out of real paper.
, and I'm looking for advice for improvements.

In the game you look after a herd of sheep, and try and increase the numbers or try to make money (although currently there is no finish to the game).

You have to keep the grass green (dead brown grass means no food and increased risk of an infection)
You can sell sheep for money, younger and healthier ones bring in more.
you can also hire a ram to make baby lambs.
Finally,you can call on the sheepdog to move the sheep around, by clicking where you want the sheepdog to go, this helps to keep healthy sheep away from sick sheep.

please, test this early version at:
http://www.sionco.com/sheepgame/preview/MonkeyGame.html

I know the game is missing something, but I'm not sure what else to include.
