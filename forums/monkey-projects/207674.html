Currently I am working on my new game: "Where's my Egg!?". In this game you play as a penguin going through a puzzle platformer stage and try to guide your egg around the polar bears, rocks and other hazards to the end of the stage. It is basically a remake of Penguin Land which I played a lot of times when I was 12 years old. A mighty 27 years ago! It will feature original levels and possible a lot of other stuff that isn't in the original 8-bit game.

At the moment I have the basic gameplay in place. A lot of trial and error while programming and surfing this forum alot! I don't use a framework, although I bought two of them since the first version of Monkey. So it is all plain Monkey with the use of some code of this forum like the 'falling snow' and the 'virtual stick'.

For now I am redoing all the sprites in Adobe Fireworks, where I create them into a vector image which I reduce in size when I am done with it.

I'll hope to finish the game next spring and release it on Android, browser, IOS, Windows and the OUYA.
