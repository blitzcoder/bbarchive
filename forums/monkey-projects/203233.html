muddy_shoes(Posted 2012) [#2]
I'm not sure about having the time to solve being so important to score. At the least I think you should only start the timer when the player clicks something. In general though I'm not sure there's a good match between people who like to solve casual puzzles and people who enjoy clicking around an interface against the clock.

It looks good though. A few polish issues with rendering order and the block breaking graphic not matching the block, but easily enough ironed out.

you might consider counting the number of moves the player makes and do something with that value against time, rather than just time.
