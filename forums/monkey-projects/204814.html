silentshark(Posted 2013) [#7]
@caffeinekid

Had a look at your stuff, which includes the awesome remake of Vic-20 Blitz. I had that game on my Vic (I think it came free with the computer). Happy days.

Anyway, here are some ideas to boost your downloads..

1. Use other app stores, not just Google Play. I've had some great experience with Amazon and Slideme, but there are others. I've had 4x more downloads from Amazon than Google Play.
2. Make sure the title of your app is "good", and you have plenty of relevant keywords in the description.
3. Work hard on the app's logo - apparently catchy/ attractive logos make a huge difference in downloads.
4. Use other methods to promote, e.g. facebook, twitter. You might want to try the IDRTG group in twitter (google it!), or one of the indy gamer pages on facebook to promote (http://www.facebook.com/groups/indydev/)
5. Think of other forums you are a member of, and promote there.

I'm sure there are more tips.. given your work, you deserve more downloads :-)
