My first monkey game is available on the Android Market.

https://market.android.com/details?id=com.wagenheimer.rabbitjumpandroid&feature=search_result

Iphone/Ipad version was already sent to Apple for approval!

I will release the Free Flash version as soon as I get some sponsor to it (or if I did not get any sponsor, I will release it in some days).

Guide the Rabbit on a springy journey up stepping on plataforms and avoiding enemys! Recover the Easter eggs and throw carrots or step on the enemy heads to avoid them.

FEATURES :

- 30 unlockable randomly generated levels with progressive difficulty
- Fixed, moving and disappearing plataforms
- Tap to throw carrots
- Cute graphics and Sound Effects

Help the Rabbit to recover his lost eggs in this fun game for all the family!
