This is 'failure', a simple strategy game in Monkey Flash.
http://www.retro-bruno.com/flash/failure/failure.html

Game rules:
Select the color you want to play with by clicking a color sphere.
Connect the spheres of your color to other colors.
(The connected spheres will be colored to your color)
The computer will do the same.
So let's color with your color all of his one to win the game.
