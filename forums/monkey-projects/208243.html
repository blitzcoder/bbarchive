Top-down platform/swing/conveyor hop game thing based around a very crude pseudo physics engine (engine likely too strong a word)- just enough to get some emergent behavior (as in stuff I never actually expected it to do!).

https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.alphapigeon.fleapyflea

Finally got round to doing an app in Monkey over the last few weeks. Has been good fun- feels like having a ridiculously powerful Amiga to play with (which was when I last did any game stuff). :)
