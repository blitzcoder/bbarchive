TCKSOFT Retro Series #5 - Mined-Out!

Original game created by Ian Andrew – www.OriginalGames.com

Have you ever played Minesweeper? I'm sure you have. What you probably didn't know is that it has a granddad, and that granddad is called Mined-Out!, and was originally released way back in 1983 when Windows 1.0 was still just a twinkle in Bill Gate's eye.

Bill the worm is trapped at the end of a series of increasingly troublesome mine fields, and you need to navigate your way to him before he dies of old age. He has been waiting for 30 years for you already, so you better get a move on or he will be a goner for sure!

Finally finished this at 3:30am this morning after thinking I wouldn't get it done before June (well I almost managed it!).

I think it plays really nicely, and much prefer the game to Mine Sweeper as you are actually in the mine fields rather than prodding at them from somewhere up in the sky. ;)
