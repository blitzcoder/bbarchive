John Galt(Posted 2013) [#19]
This is classic. Wasted several hours on this, and I don't like many games these days!

A few ideas-

1) Teleporter makes it too easy. Carry it around, then drop it an teleport to the surface and back every time you need to recharge. Kills the tension of mining at depth.

2) Same for batteries. You should only be able to purchase stuff on the surface, IMO. Once I was deep enough, I could afford to continually buy batteries to keep me going without surfacing.

3) How about lifts? Much like a teleporter, but- you can only place it once, it must be in a shaft that goes straight vertical to the surface, you can only place it once, they only go to a certain maximum depth- after that you're on your own.

4) Make rescues more costly, and don't allow rescues above a certain depth to really increase the tension.

5) It would be nice to have some way to win this- maybe earn a certain $ and you get to go home on the rocket for your holidays?

Anyway, whether you take any of these onboard or not, it's a cracking game.
