After hitting my head on table thinking of a minimalistic game I decided to use an old idea I had before I actually learned to program.

Description:



This game is about protecting the cores, called shon, while they are growing up. You have to use your cursor to spawn little spirit bubbles that make the parasites disappear.
You have three difficulty levels in which you'll have to protect a different amounts of shon.

Controls:

Just use the mouse / finger to move the spawning spirit bubbles. This bubbles will help you protect shon.

---

I used monkey with FontMachine module and diddy for the RealMillisecs() and abused of DrawCircle() . My IDE of choice was JungleIDE.
