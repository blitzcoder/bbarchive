The best way to describe it is, it's a bit like Cybernoid..
For a good while, I wasn't exactly sure what sort of game this would be..
Starting from a simple bit of maze-generation, the game slowly became a space shooter, as I added more and more elements to the basic framework.



Guide your little floaty unit through the maze, blast away the bad guys, and hunt through the barrels to find the three coloured disks.
Take them all back to the hub, and it's on to the next level.

There's LOADS of levels to play through!

The game's available from my Jayenkai Archive, or you can play it directly on GameJolt.
If you have an OUYA, you'll also find it on there, too.
Hurray for Monkey's awesome multi-target cross-compilation wonderment :D
