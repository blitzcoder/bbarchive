Finally ... after a couple of stressful days I've managed to release my second app made with MonkeyX. This time, I want to show you the capabilities to create a non-game app with MonkeyX.

I've created this app for a customer. So it isn't released under my name.
bodewo7 is a new Swiss parquet brand. The finest woods from Slavonic, German, Austrian and French forests combined with passion and shine.

In this app you can get information about the two collections of bodewo7 - collection seven and collection seven gold. Also you can get an overview about different featured projects made with those products.
Currently its only available in German. English and french is planned for the next update. Android is the next step.

I've used MonkeyX, Mojo and an extended version of fantomEngine (added an async image loader, image sliders, text-box, dramatically reduced the cpu/gpu/battery usage and some small improvements here and there).
