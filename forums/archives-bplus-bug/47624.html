If you have previously posted a bug report to this forum and can no longer find it, it has more than likely been checked out by a BRL staff member, and moved to the Bug Bin Forum.

The bug bin is a forum hidden from the main list of forums, that is used to store bug reports that are no longer considered to be 'active'. This may mean the bug has been fixed, is due to be fixed, or isn't considered a bug at all.

You are still free to reply to topics in the bug bin.
