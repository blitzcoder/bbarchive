benmc(Posted 2013) [#9]
For Fun-Putt Mini Golf Remix:

Windows Phone Market has been the best.

I'm at 40K+ downloads of the free version now, and getting some awesome results with PubCenter ads. Plus, it's #5 in the Free Sports Games category on their Market. Loving it.


Second I'm going to say is Amazon.

I don't get NEARLY the downloads here that I do on Windows Phone, but for some reason the free to paid conversion rate is a whopping 10%!!! I love that, sales every day. Free downloads are way low, but it's doing fine.


Third would have to be iPhone App Store.

I'm barely selling anything, but I get about ~16 downloads of the free version every day. Very few conversions.


Last is Google Play.

I have not leveraged my other games yet to get more eyeballs, but downloads and sales are very very low.


I haven't put it on Slideme yet, but my 9 Iron Ninja game did VERY well there, so I definitely want to be on that platform.
