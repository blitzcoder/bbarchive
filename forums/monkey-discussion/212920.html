Ferdi(Posted May) [#3]
I think if you are using ads you should at least put a link to the ads provider's privacy policy. For example, appodeal privacy policy is at "https://www.appodeal.com/privacy-policy". Put that address in your privacy policy. Say something like, we use the following SDK and here are their privacy policy. If you are using other SDKs also link those too.
