zoqfotpik(Posted 2013) [#1]
Something I would like to see would be a netplay framework that end users could download, already populated with a bunch of different games. The framework would offer abstracted netplay hooks to the client games, would offer the option to download more from some sort of repository, and would handle multiplay lobbies for the games.

I just had this flash of an idea-- a window with a list of different games in it, possibly with picture icons.

Especially if it was multiplatform, I think a lot of people might like that sort of thing. I don't know how one would arrange such a thing, how money would be made off of it if at all or anything like that-- but it might allow small multiplayer games to get the exposure they needed to maintain a critical mass of players so they don't wither away from the empty lobby death spiral.
