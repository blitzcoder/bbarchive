MikeHart(Posted 2013) [#2]
The rates depend greatly on the amount of work that has to be done. You will have a hard time finding an experienced artist that works for profit share. Some charge by item, per hour or for a whole project. So far i did whole projects where the amount and type of art was known upfront and the rates ranged from 150 USD to 800 USD.

You can help the finding process by making a list upfront where every item is described as best as possible. Size, style, length, color scheme, references, etc. This list you can hand a possible canidate so he or she can calculate the work load and what to charge. Also let them know how fast you need the work to be done.

Most artist also charge upfront fully or partially. Because they got screwed before, that is why.

Good luck with your search.
