OK, cleaned up the Apps section.

Apps are now shown straight away after being submitted, and are no longer hidden until they are shown by a moderator. Playable apps will still have to be reviewed before they can be played.

Renamed the Hidden section to Removed. Also, you can now remove your own apps (see 'Admin' section on left hand side of your app's info page).

Updated the apps news page so playable apps are shown on the left, non-playable on the right. Clicking a playable icon will take you straight to the playable app.

Cleaned up 'apps by a user' pages. Now two separate pages. One for all ('My Apps'), and one for playable only ('My Charts'). Both pages can also be accessed via a user's profile, where there is now an extra line to show the number of playable apps by a user.

Added 1-10 numbers to chart pages.

Think that's it for now. Any problems let me know.
