Today, Finnish mobile phone maker Nokia announced a new developer program aimed at those interested in building applications for its Asha devices.

If you qualify for progam membership, you can request a free Asha 310, Dual-SIM with WiFi. (Retail value $102 USD.) Perfect for getting inspired and testing your Asha app.

More: http://www.developer.nokia.com/Developer_Programs/Asha_developer_program.xhtml

Also Asha developers can participate in the Asha Developer Competition: http://www.developer.nokia.com/ashadevelopercompetition/
