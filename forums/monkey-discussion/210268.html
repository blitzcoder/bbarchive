First the short answer to it: Yes, it should bring in some money.
Now some hints about reasons and goals:
- The designer is just starting out to make a living from selling arts - this is quite an adventure
- There are a lot of small apps and progs just not reaching the barrier of effectiveness and usability - maybe due to lack of time and money
- I want to make an impact in helping some children to use their potential, not trying to get rich by using their weakness.
- I want to get it spread to a lot of children. So it needs to be free at least for some of them ...
- There are some negative associations about the quality of a product, if it is completely free of charge.
- I would be glad if I get to a point where I can work on this goal most of the time.
- Ads inside the app and IAP seem to be no option, because I think children need shelter instead of psychological manipulation through marketing tricks.
