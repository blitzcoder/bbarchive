Well, *I* love Monkey.

I just got my first app pushed out to an iPhone. I was totally blown away by how easy the process was-- the hardest part was figuring out that the osx exe was included in the same package as the windows.

There are a great many things about apple that annoy the living daylights out of me, but the fact that that sort of software push is so easy and standardized seems to validate a great deal of the anal retentiveness.

I'm also very happy with how easy it was to get Monkey apps compiled on Xcode.

As someone who has been programming for 30 years and been through numerous sorts of development hell I would truly not have believed you that it was this easy.
