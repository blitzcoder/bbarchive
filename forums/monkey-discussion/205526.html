GfK(Posted 2013) [#6]
No, I never meant to release it on iOS - long story but the shortened version is that I had a deal that fell through at the last minute - literally the day I completed the iOS version. Didn't know what else to do with it, stuck it on the app store myself, got a few sales, never marketed it, other than sending info to about 30 review sites, most of which I never heard from since that day to this. But I did get about a dozen emails from their sales people trying to get my money.

Currently its only doing probably 30 sales a month on iOS, but it more than covers the annual app store fee, so while it's doing that, I'll leave it there.

I'm a programmer, not a salesman. It made me a small fortune on PC and Mac, and it could probably do a lot better in iOS too, if some marketing was behind it. But like I said, it only went on there as an exercise in damage limitation. It's never been a priority.
