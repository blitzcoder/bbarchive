dopeyrulz(Posted 2013) [#6]
The XNA process is quite straight forward - you have an app you install on the Xbox which when run provides a key for you to enter back on your PC (on the same network of course).

Once going, you compile (from VS) and it sends your app (and starts) on the Xbox. You also have a entry in your game list.

You need to be a WinPhone/Xbox dev in this case.
