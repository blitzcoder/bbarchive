silentshark(Posted 2013) [#4]
A few thoughts:

I suspect search is key here. No-one's going to be casually searching for "trilobyte", so how will they find the app? Maybe you could do more with the descriptive text to help.. mentioning the word "snake" and "snakes" several times in the description might help.

If its name were "Trilobyte Snakes", you might find it appears higher in searches.

If you google, there are some articles about app store optimisition which is worth a read.

The icon is nice.. and this is deceptively important, apparently, in driving downloads.

I suspect success breeds success with the appstore, i.e. once you have started getting a certain number of downloads, that will boost your rating in searches, which boosts your downloads, and a virtuous circle comes into being. So getting a "boost" to trigger things will be useful. You might try something like a service from fiver.com (or similar). Use Facebook to promote. Twitter. Post on forums. Anything to get the app out there. Posting your query here will have helped :-)

Give it time. More downloads will happen in time.

(Not answering your question, but I found the best way to drive more downloads overall was to use other app stores, in addition to google play. I've had luck with Amazon, Slideme and Appslib, but I believe others have had joy with Appia and others. YMMV, as ever.)
