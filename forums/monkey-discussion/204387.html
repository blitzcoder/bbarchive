In case someone might be interested. I am taking a minute to present you Pencil a little gem ive found a while ago and their latest releases are really awesome and amazing.

http://pencil.evolus.vn/

Its an open source FREE GUI Prototyping Professional Tool.. used for making prototypes of Android, IOS, Web, Sketchs, Flowcharts, anything you can even make your own but come with all this too. I use it for my game screens prototypes.

It can be run in Linux, Mac OSX and Windows and even as a firefox extension. Enjoy! :)
