First of all, check out Jedive's "A GREAT trick !" in the UserLib section.

He found a way to have your own commands highlighted!

Now, I have found (someone else may have beat me to it) a way to take it one step furthor.

Once you have created your *.decls file:

1) go to the "commands" folder in your BlitzPlus folder

2) open the "2d_commands" folder and choose a file (abs.htm) for example.

3) Change the HTML of that to your own help page and rename it as "yourcommand.htm"

Now, when you hit F1, your command will be highlighted, hit it again and it will display your own help file!

The help file must be named "yourcommand.htm"
