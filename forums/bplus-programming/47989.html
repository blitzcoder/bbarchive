Yes, with WinAPI:
.lib "user32.dll"
FlashWindow(hwnd,state)


You can flash the window with FlashWindow(QueryObject(window,1),1) and unflash with state=0....

But when you click on the Taskbar-Button the Button is automaticly unflashed....
