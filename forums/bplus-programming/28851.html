Eikon(Posted 2003) [#4]
AutoSuspend 1
CreateTimer(60)
Repeat
Select WaitEvent()
     Case $4001 ; Tick
     ; Do all your rendering here
     VWait 1: FlipCanvas Canvas, 1
End Select
Forever


This gives the smoothest scrolling I could come up with, and i've tried many methods.
