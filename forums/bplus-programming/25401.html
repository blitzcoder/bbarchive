Mark Tiffany(Posted 2003) [#4]
There *is* a way to make B+ random numbers consistent with those generated from Blitz2d / 3d. I think you have to mask out the first 8 bits or something - i.e. SeedRnd $FFFF & MilliSecs(). Or something.

However, to guarantee the same random numbers across all PCs, you *do* have to roll your own generator - the random number generator will generate different results on different PCs, regardless of whether you apply the above tweak (should we ever find it again...).
