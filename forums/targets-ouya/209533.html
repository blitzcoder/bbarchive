Anyone into OUYA games making? The Chinese market is huge!

Anyone knows if the information below can be easily done?

To make a Xiaomi Console version, please update your game according to the following steps:

1. Download this ODK:
https://ouya-sdks.s3.amazonaws.com/xiaomi/odk-1.2.1493.zip

2. Go to this page https://github.com/ouya/docs/blob/master/enable_xiaomi_support.md for steps to rebuild an OEXiaomi APK using the AppID and AppKey

3. Submit the new APK to your OUYA Developer page as an update to the previous version.

We will test on OUYA, MOJO, and Xiaomi consoles, and get back to you as quick as we can. If we have any issues, we will reject the game from submission via email and follow up with next steps, also via email.

If it passes our internal tests your game will remain on-hold while it is submitted to Xiaomi. The last game approved, took around a week.
