CopperCircle(Posted 2014) [#2]
Hi, it is fairly easy when you know where to look, just open the .sln (Solution) that Monkey creates and then as Windows Phone is now on 8.1, right click and Select retarget to 8.1 (make sure your phone is running 8.1) in the Visual Studio solution explorer, then you can change portrait/landscape in the MainPage.xaml, you can change your project name/icons in WMAppManifest.xml.
Each project needs a unique Product ID otherwise it will overwrite any existing app with the same Product ID, you get you actual Product ID and Publisher ID when you create an app on the store, this info goes in WMAppManifest.xml also.
