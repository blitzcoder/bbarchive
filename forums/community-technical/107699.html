coffeedotbean(Posted 2017) [#14]
There are two options you can choose from in your account settings;

"Collected by itch.io, paid later
itch.io collects your payments, you request to be paid in bulk later. itch.io's default payment providers are used"

"Direct to you
You get paid immediately each time a purchase is made. You must link to payment providers you wish to support."


'collected by itch.io, paid later' supports Paypal, CC and Bitcoin.
'Direct to you' appears to only support Paypal and in some regions Stripe
