Whenever you see some machine specific graphical artefacts (something that works on one machine and doesn't work on the other machine) first try to update your graphics card driver. Blitz3D was tested with a lot of popular cards, but manufactors tend not to release proper drivers in the first place, but only as driver updates (they make you their beta tester).
