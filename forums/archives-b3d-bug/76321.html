JaredBailey(Posted 2008) [#2]
go into the blitz3d/bin folder and right click ide.exe and blitzcc.exe then click properties. Under the compatibility tab there is an option to turn off advanced text services. Check that.

Now when you compile your exe it should automatically have that option checked and should work.
