Hi everybody, here is EsseEmmeErre that has typed this text in Italy! :)
I must report that the PC app Digital Right Removal does make sound bad the sounds of programs made or source codes tested using Blitz 3D; the bug resulting in a high volume whistle when a sound is played, the original sound is totally covered by the buggy one.
After some test I have made on my PC, I have solved the problem disabling the Digital Right Removal audio device into the system peripherals list.
Note that the MIDI audio playing is not affected by the bug.
Hopeing to be of some help to people other than me, I wish to all you Happy Holidays!
