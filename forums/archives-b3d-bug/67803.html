Matty(Posted 2007) [#2]
From memory mip maps are not supported with texture flag 256.


jfk EO-11110(Posted 2007) [#3]
This is one reason why flag 256 makes writing to the texturebuffer much faster: mipmaps don't have to be updated.

You can't have both.
