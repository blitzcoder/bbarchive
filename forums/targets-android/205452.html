Nope.
It didn't work the first time I clicked "Compile", so I just switched to the drag'n'drop method.
Open your Nexus in explorer, (turn your Nexus on, and unlock it!) go to the Download directory, and drag'n'drop your monkeygame-debug.apk into there, then run the Download app on the device, and click the apk to install it.
No need for an upload/download approach, as, no matter how many times you overwrite that apk, it still sits in the same place in the download app.
