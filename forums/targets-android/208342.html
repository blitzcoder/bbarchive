Midimaster(Posted 2014) [#2]
Yes you have do this by yourself. The Playstore Developer Console offers exact descriptions of requirements images and texts you will need.

First you have to create a new entry for a new app. Then you can start upload and edit the contents and change everything as often as you want.
Don't forget to store your progress. For your first publication you may need some hours, until you have collected and corrected all the things they want from you.

As the last step you will "Publish" the new entry. Some hours later your app is avaiable in the store
