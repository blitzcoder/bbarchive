This was something I wasn't really aware of until a few days back.. There are more routes to get your game/ app onto Android devices than just using Google Play. There are "alternative" app stores.

Although the Android world is moving very quickly (and so this is obsolete), I found this a very interesting article - http://www.drmop.com/index.php/2011/10/08/the-big-list-of-android-app-stores-where-to-sell-and-what-to-void/

My personal experience to date - first app released to Google Play just over two weeks ago, and has 69 downloads - https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.yzse&hl=en

Released the same app to the alternative app store SlideMe on Monday (under 3 days ago), and it has 400+ downloads - http://slideme.org/application/yikes-zombies-shoot-em

I'm now trying Appia and Mobango to see what they offer - have heard good things about the former

I will provide some more stats in a few weeks.

Cheers, all!
