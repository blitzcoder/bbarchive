How do I change the target sdk?


Xaron(Posted 2013) [#4]
Go to /targets/android/template/templates and open the AndroidManifest.xml

Look for:
<uses-sdk android:minSdkVersion="3" android:targetSdkVersion="7" />


And change it to anything you want.
