lineNumber: 5; columnNumber: 22; Open quote is expected for attribute "{1}" associated with an element type "android:versionCode".


The open quote character is not what the XML parser was expecting, this can happen when you cut and paste between files. Retype and/or recheck the quotes on that line, in your build.xml.
