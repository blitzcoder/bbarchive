mciMP3 is a userlib to play MP3 files using MCI (doh!) - with this lib you can play multiple MP3s at the same time, all streamed from disk. Also you can set their volumes, change the pitch (speed), get playing position, total time and so on.

XInput as the name says, gives you all access to Xinput - so you can read up to 4 XBox 360 joysticks in your games... even make them vibrate :)

Get both userlibs here: http://www.icongames.com.br/b3duserlibs/

If you like those userlibs, please make a donation... every donation made will go directly to pay the hosting bill, so you'll be helping to keep those userlibs online for everyone :)

Cheers!
