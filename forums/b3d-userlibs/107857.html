Hi guys, after a few years out of the world because of problems, I decided to get back into the world of programming and game development. Sorry for not answering the emails, it was not the right time.

I have decided to work and improve one of the libraries that I believe some time ago and that many you like.

With this library, you can load files .tmx created by the free tool Tiled Map Editor, in BlitzPlus and Blitz3D.



Features
- Fast loading and easy to use
- Full control with a few simple commands
- Layer Control, tiles, objects and pieces
- Full documentation and easy to understand
- Unlimited layer, tiles, object groups and objects
