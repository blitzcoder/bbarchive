To get a smooth not reflective specular bumpmap, you need to use a smooth light gradient, I think the earthbump demo uses a Sphere map.

I looked at the setup in the kyro2 Embm demo

lumScale#=1.0
lumOffset#=0.0
m00#=0.5:m10#=0.0
m01#=0.0:m11#=0.5
