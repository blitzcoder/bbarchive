Hotcakes(Posted 2005) [#7]
BlitzMax first tries to open a screenmode with the hertz you specified for the refresh rate. -IF THAT FAILS- it will open a 'safe' refresh rate (Mark has said he wants to change this to be smarter in the future) - usually 60hz. Internally, the code will still run at the hz you specified.

If your drivers or your hardware incorrectly report their capabilities to the operating system (ie saying you can support a higher hz than you actually can) then this system will break down and cry. If your monitor is crap there is a risk of it blowing up. However, I don't know of any monitors that will let themsleves do that anymore. The good ones bring a message saying 'out of sync' or whatever. The lesser ones will just switch off/blank out.
