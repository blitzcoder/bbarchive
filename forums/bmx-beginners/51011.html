Gabriel(Posted 2005) [#2]
No, it can't do it like Blitz3d. It can do it like C++ or Delphi, in that you can use the OpenGL API if you like. It's pretty low level and while the basics are pretty easy, it's a lot of work to get anything up to the complexity of Blitz3d.

A proper 3d module is underway and due for release before Xmas, but don't hold your breath on the date.
