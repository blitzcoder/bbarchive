Ravl(Posted 2012) [#2]
you mean like: 
DrawText (varName, 30, 100)


varName = your var
30 = posX
100 = posY

Also you can write:
DrawText ("Life: " + varName, 30, 100)


Will print out on screen at 30,100 the text: "Life: " and the value if varName
