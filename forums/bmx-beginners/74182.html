It connects to port 80 (http) to download the mods.

Normally when you view at the syncmods URL in a webbrowser you should see some info. Since you see a 404 error now, it looks like they didn't put the 1.24 syncmods back online after the server move a few days ago.

Unfortunately the 1.26 PowerPC download is currently broken, so at the moment your only options are:

1) Contact BRL and ask them for the 1.26 download
2) Contact BRL and ask them to fix the 1.24 syncmods page
3) Wait for 1.28 to be released (any day now, although it's delayed from its initial annoucement)
