Presently, it would be something like:
SetBlend ALPHABLEND
SetColor 255,0,255
DrawText("Whatever",10,10)

SetBlend SOLIDBLEND 'or ALPHABLEND or LIGHTBLEND or whatever
SetColor 255,255,255
DrawImage(img,0,0)
