But WxMax can use an OpenGL canvas to render in it.
Search in the samples folder for glmax2d.bmx and glcube.bmx
But this is not exactly what you are looking for.

A lot of people are waiting for Brucey's CEGui mod.
With it you will have a GUI for the (OpenGL) graphicsmode.

Or search the forums for FryGUI.
