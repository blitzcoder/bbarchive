markcw(Posted 2015) [#2]
There is no official/default video support in Bmx, the Ogg module is just audio.

PantsOn has Mpeg and Theora (but no sound) modules HERE.

Brucey's has Avbin and Theoraplayer modules HERE.

I prefer Theora (ogg is the format, vorbis the audio and theora is the codec).

Check out the licenses before you try them and because bah.mod is so huge, only install the modules you really need.

Theora dependencies: oggvorbis is needed by TheoraPlayer
bass (audio library) and theoraplayer are needed by TheoraPlayerBASS
