skidracer(Posted 2006) [#2]
Yes, use AllocChannel to allocate a special channel just to play that particular sound effect with.

Usually you don't need to care about channels and every time you play a sound one gets allocated from a pool which is then recycled when the sound finishes playing.

A more correct naming system would be to call sounds Samples, and Channels sounds as the channel is the object responsible for making the sound and the sample is just the shape of the waveform being used.
