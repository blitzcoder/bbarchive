How do I take a decent screenshot of the program in action?


One way is to use a 3rd party tool like FRAPS ( http://www.fraps.com ) which can take screenshots when you press a certain function key. It can also record video of your game-in-progress, and overlay a FPS counter in a corner if you choose to do so. *Very* nice program.

Another one is to run your game in windowed mode, and simply use PrintScreen to grab a screencopy... you can then paste it into your favorite paint program, and save to disk.
