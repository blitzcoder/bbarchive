bradford6(Posted 2006) [#3]
might be easier than that. Blitz does it for you:


Graphics 640,480,0

Local mystring:String
Repeat
	If KeyHit(KEY_ENTER)
		mystring = ""
		c=1
		While c>0 
			

			c= GetChar()
			mystring:+ Chr(c)
		Wend
		
	EndIf
	DrawText mystring,10,10
	Flip
	Cls
Until KeyDown(KEY_ESCAPE)
