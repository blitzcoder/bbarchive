klepto2(Posted 2007) [#2]
Image to Pix:

Pix:TPixMap = LockImage(Image)

Pix to TImage:

Img:Timage = LoadImage(pix:TPixmap)


Beaker(Posted 2007) [#3]
From pixmap to image:
myImage = LoadImage( myPixmap )

From image to pixmap:
myPixmap = LockImage(myImage)
