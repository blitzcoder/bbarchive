ImaginaryHuman(Posted 2009) [#7]
Depends how you want to view it. ie

Print Int(VarPtr(a))
Print Hex$(VarPtr(a))
Print Bin$(VarPtr(a))

And by the way, you can do this too:

Local a:Int=0
Local p:Int Ptr=Int Ptr(VarPtr(a))
p[0]=5
Print a 'prints 5
