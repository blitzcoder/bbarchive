Alden(Posted 2005) [#2]
I am not sure which one is the default one (I'd guess ogl), but you can explicitly set graphics driver by calling
 SetGraphicsDriver GLMax2DDriver()
for openGL, or
 SetGraphicsDriver D3D7Max2DDriver()
for directX.
