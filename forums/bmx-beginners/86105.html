Floyd(Posted 2009) [#2]
What's a way of working around this?

Don't zoom in! Then it won't look so bad.

Seriously though, that's just way lines work. Consider the first line drawn. If we start at the top and proceed to the bottom we take 200 steps down and 100 steps left. These get rendered as 100 blocks which are 1x2 pixels. The line consists of 201 points i.e. a single point plus the 100 blocks.

One endpoint is in a block and the other is not.
