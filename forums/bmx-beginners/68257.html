ziggy(Posted 2007) [#4]
I would recomend you not to use digital signatures for binarys, as there are known issues on Windows Vista. Some users are experiencing a delay of about 1 minute to be able to open some certified binaries when they're working inside a private LAN, and windows is trying to validate the certificate. I hope this will be fixed on Vista soon, but even some comercial sites such as Actipro, Quantum whale and others are removing digital signatures on latest updates of their products.
