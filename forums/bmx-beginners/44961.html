But my first stab is getting the correct logic and flow down.

The title will be called Pig Racers and it will be 2d. There will be 2 or more pigs and a simple math problem will popup on the bottom of the screen or top not sure yet. If an incorrect answer is given the carrot will not move and neither will the pig. First one past the finish line wins.

My final project really needs this to be multiplayer and have net abilities so a class room of kids could play this and compete against each other. So if anyone has insite into this and can point me to some information that would be great.
