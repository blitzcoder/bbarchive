While Not KeyHit(KEY_ESCAPE)
Rot2#:+2
Distance#:+(Speed#/Gspeed)
waver=Size#*Sin(Rot2#)
XCoor#=(Sincoor#+(Distance#*Cos(-90+Rot#)))+(Waver*(Cos(-180+Rot#)))
Ycoor#=(Coscoor#+(Distance#*Sin(-90+Rot#)))+(Waver*(Sin(-180+rot#)))
DrawOval xcoor#,ycoor#,10,10

Wend
