WendellM(Posted 2007) [#2]
It threw me at first, too, since it's folder-oriented. You point it at a folder, and it auto-lists all the files/sub-folders in it, including those created later.

You can double-click the "Add Project" link in the right-hand panel, or use the top menu: File/Project Manager/Add Project. Then navigate to the folder that holds your project's files.

To remove a project, you must (AFAIK) use the menu approach: File/Project Manager/Remove Project.
