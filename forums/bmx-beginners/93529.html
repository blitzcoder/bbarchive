Polan(Posted 2011) [#2]
you mean this?
Graphics 800,600
Global rot:Float = 0

While Not KeyHit(key_escape)
Cls
rot :+ 1
SetRotation rot
For x = -10 To 10
DrawRect 400-x*10*Cos(rot),300-x*10*Sin(rot),10,10
Next

Flip
Wend
