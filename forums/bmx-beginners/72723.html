MGE(Posted 2007) [#5]
One thing I learned through trial and error during my last 2 1/2 months of using Blitzmax, always put an AppSuspended() call at the start of any loop. This takes care of the problem.

SuperStrict
Graphics 640 , 480
Local x:Int
While x < 1000
    While AppSuspended()
     Delay 1
    Wend
	Cls
	DrawText X , 100 , 100
	Flip
	x:+1
Wend
x = 0
Delay 1000
While x < 1000
    While AppSuspended()
     Delay 1
    Wend
	Cls
	DrawText X , 200 , 100
	Flip
	x:+1
Wend
