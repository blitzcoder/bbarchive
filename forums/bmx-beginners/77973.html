Yan(Posted 2008) [#3]
Also note that arrays are zero indexed in BMax, so...
Local f:fld[fldnum]

For x = 0 Until fldnum ' could use (0 To fldnum - 1) too
	f[x].typ = ReadByte(file)
	f[x].name = ReadString(file)
Next
