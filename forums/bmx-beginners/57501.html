Ant(Posted 2006) [#3]
I think what you should be asking is what are you trying to do with your game? Is it a strategic shootemup? a frantic shootemup? Is it a game with limited resources or one where you just need to destroy everything on screen? When you've established this, then you can begin to detail a list of enemies/game systems etc that will support this high level vision. Do this, and you'll get a more focused game, that will be a much stronger experience for the player and therefore a better game...
