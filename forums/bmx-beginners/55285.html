Go to tutorial forum and find and take a look at these two tutorials:
1 - Beginners guide to BlitzMAX from 'wave'
2 - The MAXGUI Beginner tutorial series from 'assari'

The first one teaches you the language, the second one teaches you GUI for MAX, but you need to buy MaxGUI.

My suggestion is to buy MaxGUI and begin your graphic programming using Canvas of MaxGUI. This means, while you learn BlitzMAX, begin to learn BlitzMax's GUI. BUT, I am not expert, and I change my path every now and then by gaining experience.

I think OOP, ploting and access to OpenGL 3D capability, has given BlitzMax the identity of its own.

If you enjoy graphic programming, you won't regret using BlitzMAX - "just a thought not a sermon".
