TomToad(Posted 2015) [#2]
Look to the panel on the right and click the Home tab. Expand Modules. There you will find most of the commands available. Also, you can type a command, put the cursor on it and press F1 once to see a brief description at the bottom, and press F1 twice to go to that command's help page.
