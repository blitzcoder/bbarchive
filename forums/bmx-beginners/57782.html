Wiebo(Posted 2006) [#13]
Well Xerra, that wasn't your fault :) I was tired of supporting it. Recently there are more and more features asked which were not part of the original design, and as such I had to introduce more and more hacks to get things working or changed. I wanted to stay ahead of the game and call it quits instead of ending up supporting an uncontrollable source base.
Adding Bmax to GUIde has been not too hard, but it can do the basic stuff, and not more. Introducing more stuff would've required a re-write and i wasn't up to that.
I'm still intrigued by gui's and their design so I might start something new, but it has to bmax, which means multi-platform, and i personally have no interest at all to support apps for the other platforms. (you know which)
