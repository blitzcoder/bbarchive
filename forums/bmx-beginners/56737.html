tonyg(Posted 2006) [#4]
That's not storing it into a pixmap but if it's doing what you want then OK.
Graphics 640,480
image:TImage=LoadImage("max.png")
SetClsColor 255,0,0
Cls
DrawImage image,0,0
Flip
mypixmap:TPixmap=GrabPixmap(0,0,640,480)
Cls
DrawPixmap mypixmap,0,0
Flip
WaitKey()
