GfK(Posted 2007) [#2]
X = 10
Y = 10
SetColor 0,0,0
DrawText "Hello",X-1,Y-1
DrawText "Hello",X-1,Y+1
DrawText "Hello",X+1,Y-1
DrawText "Hello",X+1,Y+1
SetColor 255,255,255
DrawText "Hello",X,Y


Better alternative is to use bitmap fonts, though, since the above code is drawing four times as much text as is actually visible.
