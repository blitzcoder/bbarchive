Derron(Posted 2017) [#2]
Did you change fonts in MaxIDE ?

If yes: does your locale (read "country") use "," for floats? So is 1.23 written as "1,23" instead of "1.23" ? If yes, then this is the culprit.
In that case: open the maxide-configuration-file and replace the "12,00000" (or whatever fontsize was used) with "12.00000". There are 2 or 4 occourences in the file. On each "configuration saving" you would bork MaxIDE start.
