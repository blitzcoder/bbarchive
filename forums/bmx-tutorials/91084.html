Following Bill Stanbrook and dynaman comments about the ColdSteel SDK lack of feedback and currently having hard time to market my own game, Metagolf, I thought it would be interesting to create a topic about Marketing.

Here's a well written guide by Rodain "Nandrew" Joubert to start with :
Zero Budget Indie Marketing Guide

What I can offer you is a list of specialized blog press around indie games.
A good way to learn how to market your game and mainly where to send emails of your prototype, alpha-beta and final release. Feel free to add your own link to that list.


Of course, I grant the right to the administrator of this forum to see if it's useful and edit / add items to this list if necessary.

Kick Starter - http://www.kickstarter.com/

The Global Game Jam - http://globalgamejam.org/
GDC China - http://www.gdcchina.com/

Other great places to find informations:

Indiegamer Developer Discussion Boards - http://forums.indiegamer.com/


But always keep in mind that none of the services above will help you if you don't have at least screenshots, videos and demo of your game.

Good luck!
