Example code:
Graphics 640,480,0

Local key:Int
Local char:String
Repeat
	Cls
	key=GetChar()
	If key>32 Then char=Chr$(key)
	DrawText char,10,10
	Flip
Until KeyHit(KEY_ESCAPE)

Have anyone experienced such thing? Is there a work-around?
