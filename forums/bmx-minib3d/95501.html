SLotman(Posted 2011) [#2]
I've just looked at the building... is it a single mesh?

Being a single mesh, using 2 textures, no wonder the alpha doesn't appear - the texture flags used are the one from the first texture.

Check UU exporter - which is collapsing the meshes into a single one. For this to work, it should export a "master" pivot, with the building as one child-mesh, and the stairs as another child.

Or you could join both textures into one, which would be faster to render, no state switching and keep the exporter as it is - but that would mean havint to reskin the 3d model.
