Basically this program was called The Architect but do to some conflict of names with another product i changed it to Building Creator.

Building Creator makes building modeling easier and less expensive for those who cannot model or create textures, what I have done is create a program which will generate a 3d building model based on the configurations you set. This will save you time and money because you will not have to buy a 3d modeler or 3d modeling software to make the models we have done it all for you. With over 20 base textures, roof textures,20 door and window textures as well as various model shapes you could make 100's of unique styled buildings for your game.


The project is still in alpha testing but going well.
