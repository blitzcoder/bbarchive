- It uses OpenGL.

- It works with MaxGUI (you can have a MaxGUI app with a Canvas gadget displaying 3D graphics).

- It works with Win7 if the system's hardware supports OpenGL at all.

- It's quite, quite close to how Blitz3D behaviors but you will find differences. Stick to the official releases\patched-versions if you want it working out-of-the-box.
