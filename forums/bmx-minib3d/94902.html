ima747(Posted 2011) [#2]
The small fixes version is the most complete and up to date. the gimble lock fix has to be added manually, it isn't part of the small fixes because it fundamentally changes the rotation math and would take the project out of sync with B3D on which it's based. Additionally it doesn't work universally (not all functions are updated for it, for my projects it causes total failure).

There may be a few fixes that are not yet implemented in the small fixes variant.

The small fixes variant is designed as a stop gap until simon releases an official update that includes them. It only contains fixes that have been vetted by the community to not interfere with anything else.
