- mod : winicon
- bmk preprocessor : bmk

How to install:
--------------
1 - Extract 'winicon.mod' from the first zip to your 'mod/pub' directory.
2 - Make a backup of your bmk file. Rename 'bmk.exe' to 'bmkOLD.exe'
3 - Extract the new 'bmk.exe' from the second zip to your 'bin' directory.

How to add icon:
--------------
Place a 'bbicon.ico' file in your project directory.
