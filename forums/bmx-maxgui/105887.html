Kryzon(Posted 2016) [#5]
There's some misunderstanding on what to expect from CreateTabber.
It gives you a tab bar gadget (something that displays tab buttons), that's it. It doesn't manage the tab pages for you.

For that you need to write a composite gadget, something that reacts to the tab bar events and changes the pages accordingly.
For inspiration: http://doc.qt.io/qt-5/qtabwidget.html#details
