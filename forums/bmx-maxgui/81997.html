SebHoll(Posted 2008) [#4]
Does the information on the "Product Updates" page not already cover what to do?

MaxGui module
The zip files below contain the MaxGUI module and should be unzipped into your BlitzMax 'mod' directory.

These files must be installed to BlitzMax version 1.30 or better.

Once successfully installed, your BlitzMax 'mod' directory should contain at least the 'brl.mod', 'pub.mod' and 'maxgui.mod' subdirectories.

You will also need to select 'Rebuild Documentation' from the MaxIDE 'Program' menu once the module has been installed to update your BlitzMax documentation.
