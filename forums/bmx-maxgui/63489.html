Brucey(Posted 2006) [#2]
there is nothing stopping you handling the EVENT_GADGETACTION that is raised when you click a link, when you create your HTMLView using the HTMLVIEW_NONAVIGATE style.

Then you can make it open a new window and show a new page etc...
