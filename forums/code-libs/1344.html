Cheetah DB by Knotz2005
Cheetah DB is a free DBase III compatible databse engine. It's robust, fast and has multi-user capabilities.

Look at Paul Squires' website to get Cheetah:
http://www.planetsquires.com 
.lib "Cheetah2.dll"
