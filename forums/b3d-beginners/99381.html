3D Pacman game
==============

1) Create Title Screen for when user press spacebar to start the game.
2) Create 3D Pacman by using 2D Arrays with 3D Cubes that look like Maze
3) put your pacman in and get your pacman control working with Collisions
4) you can put one monster in to tune the monster AI with Collisions
5) you can 3 other monsters in
6) work your Collisions between Pacman and Monster
7) Work on Game Over Screen
8) Put Highscore on title screen
9) Put music for title screen and sound for pacman when he eat pills or monsters

You got a Game!

Good Luck!
