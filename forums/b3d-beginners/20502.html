Sir Gak(Posted 2003) [#4]
When you are in the screen showing your program, highlight a command and press F1. This will take you to the Help screen. (You can also use the mouse to select Help from the menu). When you get there, on the left side there is a yellow keyword "Index". Select that, and you will see the word "Scancodes." Select that, and any scancode you want is to be found there.
