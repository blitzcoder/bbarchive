SkyCube(Posted 2007) [#2]
Hi Merrie,

I currently own Carrara 5 Standard. I have used it for some simple terrain modeling and works well with Blitz (export the terrain as .3ds format). Don't know about version 4 though (although I don't think there is that big a change). However, as an animation app it's not compatible with Blitz, as the only format B3D can read is .3ds (Carrara does not export .md2 or .b3d or .x) and, as I have recently learned, that format is not suited well for animation, or at least, boned animation.
