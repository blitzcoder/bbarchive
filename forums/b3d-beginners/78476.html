I start with reading the documentation, read the tcp commands under the 2d commands, under networking. That lists all available commands and also gives you an even more basic example of a quick tcp connection. Once I had an inkling of what I was looking for, I went straight to trial and error. When I needed more info on certain things, I will do a search through the forums. I constantly look for info on the forums. Just do keyword searches for the command your looking for. Alot of times I will look at the code archives for demonstrations of code for certain things.
