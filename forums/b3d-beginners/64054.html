Dreamora(Posted 2006) [#4]
Most such details only work within 3D Studio Max

You won't get any shadow etc within B3D, you only get the geometry with texture, shininess and texture blending settings as well as hardware lights you placed ...

The "nice looking smooth things" from 3DS Max are 3DS Max only if you are refering to those.
