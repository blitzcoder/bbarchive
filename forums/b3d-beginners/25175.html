soja(Posted 2003) [#2]
Try SetForegroundWindow()

Put this is one of your .decls files in the userlibs folder:
.lib "user32.dll"
SetForegroundWindow%(hwnd%):"SetForegroundWindow"

and use this in your code:
success=SetForegroundWindow(QueryObject(WndMain,1))

where WndMain is the window you want to bring to the foreground.
