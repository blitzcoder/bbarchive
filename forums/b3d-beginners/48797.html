Nope, i'm afraid not. Bump mpas in blitz require you to calculate the light angle, and updates it manually. If the object rotates, you will have to adjust the vertex colours/ or the entity colour to match that. Tricky business really :S The only bump mapoping blitz has is normal mapping. sswift gave a really good description of what it is. It basically uses the RGB of a colour to calculate slope of the bump. R,G and B each representing a vector.
