KillerX(Posted 2008) [#4]
Timer = MilliSecs()
For i=0 To 100
MoveEntity spheres(i), Rnd(-0.5,0.5), 0, Rnd(-1.2,1.2)
Next
While Not MilliSecs() - Timer > 100
Wend

Put that in your main loop
