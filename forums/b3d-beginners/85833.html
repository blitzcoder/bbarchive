_PJ_(Posted 2009) [#5]
Yes, as mentioned above, the SpriteViewMode may need to be changed.
It depends on 'HOW' you wish to turn the sprite, i.e. what axis you are rotating it around.

By default, the SpriteViewMode is set to Always face the camera, so effectively, you can only Rotate it to simulate a Roll relative to the camera. Pitch or Yaw changes will not work in the default SpriteViewMode.
