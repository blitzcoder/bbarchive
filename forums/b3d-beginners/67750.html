make mesh with separated head and with bone head included in to your skin mesh animation, then assign that head mesh to head bone..once receive hit, then hideentity head and at same time use some nice particle fx associated to same head bone..

yep. If you're not happy with how the head simply 'pops off' and disappears you can 'hide it' by adding particles (bullet explosion + blood splatters) in front of it - so the player doesn't see the head simply 'disappear'...
