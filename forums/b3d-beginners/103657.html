Matty(Posted 2014) [#2]
Yes.

Draw your 2d graphics over the top of your 3d graphics by placing all 2d drawing commands after renderworld but before flip.

Please note that in the past this has had an impact on performance mixing 2d and 3d but I cannot tell if that is still the case.
