big10p(Posted 2007) [#2]
Either:

x# = EntityX(door) - EntityX(player)
z# = EntityZ(door) - EntityZ(player)
dist# = Sqr(x*x + z*z)

Or:

Stick a stationary pivot at the door position and do distance check against that, instead of the door mesh.
