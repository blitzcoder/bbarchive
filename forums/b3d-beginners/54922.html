IPete2(Posted 2006) [#13]
Sorry guys I was asleep whilst all this was going on... I posted the link to those who asked in the other forum thread - but I could only find version 3.01.


@Ross -

What's so 'special' about this is that you can set up particles, bodies of water, skyboxes, flares, start points, animating doors, moving platforms, lifts and keys using CShop as you build your level, then watch as Singularity does all the work implementing them for you allowing you to get on with the job of making the gameplay.

Singularity and CShop work together to provide a rapid development environment. Once Josh has his new Engine and 3D World Studio is totally complete theywill become the NEXT generation - I for one am very excited about the prospect.

Oh and Singularity has lots of other 'inbuilt' features which you can use to your advantage. Josh fiddled with audio too so that objects which were close but obstructed by walls etc, were reduced in volume giving a more realistic ambient noise response. Also sound on materials as you walked on metal or wood or concrete, were set in CShop, and the list goes on.

The animating doors with keys, lifts, and moving platforms are features I added into Sigularity a while back btw.

IPete2.
