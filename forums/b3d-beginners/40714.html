how can I set the center of rotation in blitz-created meshes (like a box)?


Shambler(Posted 2006) [#6]
Create a pivot, position it where you want the centre of rotation to be and then parent the blitz created mesh to it.

Rotating the pivot will then rotate the mesh around it.
