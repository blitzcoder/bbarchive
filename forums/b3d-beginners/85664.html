EDIT: This one is not really Media Linker's fault. If you are running your application directly from the Zip, the BCA archive won't be decompressed and your application won't be able to load it, and so it will give a "BCA archive not found!" and will close. Make sure to properly unpack your exe so that the packed data can be extracted properly.
