jfk EO-11110(Posted 2006) [#11]
I don't think there's a Polygon Reduction tool that can handle it. Probably you could implement a smart reduction algorithm yourself (there's a example in the archives).

What I did was: clusterizing the mesh. So the clusters behind the camera and those out of the camera range will no longer be rendered. This gave me the double framerate. ClusterizeMesh is also in the archives, tho may need some modifications to work with fle geometry.
