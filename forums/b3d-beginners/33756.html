electronin(Posted 2004) [#4]
Try this:
walk=ExtractAnimSeq(ninja,1,14) 
While Not KeyHit(1)

If Not Animating(ninja) Then
Animate ninja,1,walk,10
EndIf

MoveEntity ninja,0,0,.25
UpdateWorld
RenderWorld
Flip
Wend
