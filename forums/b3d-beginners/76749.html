Ok, uploading...

For the waterfall:

I have a mesh, the shape of the water fall. It comes straight down, slightly coming out as it hits the water. On the mesh, i have a moving texture, to simulate the water falling. I also use the FastLib extentions, and it's bumpmapping extentions.

I have two ring particle emittors, emitting water foam particles. Without them, it looks terrible. I use Particle Candy for this.

It's all a big trick. I think mine looks a bit unrealistic in that it's quite shiney... But it does me :o) I added in some light shafts to draw the eye away from the waterfall a bit too.
