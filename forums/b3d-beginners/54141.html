Project Starter Source

Cellular is a 3D First Person inner space simulator in which you control D-Bot (a immuno nano bot) to protect cells from attacking viruses and collect powerups.

If viruses infiltrate a cell they multiply and continue to attack other cells. When all the cells have been infected the game is over. Whats starts of relatively slow quickly evolves into an addictive shooting frenzy.

I wrote the core source in under 24 hours. Currently only B3D primitives are used and I see lots of potential with improved effects, graphics, and sound. The community could easily expand the core adding a variety of viruses, cells, dbots, blasters, powerups, and maybe even networked multiplayer.
