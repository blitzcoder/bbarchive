jfk EO-11110(Posted 2003) [#2]
It's simple. The brighter Pixels are higher Places, the darker Pixels are the lower Places. Just take a black image, select the Airbrush in a Drawing App, make some bright Spots and Things, blur the image a lot and then save it and load it in Blitz using LoadTerrain().

The image must be a power of 2 square size, eg 8*8 or 16*16 or 256*256 or 128*128 or something like that.

Power of two numbers are 2,4,8,16,32,64,128,256,512,1024...
