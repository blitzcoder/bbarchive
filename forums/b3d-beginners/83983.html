fox95871(Posted 2009) [#14]
Wow, I just had this problem the other day! I solved it with this:

http://meshlab.sourceforge.net/

By the way, if you happen to be making your obj with Maya, you'll need to make it white before exporting it. If you don't, and then make it into a 3ds with Mesh lab to use in Blitz, it will always be grey no matter what you do, darkening all your textures by half.

Good luck! Mesh lab is a great free program for converting file types.
