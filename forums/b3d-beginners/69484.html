GfK(Posted 2007) [#2]
Milkshape and Ultimate Unwrap are fairly 'mainstream' for around these parts. Both can export to .B3D format, which you will need to use for boned/animated deformable meshes.

You can create them programatically if all you want is cubes and spheres, and a handful of other primitives, but on a bigger scale you need a modeller.
