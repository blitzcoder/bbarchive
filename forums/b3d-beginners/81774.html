steve_ancell(Posted 2008) [#5]
Answer to your question WERDNA...

1) Login.

2) Click on the "Account" Tab.

3) If you haven't already done so, click on "Register product", and use your BUID to register your copy of Blitz3D.

(The BUID, is that long Alphanumeric number on the front of the User Manual that was packaged in the box along with the Install Disk.)

3) Finally, click on "Product updates". The rest is childsplay.

4) Enjoy.
