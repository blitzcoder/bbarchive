hey! well blitz can do alot with images. shear, rotate, scale, and various other things. Problem is that these aren't quick enough to do in real time unless you use the afore-mentioned UserLib by John Pickford i believe.

When i say not quick enough in real time it would be okay to use, it would be fine in a board game where constant moving and rotating is happening. The bit about the custom image is do-able to, you set the drawing buffer to the image and draw over it to produce your combined image. post back if you want an examples of anything like drawing onto image or shearing and stuff :D

And good luck with your project!
