xlsior(Posted 2016) [#3]
Is a BlitzBasic3D program, even the simplest,
can work under WinPhone ?


No.

Blitz3D requires an Intel x86/x64 processor, and most windows phones use an ARM processor which is not compatible with x86 binaries.
On top of that, B3D requires DirectX7, which is not available on the phone.
