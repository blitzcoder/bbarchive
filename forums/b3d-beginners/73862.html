(tu) sinu(Posted 2007) [#6]
try the deltayaw/pitch commands, they can be used to determine what position an entity is to another ie camera.

a deltayaw of 0 would mean straight in front and a deltayaw of 45 would mean slightly right and in front. Combined with deltapitch and entity distance (or even the tformpoint command) you can check the height to the camera and if ti's in range, all very quickly.
