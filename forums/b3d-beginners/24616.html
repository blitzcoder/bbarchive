Generally people don't do a lot of swearing here... I think it's likely because the majority of people here are adults.

Some guidelines that I and others observe, are to avoid giberish (Haxxor or whatever), because it seems childish to most people here. Use your emoticons carefully and when needed.

If you write something in a language other than english, then please make a 'translation' below in english, as a courtesy... You never know if one of those who doesn't speak German or Danish or Spanish actually know the answer to your question...

Also, as a courtesy to the MANY people here, who doesn't speak english natively, please try to write in as close to english as you can...

Some topics are deleted or locked... If it happens to you, let it rest. It doesn't mean that the moderators hate you, it just means that someone broke the rules... Some subjects are touchy, and will automatically be locked... Don't take it personally, and don't start another thread about the same subject or complaining about the first being locked.

Please remember to try to give an accurate topic description... WAAAAAAAAAHHHH, HELP!, IMPORTANT etc are not good. This is less of an annoyance in the Off-Topic forum, as alot of goofing around takes place in there.

And perhaps most important... You are free to ignore anything I just wrote :) As long as you have lot's of fun...
