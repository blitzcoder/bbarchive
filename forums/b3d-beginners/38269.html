AdrianT(Posted 2004) [#2]
B3d is way better than 3ds, It supports all of blitz texure and brush features, and with the pipeline extensions you can do a lot more besides, animate lights, fog, brushes, textures, and just about anything else using the 3dsmax keyframe editor the way you would for regular 3dsmax stuff.


N(Posted 2004) [#3]
Personally, I'd use my own format that contained definitions of portals, entities, and other things (more: paths, projectors, etc.), but if your world is small you might as well just go with B3D. Otherwise, you'd probably want to come up with your own format. B3D is easily extendable, so maybe you could just add in your own chunks and read it accordingly.
