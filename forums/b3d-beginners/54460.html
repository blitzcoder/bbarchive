big10p(Posted 2005) [#7]
First of all, forget about the w# coord - it's not used. Also, forget about the coord_set for now as this is only used for multi-texturing.

The u and v coords specify the horizontal and vertical coords of the texture to map to the vertex. However, these coords aren't expressed as pixel positions, they're a decimal fraction in the range 0.0 to 1.0. So, the top-left of the texture is u=0,v=0 and the bottom-right is u=1,v=1.
