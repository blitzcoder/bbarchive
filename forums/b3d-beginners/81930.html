big10p(Posted 2008) [#2]
It's been a while but I dont think you need to enter your name and registration code (BUID) to actually use Blitz3D. You enter your BUID on this site by clicking on the 'Account' tab at the top, then click on 'Register Products' in the menu bar. Doing so will get you full access to this website, including the ability to download product updates. You will need to do this as I think the version you get when you first buy Blitz3D is very old. Do this after you've registered by clicking on 'Product Updates' under the 'Account' tab. Have fun!
