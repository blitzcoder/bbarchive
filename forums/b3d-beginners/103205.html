->future proof (Hardwired uses directx9 and Blitz3d uses directx7 which is old and may have compatibility problems with the future versions of windows or with new graphics cards, for now i haven't seen any...)
->shaders to add some graphics effects

That's all.

The shaders can be very useful, especially to add per pixel lighting or bump effect, this really improves the appearance of the scene.

I think that it is important to not continually chase after the new best game engine but rather to finish your apps/games, then you can always learn a new language/syntax and convert it.
