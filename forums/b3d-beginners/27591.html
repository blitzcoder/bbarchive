Beaker(Posted 2003) [#11]
I know that this can vary depending on the version of Windows but try:
SetGadgetText label, "Hello My name is Alex"+Chr(10)+"I come from somewhere."
or
SetGadgetText label, "Hello My name is Alex"+Chr(13)+"I come from somewhere."
or
SetGadgetText label, "Hello My name is Alex"+Chr(10)+Chr(13)+"I come from somewhere."

They all work on Windows 98SE.
