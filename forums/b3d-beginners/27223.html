Ghoula(Posted 2003) [#2]
*.jpeg, *.bmp, and *.png formats are supported. PNGs are far superior. Don't know of a utility to chop up an animated *gif. If you have Animation Shop or something similiar you could do it manually.


GfK(Posted 2003) [#3]
you can load and play animated GIFs with OpenMovie/DrawMovie.

So in theory you could write a converter in Blitz - load the animated GIF and copy each frame manually into a single bitmap, then save the image buffer.

However, due to the nature of Blitz's movie commands, its likely that you'd get duplicate images, so you might have a bit of tidying up to do afterwards...
