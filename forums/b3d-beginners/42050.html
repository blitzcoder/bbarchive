TomToad(Posted 2004) [#6]
Dan23, under display properties, there should be some settings to change how the screen is displayed on the monitor. On my card I have to go to Advanced/GeForce5200/Screen Adjustment, it might be different on yours. You basically change the resolution of the card to what you want to adjust, then use the screen settings to move and resize until it fits properly. Then you switch to the next resolution and do the same thing, and so on until all resolutions fit on the monitor properly.
