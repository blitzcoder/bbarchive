Ugh.. this is blending, and I always get blend modes the wrong way around. I'm 99% sure it's Multiply Blend mode that you want. It will ignore white pixels and draw black pixels, thus eliminating the necessity to writepixel every pixel the way you're doing.

EntityBlend carshadow,2
EntityAlpha carshadow,0.25
