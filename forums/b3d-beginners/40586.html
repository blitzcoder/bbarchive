Rhyolite(Posted 2004) [#14]
Yeah, I believe they sometimes use 1000 bytes = kb and 1000 kb = 1 mb (instead of 1024 for both).

So,

128 MB x 1000 = 128,000 KB x 1000 = 128,000,000 Bytes

128,000,000 bytes / 1024 = 125,000 KB / 1024 = 122 MB

Close enough I guess ;)
