It will round correctly. :)

Graphics 800,600,0,2

SetBuffer BackBuffer()

While Not KeyDown(1)
Cls
xpos = MouseX()/32
ypos = MouseY()/32 

Text (10,10, "X: "+xpos+"  Y: "+ypos)

Flip

Wend
End
