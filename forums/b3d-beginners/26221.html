"My program needs to know when the client has logged out of my networked game."

You should never assume that you'll know when the client has logged out, because even if your game is designed to fix things up, you still have to watch out for lost internet connections or crashing PC's. So you should have the server clean stuff up and remove the client if it hasn't heard from them in a minute or so.
