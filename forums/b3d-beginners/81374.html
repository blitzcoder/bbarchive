Why are you guessing the dimensions? See Meshdepth, Meshheight and Meshwidth.

Also, you could just use fitmesh brick, -.5, -.5, -.5, 1, 1, 1 after you've loaded the brick.

Pongo(Posted 2008) [#4]
In wings, use the measurements in the upper left of the window to see dimensions.

One unit in Wings equals one unit in Blitz, so you simply need to get the wings measurement correct. A default cube in Wings is 2 units (-1 to 1 on each axis) If you use scale uniform and scale to 50% (use control key to snap in 10% intervals) you will end up with a 1x1x1 cube for reference.
