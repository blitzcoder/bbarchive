Hi everyone ! my bad ! I just found out the solution ! thanks everyone !
Here it is :
"EntityTexture V_Terrain_1_E,V_texture_Terrain1_E,0,0
ScaleTexture V_texture_Terrain1_E,TerrainSize(V_Terrain_1_E) / 8,TerrainSize(V_Terrain_1_E) / 8

EntityTexture V_Terrain_1_E,V_texture_Terrain2_E,0,1" the solution is to change the index value !
