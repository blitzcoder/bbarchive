GfK(Posted 2011) [#2]
Multipass rendering is what you need.

Pseudocode:
Wireframe False
CameraClsMode cam,true,true
Hide baddies
Show environment
RenderWorld()
Wireframe True
CameraClsMode cam,false,false
Show baddies
Hide environment
RenderWorld()
Flip
