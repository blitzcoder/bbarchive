Graythe(Posted 2004) [#4]
Hi BlueWolf,

There are two commands to rotate entities. The most obvious is ROTATEENTITY. It rotates an absolute angle from Zero degrees of rotation. So - "RotateEntity MyCube,90,0,0" will appear to work only once if called many times in strict succession.

The other command is TurnEntity - this rotates an entity relative to it's current rotation - so TurnEntity MyCube,1,0,0 will continually rotate the entity about it's x axis. Personally I find this the more handy of the two commands.

I hope it helps,
