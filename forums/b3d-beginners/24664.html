soja(Posted 2003) [#2]
Blitz3D is a superset of BlitzBasic (Blitz2D).
BlitzPlus is a superset of BlitzBasic (Blitz3D).

There are some items that are mutually exclusive between Blitz3D and BlitzPlus, for instance, GUI & event-based model, and 3D Blitz commands.

Search the forum and FAQ for other information -- this is a very common question.
