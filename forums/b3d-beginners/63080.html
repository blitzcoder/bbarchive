stayne(Posted 2006) [#3]
Do you have a sound editor? It sounds like your clip is not looped well. Think of a sine wave. In order to make a good loop you need to make the wave match at the beginning and the end. Sound Forge is pretty good at finding loop points. You can do it manually too.

If you want to do it well, find several samples at different pitches and blend them together according to acceleration.
