dynaman(Posted 2003) [#5]
Try changing the code at collsions 1,2,1,1 to

collisions 1,2,1,1
collisions 2,1,1,1

While Not KeyDown(1)
MoveEntity v_sprite1,x1#,0.02,z1#
updateworld
MoveEntity v_sprite2,x2#,-0.02,z2#
updateworld
RenderWorld
Flip 1
Wend


Spikenspan(Posted 2003) [#6]
I was missing the "UpdateWorld" keyword! Now it's working fine. I see you have to specify two "Collisions" statements to make two-way collisions between objects as well.
