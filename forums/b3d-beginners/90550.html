Serpent(Posted 2010) [#4]
It depends on what you are trying to do - if it is only a small game and you want to keep load-times down, you could preload everything - or most things - at the start. Unless you have a decent amout of media, there is nothing wrong with this. However, if your game is large it would normally be best to preload a few global things and then load level specific meshes, etc. when needed.
