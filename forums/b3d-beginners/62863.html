puki(Posted 2006) [#2]
Personally, I never use it.

Basically, it smooths out jagged lines on geometry, etc. It does make a difference to quality. However, it is a bit hit and miss with Blitz3D - some cards appear to represent it better than others via Blitz3D.

My advice is to not bother with it, unless you can see an improvement in visuals that you want. You might not notice much difference via Blitz3D.
