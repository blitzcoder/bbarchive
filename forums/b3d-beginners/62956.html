jfk EO-11110(Posted 2006) [#2]
Sounds like it's textured with a UV template bitmap, containing the unwrapped Wireframe. Open the model in Notepad and see what texture is referenced.

(BTW: opening the model in Notepad should be the very last choice, when the modeling tool won't help and if you really want to see plain text. Of course you may have to search for the texture filename in a lot of non-text data. Then again, if you have a notepad link on your desktop, it's opened quickly)
