dawlane(Posted 2013) [#2]
Have you changed both files to the resolution you require? To get a playing area of 1024x768 you would do.

In monkeygame.html
<embed src="MonkeyGame.swf" type="application/x-shockwave-flash" width="1024" height="768" wmode="direct">
Not forgetting that you would have to format the page correctly so you get the embedded swf in the right place.
And in monkeygame.ac
[SWF(width="1024",height="768")]

If you see any stretching are the graphics meant for use at those larger resolutions.
