Almost everyone of us use this self talk in his daily life.
It is this voice in the 'head' which i'm talking when i say 'Loud voice'.

Sometime you actively talk to yourself, sometime to calm yourself down.
Sometime you listen to it and get angry. And maybe you even do not realize that
there is this voice, because it is there and you converse with it on such frequent basis that you do not even question it.

That (above) is the type of voice which is common here on earth.
You recognize it, because it is pretty Loud and it seems to be always there.


But there is a second type of a voice. It is so silent, that, if you do not know that it is there, you would almost never discover it.

The next thing is, that the first type of voice does know when you are able to HEAR the silent voice, so it will always try to make you not listen to it.

And with this, it should be obvious, that you'll need to somehow quiet the Loud voice, to be able to discover and to listen to it.
(a word of warning: the Loud voice will try to speak you out of even trying to)

The silent voice does not talk with words, but when it talks and you hear it, you will understand.

It may take practice to recognize it, because it is covered with silence. But it is there.

So my question is, have you discovered it yet ?
