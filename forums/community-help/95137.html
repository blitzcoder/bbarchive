Download Audacity and the plugins pack; on the same site download the ogg exporter, install: load in your mp3's and export to ogg. There are various options that allow you to change the compression and audio quality during export.

And, best of all, it's all free! :)

JKP(Posted 2011) [#5]
My recommendation would be foobar2000 as it has never let me down and has excellent support for converting between various audio formats. However, like Audacity it does not come with an MP3 encoder/decoder for copyright reasons so you will need to download the LAME MP3 library yourself. When you start conversion foobar will ask you for the location where you installed LAME.
