And keep in mind: even if you AREN'T sending spam, you're still bound to a get a ton of people who down the road decide they are no longer interested in your mailings.

Instead of taking 5 second to properly unsubscribe, a lot of people (unfortunately) just drag them to their spam folder instead. 'problem' solved for them, but unfortunately a side-effect of that is that their ISP can flag you as a spammer and start rejecting all your stuff.
