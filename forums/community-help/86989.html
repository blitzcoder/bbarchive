Gabriel(Posted 2009) [#2]
There is an icon on your taskbar tray (bottom right by the clock as default) called safely remove hardware. Click it, then click the drive you want to remove. Wait until it tells you that it's now safe to remove your hardware, and then you may remove your hardware, safe in the knowledge that any data which was cached and not yet written has been written.
