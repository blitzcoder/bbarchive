ottykool(Posted 2013) [#12]
Most of Samsung tablet supports reading mp4 files.

So just convert your dvd movies to mp4 format then copy onto your tablet.

If your DVDs are home-made without decryption, Handbrake handle the job well. Or you will need to use other alternative to rip your DVDs and encode to common format.

I use AppGeeker DVD Ripper with good success. Most of DVDs I've been able to rip to my hard disk.

http://www.appgeeker.com/convert/rip-dvd-to-mp4-mac.html

BTW, if you like command line applications then FFmpeg works great too.
