Don't know about MSN messenger, but that automatic folder sharing is exactly what Dropbox does. It allows you to create a folder, and share subfolders with one or more other people. Any changes made to that folder are replicated to the computers of all the participants, including ipads or other tablets if you download the dropbox app.
