N(Posted 2009) [#2]
#define A B

Replaces A with B. So..
#define foo bar

transforms
foo(1, 2, 3)

into
bar(1, 2, 3)


Generally not a good idea to do this though, since it'll make your code very confusing when you have to look up what a macro does when you can't remember its purpose.
