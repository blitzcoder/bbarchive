But seriously: Technically, the physical conduit and the data allowance over said conduit can be billed separately. Just like you paid a flat fee to have a landline phone hookup, and get billed separately for each long distance call you made over that line.

So even though converting old copper connectivity to a fibre cable one may be free, the associated internet plan may be billed at a different rate.
