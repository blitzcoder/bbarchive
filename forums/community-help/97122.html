Hey guys..

Over the last few years i've made hundreds of tunes to slowly improve my skills with audio as a sub-hobby. In that time i've also forked out a lot of money for game resources, and while there are plenty of free resources out there I wanted to include myself as part of publishing free resources.

I have 25 songs released on the site now, and I compose 2 songs a week [Wednesdays and Sundays], then release them on WrathGames Studio.

It will either be useful, or not to you, but over time i'm slowly expanding genre/music tastes to meet the needs of other developers as much as I can. Over the next few months the site should be home to a large amount of usable resources in Game/Video projects.

Within the next 6-12 months i'll be paying a few artists to design some professional tilesets, sprites, etc for release as well.

Flash is the general focus but in case the content is useful for blitz members as well i'm just throwing it out there. Enjoy (or not)
