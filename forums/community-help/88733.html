xlsior(Posted 2010) [#3]
Depending on your version of Windows:

Right-click on "My Computer"
Manage
Storage -> Disk Management
Wait for it to enumerate all your drives
Find your USB drive in the list
Right-click the USB drive -> Change Drive Letter and Path
Remove the current drive letter, and add the one you want.

If for some reason the letter you want is already taken (for example by a card reader or network drive) then you'll need to change that one first so the driveletter is available to be assigned to the device you want it to use.
