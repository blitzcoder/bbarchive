Yasha(Posted 2011) [#2]
What do you need it for? If you were willing to use Psionic's models, I assume you weren't going to try to sell the game yet - they're highly recognizable. So if it's still in a testing/playing-by-yourself phase, you don't necessarily need to limit yourself to redistributable models.

i.e. Go and find an asset-extractor for your favourite game that matches your style on a modding site, and rip characters out of that. You'll have a more interesting test environment.

Just remember to replace them all before you distribute it to other people.

Alternatively, you could start low and use the Psionic models as a base - tweak the mesh and texture and animation in little places using an appropriate tool. Perhaps easier than starting from scratch. You could do this with ripped models too, but it would be a bit of a waste of effort since you still couldn't use them in public.

You may also have more success finding an artist who will work for free or cheap on an animation forum.

I too wish I could do any of this stuff.
