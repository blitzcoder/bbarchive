Just an FYI: Keen in mind that many/most webhosting solutions that provide 'oodles of storage' explicitly do NOT allow you to use it to store non-web accessible data for backup purposes. I know that godaddy for example considers that a violation or their terms of service.

Being able to "stream any song to any device via the web" is going to be a tough one, considering that each device has their own capabilities and limitations... Especially since you also want access restrictions and encryption, it's going to be near impossible to meet all your requirements.

I think it's extremely unlikely that you're going to be able to do all of the above with just plain vanilla file storage, you're most likely going to have need some kind of media server software involved...
