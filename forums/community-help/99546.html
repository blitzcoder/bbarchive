Derron(Posted 2012) [#2]
Just use whatever ad-service you want for your game.

As you said: it is a html5 game - so you can utilize all standard ad-styles. Even google ads.

10k unique hits a day ...they do not talk about unique visitors.
10k unique hits means: 10k of normal page renders.

Imagine you have a manager game and a player plays 10 minutes. and during that 10 minutes he fully-reloads 100 pages (not the small ajax updates but real "screen" switches) .. he has done 100 hits.

The days my website had 15k unique visitors a day I had about 60k-80k page impressions (and some thousands of downloads started).
Aehem... that website was really famous that time. So I imagine their "10k a day" is way to much for normal game devs.

As long as you do not find better alternatives: stay with good old google-ads etc. (remember to not show some bare skin - or sprites of cartoon condoms ... google does not like that).
