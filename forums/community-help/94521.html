iprice(Posted 2011) [#2]
Yep I got it working with a UK Wii and a 3DS.

You Press (not hold) A then B, then 1. Then you HOLD 2 for about 2 seconds and then DS connection message pops up. The DS connection logo then remains on the screen, even if you exit and restart. My 3DS had to be very close to the Wii though.

It definitely works :)
