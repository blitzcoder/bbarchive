_PJ_(Posted 2010) [#6]
I'm not a graphic designer, but whilst it all looks very neat and tidy (and crystal clear which is invaluable!), something about the white boxes seems a little 'clinical' to me.
I think a slight colour, (of course keep it light and soft, with a good strong contrast between the text and the background) would make the world of difference.
The same too for the 'title' Whilst I can appreciate the "Black Lioghts" may be the logo etc, having such a colourful background and the coloured buttons leaves the white areas looking like a piece of canvas left blank in a painting.

If it's just my eyes/resolution and they are not fully plain white, then ignore me, but otherwise, I really think just a very soft shading in pale blue\violet or contrasting pinky-orange-yellow thing would really help!

Other than tht, I think it's great. Too many such sites are often cluttered, messy and hard to actually spot the 'important' bits quickly. (Take Mmany bands' "MySpace" pages for numerous exmples). I appreciate this isn't the finished product, and it's the scheme/layout we're looking at here, but some images, whether cartoony or photos would go great on that front page too!
