Kryzon(Posted 2015) [#20]
I don't think you're exploring enough.
All your variations involve changing the symbol and the typeface (the "font"), but they all have that title and subtitle layout.

You won't find something new and original if you don't agressively explore new layouts and designs (best done with pencil and paper so you can quickly try new ideas).
How much reference did you collect for this design project? You need to be inspired in order to design something beautiful. You can collect images of not only existing logos, but any kind of design or style that inspires or interests you.

Before you start collecting references, you need to answer this to yourself: what are you trying to achieve with your logo, what is the message you want it to convey?
