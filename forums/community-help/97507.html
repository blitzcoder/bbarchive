Is there different between i5(3.1GHZ Quadcore) and i7(3.4GHZ Quadcore)?


xlsior(Posted 2012) [#5]
Aside from the slightly higher clockspeed, the i7 support shyperthreading while the i5 does not.

(i5 has 4 cores, 4 threads. The i7 has 4 cores, 8 threads)
Whether that makes any difference greatly depends on the software you're running, for certain programs hyperthreading can make a noticable difference, while you won't notice it at all for other programs.
