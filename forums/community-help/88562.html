If it sounds too good to be true, it generally is.

That said, Adobe is one of those companies that charges a LOT more in the UK than in other countries, like the US -- so it's possible that these are 'grey' imports, where the reseller buys them directly in the US and resells in the UK, instead of following Adobe's official retail channels.

But given that Adobe pretty much sets their own prices in stone, you'll find little variation in pricing across vendors.

for some US mainstream outlets for software, check newegg.com and amazon.com (no idea what their policies are regarding international shipping and/or non-US creditcards, though)
