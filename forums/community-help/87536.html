GfK(Posted 2009) [#2]
Its not just Vista - every version of Windows right back to Win95 has done this from time to time.

Go into My Computer>>Organise>>Folder and Search Options>>View.

Make sure "Remember each folder's view settings" is ticked. Or you could just hit 'reset'. Might work.
