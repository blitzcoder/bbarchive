xlsior(Posted 2009) [#2]
Quickest thing to try: hook it up to a different PC, and see what it says.

If it works OK there, then it was either your PC hardware or windows giving you the finger.
If it fails there too, then the drive and/or drive enclosure is knackered.

Or something else to try: connect it to a different USB port. Especially if you have a USB_powered enclosure (2.5" drive?) there's a chance it may be a power-issue.
