Things I'd try

Hold down the printer self test buttons and make sure it prints at all. Your printer manual (which you should be able to download) will tell you which these are.

Switch USB ports
Fully power down (as in unplug) printer
Check paper OK
Check toner OK
(maybe) check USB cable (try a different one)

Then I guess I'd do something like:

Start->Control Panel->System (Or windows key + pause)
Check your OS version (32 x 64)
Go to Lexmark site and download the right driver package and unpack it to desktop
Start->Devices and Printers and right click on your printer
Printer Properties->Advanced->New Driver->Have Disk and steer to your fresh driver download and install
