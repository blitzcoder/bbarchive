Hello all,

I've been working on this top down corridor shooter (think something similar to Alien Breed except with zombies not aliens, and these zombies are not just mindless shambling automatons but carry flamethrowers, rocketlaunchers, shotguns, emit poison gas etc, have multiple pairs of arms etc). The player has to find/rescue the surviving scientist on each level, and then make his way to the exit. Along the way enemies drop ammo and credits/cash which can be picked up and spent at the computer terminal on each level to purchase equipment and better guns. Not only that but each level has a time limit before the level is flooded with poison gas meaning the player has to be quick.

That's a basic overview. Currently all graphics are placeholder but our artist will begin working on them soon enough - and it's a lot of fun for all who've played it.


Now I'm struggling for a title. The backdrop is that the player is a soldier in a 'monolithic corporate entity' enlisted to put down a zombie virus outbreak in a research facility...The gameplay is action packed fast paced with lots of firepower.
