I've ordered plenty of things myself there over the years, and never had any problems. Anyway, if you're wondering how good or bad a particular online company is, I'd suggest taking a look at resellerratings.com -- people can rate their experiences with various companies there, so you get a bit of an idea how reputable they are.

ima747(Posted 2011) [#10]
I find their standard shipping a little slow, but with a prime account I buy just about everything online from them (except computer hardware, for that newegg is the king).

Take note of who you're buying from THROUGH amazon. Other retailers/individuals/etc. can sell through amazon, sometimes they're better than amazon, sometimes note, check reviews of the seller if it's not coming direct from amazon. Amazon can also do the handling for other people... nothing wrong with any of that, just something to be aware of (so you know who is supposed to put what you want in a box).

As others have said look for their country specific site if possible, you're likely to get faster/cheaper shipments and localized customer support.
