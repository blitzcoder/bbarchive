It will be probably cheaper to buy a cheap PC to do the job than to hire a company to do it. You can get some cheap laptops with FireWire for around $500. Some older Sony CamCorders have USB 2.0 and FireWire, and the quality is purely depending on the streaming speed. So if you want full quality, you need FireWire.
