When used with "normal mapping" or "bump mapping" you will create the look of the "bumps" every 5 cm (when your polygons end)

like the one in the middle of this pic linked by AdamStrange:




For solar panels: use a solar panel texture - and if your model does not have enough details (metal bones) you will have to draw them on the texture too.


Pay attention to methods like baking textures: you create a high res model and bake the details into a texture (google for it - there are some blender tutorials for this topic).
