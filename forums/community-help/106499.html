xlsior(Posted 2016) [#2]
In all likelihood, it's extremely unlikely since most devices use some proprietary way of accessing/updating, plus many modern devices use cryptographically signed firmware to prevent 3rd party tampering.. Aside from the fact that the firmware will be the equivalent of executable code for which you don't have the source...

So unless theirs already a vibrant ecosystem of 3rd party ROMs for your specific device, more than likely your out of luck.
