Typical - post on a forum, figure it out two minutes later!

For anybody wondering (or for me wondering nine years from now):
1. Click just inside a cell at the left edge (when the mouse pointer becomes a small black arrow).
2. Drag the edge of the cell.

If you don't do step 1, it just resizes the whole column instead of just for that row.
