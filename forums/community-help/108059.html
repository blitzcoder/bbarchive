Yue(Posted May) [#4]
Hi, already solved the problem. The animations work correctly in Blitz3D but not in Xors3D. The problem is that Xors3D, does not allow loading a model which has bones without vertices assigned to the model. The mistake is that the model has a bone in the back and others in the hands that do not have assigned vertices, these are to carry backpacks or have objects with the hands.

In Blitz3D this is not a problem, but in Xors3D, the requirement is that those bones have a vertex assigned at least.



All this I discovered after pruba and error. It's awful.
