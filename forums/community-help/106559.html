xlsior(Posted 2016) [#2]
Three options:

1) Mount the iso using a virtual CD program like Daemontools Lite/free. (Win8 and Win10 may have iso mounting built in)
2) Extract the contents using 7-zip or WinRar. Depending on the contents of the iso, this may or may not work for you. Some programs check to see if they are launched from CD
3) Use CD/DVD burning software to burn the iso image to a physical CD/DVD, and read that. (Note: you can't just burn the .iso file onto a data disc, you'd have to pick the option to actually burn a disc image and give it the .iso as a source image)
