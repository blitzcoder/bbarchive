GfK(Posted 2013) [#5]
Forget the volts. Everything in the UK operates on 230/240V AC.

Any 3A fuse will be fine. However, if your previous 3A fuse has blown, its quite likely that a replacement will blow, too, as the device it is protecting almost certainly has a fault. Fuses are simple devices and they don't normally fail for no reason.

Oh, and just to be clear - don't be tempted to just put in a 5 or 13A fuse if the new 3A does blow. At best you'll finish off your Vita.
