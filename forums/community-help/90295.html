xlsior(Posted 2010) [#2]
Tip: There's quite a few pieces of music out there licensed under 'Creative Commons' (CC) that are free to use. There's different sub-licenses as well, e.g. some music can only be used in non-commercial projects, others require a mention in your credits, some only allow you to use it as-is without modification, etc... But perhaps a good starting point for a google search?
