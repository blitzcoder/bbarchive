Adam Novagen(Posted 2012) [#11]
Two things. One is that the default hyperlink blue doesn't blend well with the green background especially for people with substandard eyesight; try changing those link colours. The other is that, at the bottom of each page, you really should have PREV/NEXT links as well as the BACK link, or at the very least make each "page" of the manual link to the next one if the reader clicks on it. Standard webdoc navigation convenience these days.
