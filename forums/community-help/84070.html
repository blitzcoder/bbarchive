xlsior(Posted 2009) [#11]
If you boot from the Vista Install DVD, there's a 'repair boot' option, which will do the fixmbr and other things to straighten out most boot problems.

There are several levels of fixes, so you may need to choose the 'repair boot' multiple times. (I restored a disk image of my vista install that wouldn't start either, had to do the repair boot thing two or three times in a row, but after that everything was OK and it booted up fine without needing to do a full blown repair or reinstall.
