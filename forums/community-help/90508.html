xlsior(Posted 2010) [#3]
The vast majority of notebooks do not allow you to replace the video card. Few do, plus there are a couple that support an 'enhanced' docking station which can include expansion ports that may allow you to put a 3rd party video adapter in.

Most of the time you're just out of luck, though.
