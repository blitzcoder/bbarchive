Kryzon(Posted 2011) [#2]
Hello Mark,
I hope I'm not too rude, but I simply googled "SKP to 3DS" and the first result that comes up has a guide on how to do that using a free tool from AutoDesk called "FBX Converter".
I imagine the model you already have you downloaded from Sketchup's 3DWarehouse (that's why it's in .SKP format).
But you are also allowed to download it in .DAE (Collada) format, which FBX Converter can convert to .FBX and then from .FBX to .3DS (it's all in the guide).

So, bottom line:
- Download the Sketchup Flak 88 model again, but choose the Collada file (it comes in a .ZIP).
- Google "SKP to 3DS" and look for a "Dev Shed" forum thread result, it should be at the top of the results. This is the guide telling you what to do.
