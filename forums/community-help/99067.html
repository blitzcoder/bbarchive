One question. What should be the proper distance to set the DOF effect?




Yasha(Posted 2012) [#2]
It should be based on what the player is looking at.

However, since it's near-impossible for you to tell what the player is looking at (you merely know where the camera is aimed), I would strongly recommend never using DOF at all. Even in the best case, it won't improve a scene because it's entirely based around affecting the things that aren't supposed to have the player's attention, wasting GPU time. In the worst case (likely unless it's tuned very finely), it will be distracting and very annoying.
