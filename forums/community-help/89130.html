I got an asus 1201N netbook, which has nvidia's ion chipset, built-in HDMI & VGA, and a dual core processor. It's fast enough to decode HD video, and a pretty nice unit overall.

Biggest downside of any of the netbook/tablet solutions is that they typically lack an internal optical drive, so if you wish to play DVD's you either need to rip them to disk first or get an external USB DVD drive.
(Around $50 or so for a slimline, buspowered drive)
