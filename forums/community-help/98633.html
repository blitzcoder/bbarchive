xlsior(Posted 2012) [#3]
Just one word of warning: There are two different pin layouts in use by motherboard on-board USB headers, and they are NOT interchangable.

Each header supports two USB ports, and has two rows of pins. On some motherports the two rows are identical, and on some motherboards the second row is flipped backwards.

Before plugging in the cable, MAKE SURE that the wiring order of the hub matches that of your motherboard, or you risk frying the port on the motherboard. (Found that one out the hard way myself a few years ago, as soon as I plugged in a mouse to the USB port, my motherboard literally started smoking and one of the USB controllers shorted itself out)
