Generally AMD will be cheaper, but intel's current generation CPU's are significantly faster -- check out cpubenchmark.net to compare individual processors.

(Oh, by the way -- be aware that most of the mobile CPU's have less cores than their desktop equivalents, so you can't just go by the model name/number and Ghz to compare -- look at the actual benchmark scores. for example, the mobile i5 and i7 CPU's are all dual core, while the desktop i5 is quad-core, and the desktop i7 can have 4-6 cores)
