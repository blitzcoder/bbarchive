Qube(Posted 2009) [#8]
WiiConnect24 is free and will keep an active connection to the internet via WiFi (will not suck bandwidth usage). It will let you know when there is a system update, a friend sends you a message or one of the channels you subscribe to has new information, for example a new competition has begun on Mario Kart Wii (if you've added the Mario Kart Channel).

Wii Connect 24 does not contain games... For that you need to connect to the Wii Store (as mentioned above) and purchase games via Wii Ware or for older SNES, NES, C64 games via Virtual Console.

For Wii Store you need points. Points cost anywhere from £7 for 1000 points upwards and can be bought via your Wii console with you credit / debit card.
