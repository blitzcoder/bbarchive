DGuy(Posted 2011) [#5]
(You can find RETINA_ENABLED at the top of main.h)

RETINA_ENABLED = FALSE
iPad: 768x1024
Non-Retina: 320x480
Retina: 320x480 (pixel-doubled)

RETINA_ENABLED = TRUE
iPad: 768x1024
Non-Retina: 320x480
Retina: 640x960

The above are, of course, device orientation dependent.
