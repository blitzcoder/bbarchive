To do this in iOS you change Product Name in Xcode/Target Build Settings.



Alternatively, if you want to do it by hand you can go into the MonkeyGame-Info.plist and change Bundle Identifier. You'd also want to change the Bundle Display Name (what appears under the icon) and Bundle Name (16 character limit on this one) for completeness.
