Chapter I
=========

Candy was a little dog we raised in our little family from 1986 to 2001... She was very kind and she was a really beautiful female dog. In 2001 she has a great sickness. She died in April 2001 at 14 1/2 years old...

2001, In that time I has nothing to play on my PC... Nothing in 3D !

Four years (2005) later I learned Blitz and started to model Candy the best I can I arrived with this :
