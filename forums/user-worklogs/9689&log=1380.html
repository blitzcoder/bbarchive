Grand Re-Opening(Posted 2006-10-05)
Okay, enough slacking! BB.com, I am SO back out there!

I dunno what happened when the worklogs went *pffft*, but I 've now transferred all necessary images & files to get this thing back in action - as is my "company," Gigahertz Games! All in the two days since I turned 15! (Happy birthday to me, happy birthday to me...)

So, for those of you who don't already know, Blox is a classic style of ball-and-paddle game, (a la BlasterBall/Arkanoid/Ricochet/Breakout/etc) with a few major twists, including combo moves for MAJOR points, (I'm talkin' millions per level!) 50 gorgeous levels, powerups that do everything from teleporting the ball, to making it split into a hundred copies, and a custom level editor so you can build 50 of your OWN levels!!!

If you think this game sounds cool, fun, interesting, mildly entertaining, or even just a piece of crap, then email me at gigamaster.adam@... - I'd love to hear what you have to say! You should also email me if you love Blitz Basic, or are interested in joining Gigahertz Games, or at least being in contact with us, or even if you think Sly Cooper is cool!
