Subracer(Posted 2002-09-01)
Well, this is my very first time in this worklogs space...
I'm working (as someone knows) on a underwater racing game " SUBRACER " for IDIGICON
Here some features....

- Fast, adrenalitic
- Every racer has his weapons and look
- Racer physic features differs from each other
- Water Physic and underwater ambients
- About 25 racing tracks
- More than 10 awesome tunes all jungle/techno punk (ala Prodigy and Chemical ) :)
- Player career with xperience points to reach extra tracks
- Full of particles and special effects (featured by PSYS)
- Copletely configurable (xtra packs will be realized)
- It's made completely in Blitz3D!
