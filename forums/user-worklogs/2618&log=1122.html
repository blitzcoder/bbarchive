BumperBoats(Posted 2002-05-18)
Working on a Bumper Boat simulation - these are interesting beasts as the physics and actions are very "response driven" i.e. turn the direction too fast and the boat spins rather than changing direction. Also even for slow control movements the boat will "crab" then eventually straighten.

Stage- made the models, the heightmap, colourmap and are starting on code
