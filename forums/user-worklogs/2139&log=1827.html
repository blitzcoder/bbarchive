Draft narrative(Posted 2011-08-04)
Relations developed between United (Con)Federation of Planets and the Argalian? Imperium to better counter increasing incursion outside the (Con)federations borders at which resources ferried by free traders for the (Con)Federations consumption arrives. Regrettably the region is become a hotbed of pirate activity as the Imperial Navy number are currently fully committed at their own borders with the Ta'gral.

The Argalian fleet has agreed to undertake escort missions involving the merchant fleet travelling Imperial spacelanes. A number of fighters and bombers have been made available for escort duty. All the ships available are of Argalian specification and differ somewhat from (Con)federate ships. They are smaller but are considered at least equal in terms of operability, firepower and armour. Whilst Argalia posesses heavier ships these are already assigned to those purposes essential to Argalian security and are unlikely to remain in any region for sufficient time to enjoin any local skirmish but are a regular feature seen by patrols and escorts thru the region.

The Imperium has also agreed to an exchange program of officers. Partly because they now have more ships than crews available due to plagues affecting the inner planetary systems. Fortunately these are well outside the main trade routes and Confederate officers will not be exposed.

Procurement of meshes(Posted 2011-08-04)
Possible external source for meshes: http://solcommand.blogspot.com/

Here a variety of sci-fi meshes are available provisionally for download at nil cost. The models appear to be unique and well crafted and are of sufficient variety to populate, probably, the projects initial requirement. Permission is already granted to alter the meshes to user requirements. They are each of a single surface and will require some work to isolate and manipulate such features as gun turrets, cargo containers, bay doors and escape pods etc. Possibly the designer will be willing to undertake some or all work on the basis of eventual profit from the venture. Most likely any other meshes required may be created procedurally such as planets, stars, moons, comets and asteroids.
