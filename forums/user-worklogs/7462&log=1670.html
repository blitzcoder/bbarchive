version 0.97 is drawing near !2009-07-13
Hi all, I hope I waited enough for getting the more
information I could.

This game got restarted in June after 2 months of delay,
so we got no Candy World Adventure updates since then.

But I have a great news for you blitzers: Candy World
Adventure is Back !

Now with these new features:
12 Worlds
Candy's House
Toopy's Ball
Freesbies
Boomrang
Music

Now with these new effects:
Better gravity
This feature is set on particles and the Ball.
It's making the best I can for 2D games.

Particles
Every actions for bonus and themes needs particle. Some are
simple and use trajectory such like fireflies, but others
uses gravity such like Hearts.

Actually, I may call this game Candy World Adventure : The Fate of Lillians



Nowaday, several changes occured in the Candy World Adventure issue...

-Worlds got enlarged in world 3 and 5, cause those worlds
were only having 100 candy bones and 10 crecents:
Now they have the maximum of Bones
and Crecents: 200 and 20.

-Two new ennemies: Muhh and Sharkies. I may add some more.
Muhh are a kind of monster blended with ghost, he's
fully functionnal Sharkies are a evil shark
who will swim in any direction
to get you.

-New pals named:
Toopy the little dog.
Maory the grey cat.
Nooky the Dolphin.

I've added a whole temple but not complete and is basically
the Owl's temple. Yeah, you will have to save a owl.
Dorka the owl !

New ennemies, new worlds, new pals, new tiles that what
I'm going to add in the remaining development time.

The game will be divided in three parts:
The first one will be for beguiners.
The second will be for intermediare.
The third one will be for
advanced gamers.

Those three phases of the game will be a completion of a
huge mission. Players will have to freed Lillians
in order to defeat the evil doctor.

Every new worlds of the second and third part of the game,
which includes 24 worlds, will be more difficult.

36 worlds in all, that
not alot you may say.

Dont worry, there will be alot of places to visit before a phase in the game finish. Worlds are huge!

First attempt in the 2D world2009-03-15
Xilvan Design are not just making games for money
and offers now great games for free!

Having not a 2D Candy World in our gaming library,
we thought that it would be nice to release
a entirely 2D Candy World game
for the public.

In 2008 the project were unsuccessfully started and
a few graphics were made such as artworks and sprites


Wednesday, March 11 2009
The developpement of the game Candy World Adventure:
Prelude of Lillies started from scratch with
few graphics and great ambition.

3 days later, the version 0.2 of the game is out and offers
altmost all the basic features of the game. The overall
graphics and physics engine is done.

The basic actions such like walking, running
and jumping are available.

In the game, Toopy is altmost kidnapped by Doctor Picpic
who is back from his exodus. He has stolen many mimzy
in the whole world and try to steal Candy's friends.

Dont worry, Toopy is free in the game at the beguining
and will help you in your progress.

In the demo, you must find a button to go further the
way... By exploring the game enough, you'll find it.
By pressing the R(Ressort) button, you will
activate the springs ahead and be able
to explore more of the first
level I've done yet.
