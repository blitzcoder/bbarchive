First day of Candy's Space Adventures: Messages from the galaxies !2013-02-25
This is the end of the Candy in Space I, II & III,
it was a trilogy and the beguining of
a new era for Xilvan Design.
____

Lets start a new
Project callen:

Candy's Space
Adventures:

The Messages from
the Galaxies.

The game will take place in 7 nearby galaxies.
There will be altmost 4-10 solar systems per
galaxies. 25-100 planets per galaxies. Most
of them will be hostiles and should
need terraforming.

Each planet will be probably inhabited by
little creatures, the Lillians type
will be a few of them.

The Planets' terrains will be spherical
and the surface will be drawn randomly.
It must be normal render of planets
and asteroids will be enhenced.

For the moment, nothing is coded. I will start
from an empty page and ideas will soon
be added to the list.
____

List of ideas for this brand new game:
_______________________________

(1)- The goal will be to localise the
Milky Way through 7 galaxies.

(2)- It will be possible to create
new worlds with an editor.

(3)- Thousands of Randomly
created worlds to explore.

(4)- Grow lives( animals, trees, plants
or flowers ) and/or factories.

(5)- Beat evil aliens;
Fight for good ones.

(6)- Sell items to good aliens;
Avoid evil aliens ships.

(7)- Enhence your spaceship
( Many of choices ).

P.S. It will be in 3D.
