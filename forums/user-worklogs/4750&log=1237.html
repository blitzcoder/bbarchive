Mutant Gene Software: Cardwar - The Vault - Drei Fragezeichen Archiv - Pocket Radio Player

Cardwar(Posted 2003-05-24)
Cardwar is a simple yet fun and highly addictive card game where you match card together.
If you get a wrong pair the cards are turned back over, you must remember where that card was before.

Cardwar has multiple levels of gameplay, starting from a beginners level with only a few cards,
all the way to the high levels with cards filling the screen!

The game is user friendly, accomodating user accounts so that each person may choose their own
personal settings including picking any of the 11 languages.

Your high scores are stored so that you can show your friend how good you are!

Feeling creative? - Make yourself your own card pack by using the Cardwar Deck Builder or download new ones from the website!

Enjoy your own type of music while gaming?
Carwar has a music player feature which allows you to listen to music in many different file formats.

One of the best parts is: there's more to come!

If you want to send in your own cards / decks please mail them to me.

If you have any constructive suggestions for the game or questions of any kind please let me know.
