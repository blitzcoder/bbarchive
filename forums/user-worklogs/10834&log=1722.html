In the Beginning(Posted 2009-12-02)
My friend and I have decided to make a game one day. I have been coding in blitz3d, of and on, for about 5 years. I had never tried to make a real game yet so this is very new to me. My friend already had a full storyline with at least 30 in depth characters and is called the Pirate Show. It may sound dumb and shallow but it is a very genius creation. Because this was the only story we had we decided to make a rpg with it. As of now he is working on the main character, a sea otter named Connor. Yes, every character is an animal. I purchased fast extension and i am now planning on getting JV-Ode and AC3D. This project is really helping me learn more about gaming as it is my planned career. I am only a sophomore in high school as of now. Well I could go further but i do now want to write a biography. So i will try to keep this log updated as well as i can.
