Dungeons and Magic (working title)(Posted 2002-12-27)
Hi everybody!

I just made a movie of my newest project. Dunno if you guys are interested but here's the story (in short). It's about a female mage who's, yet, very limited in her skils. She has to retrieve a very powerfull magic rod from the depths of the aincient dungeons infested by deamons and other foul fiends. The young mage's master has entered the dungeons before her to recapture the rod, but he has not been seen for weeks. So now lies upon they to find the rod before the evil deamon gets his filthy hands on it.
Okay I realise I'm not good at writing stories but the guy who writes them usually is on vacation...ghehehe. Here's the deal with the gameplay. It's a 3rd person shoot 'em up ala Drakan, only it has more of a mage then a warrior for main character. You have to learn spells from ancient artifacts you find in the dungeons and cast them at your opponents using a zelda64 style target system. Perhaps we'll even add a certains spell casting system where you'll have to move yer mouse in certains figures to cast spells. Just thoughts, just thoughts.
The game will be based upon bsp cos it's mostly indoor. And it'll ofcourse be decorated by beautyfull 3ds/md2/b3d models. Below here you'll find some media to get sneak inside the production:) Please comment us so we can improve our game;)
