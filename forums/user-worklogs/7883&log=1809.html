All about the Rank(Posted 2011-03-05)
Ranks will help you on your progress over the course of the game

You will start the Game with $0 the main currency of the game

Complete a level and you will recieve $250
depending on the levels difficulty

in addition to the $250 fund you will earn Cp(Construction Points

PayDay = $250 * Difficulty
CP (Difficulty * Stars)
Getting 5 stars in a Achievement grants 50cp $250
getting 21 stars gives £2500 250cp

So for a level 1 Difficulty
MaxWin
$3750 = ($250*1)+($250*4)+$2500
Cp321 = (1cp*21)+(50cp*4)+250cp

The GameDesign(Posted 2011-03-05)
The Core Element of the game is Physics

Each Level will consist of
a. Complete Target
b. Timed Target
c. Score Target
d. Budget Target
e. Bonus Target

a = a single achievement
b,c,d,e give ranks


a. redirect the energy ball to the power powercell

b. Ranked time 60,70,80,90,100+

c. Score 50k 30k 20k 10k 5k

d. Budget spending 1000,2000,3000,5000,10000

e. Release the Bonus Balls 4,3,2,1,0

this will determine your overall rank
