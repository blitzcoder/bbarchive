Compo now on!(Posted 2009-04-16)
The first Blitzmax Community framework competition is now open!

The Theme for this competition is RETRO!



To enter, make a game in a retro theme that has features that we can use in the community framework. You don’t have to use any of the framework in your code. Entries must not contain copyrighted images etc, because the framework is released under a MIT licence.

For example…

A simple shooter with a 2d particle system.
A platform game with tilemap code.
A point and click game with pathfinding system.


Post you entries here!

The deadline is 8th may 2009 (3 weeks from Saturday)

More details on the framework, competition, sponsors and prizes.

There are prizes for first, second, third and small prize for most entertaining runner up. The first 3 winners get a choice of prize from our prize pool. First position choosing first, the second place gets the choice from the remaining prizes then the third place winner.
All three winners will get a free copy of Bullet Candy as an extra prize!
