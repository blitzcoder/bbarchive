New Demo version 0.8(Posted 2010-10-01)
Well, this is another "space" shooter in 3d . It is a pale copy of similar games like "Blazing angels" or "Rogue Squadron"and the likes
coded in Blitz3D + FastExt libraries.
It is my second attempt to mess with blitz3D.
Mainly developped for my nephew's next birthday.
It will be free (but I keep all the intellectual property and rights)
There is about 1 year of developpement already, hope you shall find semi pro gameplay.

You take control of different ships from the Valkyrie squadron (light figthter , heavy fighter, bomber , etc ...)
Mouse , keyboard, and joystick control are available.
Project is still in beta developpement.
So far ground attack , bombing, and dog fights are almost fully implemented.
Space Environment and some additional eye candy stuff have been added. Thanks to some greate routine by kishan
