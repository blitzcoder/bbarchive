Itemizer 0.9 Beta...(Posted 2003-10-10)
Itemizer is a "Droplet-Clone" where you
can place your items (Meshes, Sprites, Lights) in
"raeltime" into your B3D-Levels.
You can scale and rotate the items and change
their properties.

In this early version of Itemizer, you have to change
the itemizer.ini manually to fit it for your own
projects.

Read the readme.txt and have a look at itemizer.ini...

You can download Itemizer 0.9 Beta here.

next to come:
-------------

- adding a Itemizer-Project-File:
the project-file will be created automatically when
you choose "New Project".
Then you will be asked for the project-properties like
- path and filename of the B3D-Level
- Itempath
- Imagepath
a new itemizer.ini with default items will be created.

- adding new items to itemizer.ini
you may add new items to itemizer.ini directly from
the program.
