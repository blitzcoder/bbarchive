The Story revealed(Posted 2011-05-13)
The story of my upcoming game, Candy to the Rescue II starts like this:

Ten years ago, Dark clouds were surrounding The Lillians Planet,
Candy was on her way to her home and she did not wanted to get wet.

Suddently, she got trapped by a weird man.

Noopy searched her everywhere, he found nothing.

One of the other Noopy said : A weird man kidnaped Candy and went away.
We must hurry to save her !

All four dogs of the level are reunited to search.
They are all following the footsteps they have found near Candy's House.
They are leading to a mysterious Portal near The Queen of Spades Castle.

With brand new levels, obstacles and challenges, it will also allow a better scenario.
