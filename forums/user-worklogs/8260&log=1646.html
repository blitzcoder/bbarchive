Hmmm! What no downloads?(Posted 2008-10-23)
Well nearly a week and hardly any downloads and no sales!

So just need to figure out a way to get my site onto the fast lane of the internet highway, as at the moment it seems to be stuck in a slow lane!

Anyway early days!

Just cross platformed the game to the Mac and it was an almost painless transition...

0. Get BlitzMax for Mac.
1. Ensure you have the Xcode applications installed (1.0 Gb download).
2. ?win32 everything that refers to windows specific including directX!
3. Build and Run!
4. Learn how to create a .dmg mountable file for distribution.

That's it Qbix now works on the Mac!

So if what most game developers are saying is true...

The Mac market is smaller therefore less competition so more chance to make an impact!

...then fingers crossed and who knows this time next week I might even have a sale!
