Conceptualizing(Posted 2005-11-19)
I felt creative last night while deciding on what my next project would be. While brainstorming I decided to make a 2d hybrid platformer of spanky's quest, lemmings, and a little bit of bejeweled. It may sound like a complex game, but what I have planned is actually really simple and hopefully fun.

The object of the game is to locate all of the gems in each level, which hold captive forest creatures, and free them. Once they have been freed from the gem(s) they will make their way to a 'exit' lemmings style(sorta). There will be a number of things blocking their path, it's up to you to control a mouse-the hero and make a path clear of danger.
