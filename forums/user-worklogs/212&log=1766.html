First entry(Posted 2010-07-19)
I started writing code for this game a while ago, right now you can move your tank around, and fire. Your shots bounce off the walls and any blocks on the map. The map will be simple blocks which will deflect your shots.
I plan to have a two player mode and a single player mode against enemy AI. eNEMy AI will either be other tanks or turrets. I want the enemy AI to look natural, so there'll be a large amount of AI code to write.
