Candy World II: The Golden Bones are back!2009-08-10
Candy World II: The Golden Bones is a 3D plateformer game made
back in 2005-2009 by the creator of Lights of Dreams,
Xylvan.
It comes with the best I can in 3D for Blitz3D
language and engine.

Lately, many bugs has been removed, so the finalised
version is destined to be published in September 2009.

I written this blog to present you the best 3D plateformer
I ever created, a plateformer who helped
me alot in my life.

We take the origins of Candy World back to the 90'
the year 1998 exactly, the year where the dog simulation
game named Save Candy were made in secret on my
Commodore128.

The game were using more than one dozens of dog
sprites and a simple screen with a ben.

In the ben, two dogs of different color and maybe shape
were waiting for command. The only command I programmed
were walk and changing of dog. It was in 2D
and looked so simple compared to
Candy World Adventure.

Back in 2001, I was planning to make anoter Save Candy
Plateformer, but it was cancelled due to lack of basics.
In 2006 we was starting the "real" Candy World 3D
game with all the ideas i needed to make
a game like any plateformers.

Development are made of models, sprites, terrain, sky, water. The Bonus are Bones, Crescents and Hearts.
The weapons are Freesbies, Boomrang, Noopy's Ball,
Ice breath & fire breath.

More likely this game was going to be released many times on Download.com. But there was some sales. I presume people were waiting for final product. Until today, I've made some enhencements and debuguing for this game.

Hope you will enjoy the
finalised version
of this game:

v2.07!
