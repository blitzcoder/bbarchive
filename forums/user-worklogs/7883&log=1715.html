Physic System Test 3a(Posted 2009-11-05)
The Extreme Tests have given me a good insight into the do/dont's of Newton

So the Target for the Next Test is to set up a very basic level

it will be a collection of platforms with emptyspace the target is to make a chain from the Hammer all the Way to the Button

Alongside my testing ive been working on two placement gizmo's

1. Single Click to place use buttons to move/rotate
2. Line Click two Points creates a line of dom's from point 1 to 2 and facing point 2

What is the Hammer ?
This currently is a level Object with the press of space bar an UpVector on a Pivoting Hammer is Destroyed causing the hammer to fall ie starting a Domino chain
