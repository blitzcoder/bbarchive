fond farewell(Posted 2015-05-02)
As loads of you probably remember I had been working on Elite Multiplayer on and off for several year's till about four years ago about the time Elite Dangerous was mentioned.

When that was started I got a 'Cease and desist' so production stopped.

On the whole the product has been canned and ideas and code has made its way into Star Rogue now I do still ask about EM from time to time but last time I got a definitive NO from Michael Brooks :(
