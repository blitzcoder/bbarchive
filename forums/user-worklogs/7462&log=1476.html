The final controls of LOD(Posted 2007-09-28)
BASIC CONTROL
Enter Talk
Space Jump
WA Go front back
AD Sidestep...
or Arrows Control of direction

SECONDARY CONTROL
Shift Left/Right Run fast
Ctrl Left/Right Parry
E Walk very slow

CAMERA CONTROL
C Control Camera
End Turn Camera around with arrows
XZ Look UP/Down

MAP CONTROL
M Show Map
O Show Mission
I Inventory
F2 View Controls

MOUSE CONTROL
LEFT CLICK Attack
MIDDLE CLICK Choose Item
RIGHT CLICK Use Item

September 27 2007 Studio Review(Posted 2007-09-28)
I've finally done great with the trees...
The leaves sprites, trunks and branches' textures are now altmost neater.

It was the last day of LOD's making phase... We will look for a publisher such like Midway or Ubisoft...

Fryday 28 September 2007 will be the game's final analysing and we will release a patch when we will be done with the whole beta testing...

I'm heading now to:
1) Legends of Dreams Online:
-A MMORPG Adventure game made from LOD

2)Angels of Dreams : The captive ones
-A fully improved and more addictive prequel of LOD inspired from the amazing 2d game from Xilvan Design Soul of Sphere Platinum... Same Story, same and more characters, and amazing 3d graphics... not yet started but devoted to facinate the next generation of blitz gamers...

3)Candy World
-A kids or all ages game oriented on action and adventure where a female dog named Candy must gather 100 bones per levels to acceed the next level... I'll post a demo soon...

4)Soul of Sphere Platinum
Once made in Visual Basic and finished in 2003 with two games in one, this solo 2D game introduce the serie of Xylvan... The title has been reprogrammed in Blitz3D in 2D in 2006-2007 to work on every PCs...
