A dorm room(Posted 2009-07-13)






115 shadow casters so far in this dorm room and I am nowhere near done.

Shadows are thanks to FastExtension.

The shadows are powered by my dynamic working lights. All lights are dynamic and can be turned on and off/destroyed.

Main shadow casting in this room is from the two overhead lights and the player's torchlight.
