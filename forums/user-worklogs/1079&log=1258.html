Medival weapons pack released(Posted 2005-04-24)
Tridinat GFX Group is pleased to announce release of their medieval weapons pack. Consisting of 53 hi-quality models and hi-quality skins at 512x512 resolution this pack is aimed at indie game developers. With that in mind this weapon pack is priced at incredible 50$.

This pack includes following formats dts, 3ds, obj, max5, and max7 files.
