CTCC - 2D Racing(Posted 2002-07-02)
I'm developing a 2D Racing game (similar to the good old SuperCars on Amiga), only packed with features. Race up to 8 players (up to 4 on a single PC) and across networks.

Choose from a huge selection of cars and tracks. Cars are scaled down appropriate in proportion to their "real" counterparts with different handeling for FWD, RWD and 4WD cars on a large number of surfaces. Head through tunnels, over bridges, push your mate into the path of a train!

All cars are tunable with additional parts to boost performance! Use that engine power to draw alpha generated skidmarks and do some mad donuts!!

Check out the full list of features, large selection of screenshots and progress at
http://www.imphenzia.com/sideprojects/ctcc.
