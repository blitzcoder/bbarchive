Introduction(Posted 2007-01-01)
StealthRoom V2: One House Divided

What is it? Well, basically it's a Halo/Lord Of the Rings/Splinter Cell/Oblivion combo. But it's only online and has a twist (which will be mentioned later in this post).

Storyline:
A dictator had named himself king over the lands 50 years ago. The the government was split into factions (known as districts) under the King. The districts all sent assassins to kill the king, not knowing that the other districts did the same thing. Now you're just one of many assassins trying to get to the Palace first.

GamePlay

The main idea is that you progress to the palace with levels that you have to "win" example: you enter the
swamp level and have to fight the other assassins there. Last man standing progresses to next level. You use sword, projectiles, magic, traps, beasts and vehicles, and pure stealth to win. But there is a catch... You cannot see the other assasssins as they can't see you.

WIP:

go there if you want a more interesting rendition of the plot.
