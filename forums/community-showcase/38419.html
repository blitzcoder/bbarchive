Hi everybody,

A while ago I wrote this game for the Blitzcoder one-button competition and I realized now that I have not yet posted anything about it here. It's a game about fantasy characters fighting to reach the other side of a field, as fantasy characters often do! You have to press the space bar and hold it down until the preferred character shows up, then let go of the space bar. If a fighter reaches the other side you get extra "launch credits" so you can have more fighters active at the same time. If the "Wizard" character reaches the other side, that player has won the game!

You can download the game here. (1.3 Mb)
