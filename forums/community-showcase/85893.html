Hello! Over the years I have been kinda of lazy with my Soul Shooter Online and this time I have made my promise to continue my work on it. SSO is my first real Blitz3D project and just never had the time to work on it


Soul Shooter Online is a heavily modified version of the FPSNet Engine created by Jeremy Alessi and I would like to thank him for his wonderful framework. It allows me to add on to what the framework is lacking. Soul Shooter Online is based on a futuristic action type setting with a Quake 3 Arena feel to it. You are equipped with one gun called the "SoulGun" which enables you to perform multiple ablities


Features:
-SoulGun that allows you to cast Flamethrower, Spark, Deadly Gas(coming soon) and more to come soon.

-Crawl up walls to avoid your opponents blows.

- Warps to teleport to different areas of the map.. (Coming Soon)

- Ranking system (Coming Soon)

- Online Login System with Local Profile System (70% Complete)

- Xbox 360 Controller Support (Coming Soon)

-Using NetBlitz (UDP) to fully replace the original DirectPlay code used in FPSnet



and so much more to come.. I have a beta planned for August 1st. So if you interested, please join our community at www.sso.comxa.com

We will be transferring hosts next weekend, so I will be posting the new website link as well in this thread..
