Since there isn't a real demo showing all of Blitz3Ds awesome features, wouldn't it be great to start a CP that would extensively showcase the abilities of Blitz3D; graphical as well as other abilities.

This could be done as some sort of long cutscene or animation, or as a FPS or TPS kinda of presentation.

There's so much to show! And now all those features are separated in small demos that are really hard to even find as .bb let alone .exe. Normal mapping, mesh deformations, boned b3d files, cubic mapping and water effects, particle systems, maybe some shadows, scene complexity blitz can handle, 2d onscreen graphics mixed with 3d, sound pitch and volume control, collisions, tokamak, texture blending, you name it!

I'd like to see that personally, and i also believe it would expand the Blitz community by quite a lot since people will be hooked to see what they can make.

I say this cos just now i was saying to a friend what Blitz can do and after searching this whole site and forums, and blitzcoder, i came up with little more than a few odd demos. And noone can get an "impressive impression" after even all that. I'm sure most of you realised this long ago, but i never saw someone suggest a CP for it, which i think would be a GREAT idea.

Maybe if there are enough supporters we could start it up?
