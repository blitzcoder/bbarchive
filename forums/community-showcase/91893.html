Defend yourself!

Grab your tennis-ball gun and prepare for the onslaught! Defend yourself against an ever-tougher and faster army of clowns! Grab powerups to help you banish clown upon clown!



Download it here: 986kb zip
http://www.mediafire.com/?o1qm50z1ft49axm

Instructions:

WASD keys to move.
Aim with the mouse.
Your target is represented by a small white sphere which is attached to the mouse.

Powerups:
Red: 25 points
Green: +3 hp, but won't increase over 100
Blue: +10 hp, but won't increase over 125
Orange: 10% fire speed increase, and yes, it's limited to 200%.

Shoot the green shirted clowns with your tennis-ball backpack shooter until they decide to exit via the south door. Once they start heading to the south door they will no longer attack you, even if you get too close to them. Every clown drops a bonus box when they decide to leave.

Hitting an enemy with a ball makes the tennis ball bounce in a random direction. It CAN and will sometimes hit more clowns and continue to bounce.

You cannot run out of tennis balls.

All clowns are not created equal. They have varied speed and also varied tolerance to tennis balls. Shoot the fastest ones first!

For every 20 clowns you banish the enemy tier increases by 1. Each tier will add 1 hp to each enemy clown that spawns, and also increase their speed by a slight amount.

The goal is to get the highest score possible.(old school) You cannot win because there is no winning objective. Simply banish clowns as long as you can before they eventually knock you out.

Hint: Due to the angle of the camera, it is difficult to see the pitch of your tennis balls. Counter this effect by placing the aiming sphere as close to your intended target as possible.
