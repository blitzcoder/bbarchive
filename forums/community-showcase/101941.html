xlsior(Posted 2014) [#17]
@GaryV is that true? Where does it say that?


Pretty sure your game creator is perfectly OK -- what they do frown upon is someone just wrapping all the blitz commands to a bunch of pass-through placeholders in a DLL and releasing it as their own language, without really adding anything.

There's been one or two instances of that happening to blitz3D hence the addition of:

You may distribute executable applications built from
this source code under your own licensing terms. You may
not distribute static or dynamic library files based in
whole or in part on this source code.


to the blitz licenses.

Obviously, your game creator isn't just a language wrapper but an entirely different animal -- should be perfectly OK.
