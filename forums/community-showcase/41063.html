Hello everyone.

Brandon and I work together on projects, and I have recently completed my own. My name is Daniel, and I represent Nemesis.

Mega Fusion Rhythm and Beat is rhythm themed, and loosely based on the Dance Dance Revolution series. The game is designed to be played along with an XBOX/PS2 Dance Pad, but is functional with a joystick/game pad, and keyboard controls.

Demo version, and more information may be found at:
http://nemesis.notwhatyouthink.org

The game comes complete with 16 original songs, by 7 different bands.

Three different control options.

Three different game modes.

Many game options and settings.

A patch is under development for the bugs found so far, and should be released within a few days.

A massively multiplayer expansion set is also under development.

More songs, and levels will soon be available for download off the Nemesis website.
