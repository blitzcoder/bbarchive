Hi,

This is my new project, "Brium XG-72". It's a plateform/shooter game. We are in 20XX and you control Brium, a robot, into big levels. I can't tell more on the story and the gameplay, but it will play a little bit like "Turrican" on the Amiga. You'll shoot ennemies, gardians, collect power-up, life, jump on moving plateforms/invisible plateforms, etc, all that with a multi-directional ( ultra smoth ) scrolling. "DR-800", a female robot, will contact you by radio during the game to prevent you from danger. She's got a scaner and she's in orbit in the mother-ship. There will be digitalised voices in french, I don't know for english. I hope the game will be finished in 6 months !

it's a very special toon style. Your graphics remind me partially of it. The "samurai jack" toon series do live from the unique graphics and from the big effort for the soundtrack. you'll find pictures in the web, it's pretty popular. I guess it's made in america, but it's completely diffrent from the stuff I know from disney, wb or mgm etc. A samurai warrior is thrown into the future and has to find his way back to the past. Of course this isn't so easly, so there's a lot of series. They play it on the "cartoon network" channel here.

But don't let yourself influence too much by this stuff. I just wanted to say: the way you do the graphics may be very successful these days. So keep it up.
