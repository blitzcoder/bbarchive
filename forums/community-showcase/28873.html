I'm not sure if this forum is for Blitz only creations, but before I became acquainted with my new all time favourite programming language, I used to program in Macromedia Director Lingo.
Santa's Mix-Up is one of the only fully playable games that I completed, and released last Christmas.
Basically Santa has mixed up all the presents he is to deliver to each house and it's up to you to help sort out the correct order of the presents with regard to their size, shape and ribbon colour.
It's a fun game, although quite brain-taxing, with cartoon style animation and humour.
