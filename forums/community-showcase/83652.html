Xilvan Design are not just making games for money and offers now great games for free !

Having not a 2D Candy World in our gaming library, we thought that it would be nice to release a entirely 2D Candy World game for the public.

In 2008 the project were unsuccessfully started and a few graphics were made such as artworks and sprites


Wednesday, March 11 2009
The developpement of the game Candy World Adventure : The Fate of Lillians started from scratch with few graphics and great ambition...


3 days later, the version 0.2 of the game is out and offers altmost all the basic features of the game. The overall graphics and physics engine is done.

The basic actions such like walking, running and jumping are available.

In the game, Toopy got his rubber ball stolen by Doctor Picpic who is back from his exodus... He has stolen many Lillians in the whole world and try to steal Candy's friends.

Dont worry, Toopy is free in the game at the beguining and will help you in your progress...

In the demo, you must find a button to go further the way... By exploring the game enough, you'll find it. By pressing the R(Ressort) button, you will activate the springs ahead and be able to explore more of the first level I've done yet...

Controls:
[Arrows] Move
[Up] Enter Portal or Door
[Space] Jump/SWim
[Shift] Run
[E] Walk very slow

[H] To get a butterfly on your back (To fly)

[B] Set Toopy's Ball
[N] Throw Toopy's Ball

[Enter] Talk/Throw Freesbies or Boomrang
[M] Drop Boomrang

[F1] Menu Save & Load
[F2] Save configuration
[F3] Toogle Music

3 modes :
No Sound / No Music
Sound / No Music
Sound / Music

[F12] Take screenshot

[F] Switch Language (French/English)
