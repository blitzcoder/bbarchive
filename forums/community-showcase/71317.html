That looks really good! A worthy spin (hehe, nice pun) on match-3 that attracts my attention by being clearly different from the pack.
Visually it is good, but I particularly like the audio. The clicking noises fit it really well.

I like the mini game you showed there. It gets the player to start using his head, rather than a lot of mini games which get the player to enter button mashing mode. (Which is not what you want in a puzzle game).

One "thing":
When a single wheel thing (the objects in the middle, with the spokes, that hold the tiles) runs out of tiles, it leaves the scene. I think the way they do this is odd, and not really fitting with the feel of the rest of the game. They seem to float about magically, with no reason for this to happen or sign of magic occurring, and being quite distracting... Perhaps they could disintegrate, fall apart into chunks, or something like that?

Other than that little detail, it looks really good and well polished. I'm looking forward to playing with it :)
