Its some time in the future, the world has colonised all the planets in the solar system, and are about to make a voyage to new star systems.

Just before all this happens the earth is hit by a rouge asteroid, and an investigation is started to find out why it happened, during this time the earth force detects another incoming asteroid, big enough to destroy all life on the planet..

The military set about creating a space craft with enough fire power to destroy the asteroid, this is where your mission picks up the story..

Bottom line is that you have to protect your planet from the asteroids that are on a collision course. trouble is that they are not on this course by chance, and you soon find yourself knee deep in alien scum.!!!!

Enough of the short story on to the details.

Arrow Keys to control Ship.
For = Thrust

Space = fire Main guns
M = fire missile.

I,O,P = The main weps i is thunder and requires you to be close to a roid.
