- The story :
You take contol of Roy and his faithful dragon Dubby.
You go to the three close villages which decided to steal the rice reserves of your village this night.

You can launch projectils with your dragon by charging the energy-bar :

1/yellow : long shot,
2/pink : medium shot,
3/red : short shot,
4/flashing bar (maximum charging) : short shot, but which has the possibility of rebounding towards the back when touching the ground.

What's new :
- new code (BlitzPlus),
- new pause,
- news ending,
- 3 levels of dificulty,
- choice of 3 soundtracks style (Pulse renovator, The Twisted MC et Trente trois),
- new energy-bar,
- improved 32bits graphics,
- better gameplay.

Have a nice game !
