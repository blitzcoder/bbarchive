Here is a WIP demo of a bumper car game that i have coded some weeks ago and that i will improve when the inspiration comes back.



http://rd-stuff.fr/Vehicle-controls-+-collisions-with-3-CS-2012.04.01-11h31m.zip

Controls :
Z > Accelerate forward
S > Accelerate backward
Q > Turn Left
D > Turn Right
Tab > fpv ou tpv
W > Choose another car
Mouse> Rotate the view

There are still some incoherences with the collisions but this is a good beginning.

If you find a bug, please post a short description of the problem.
