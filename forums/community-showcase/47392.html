Caff(Posted 2005) [#12]
I tried this demo about 2 weeks ago, I searched for your site for the download (maybe from this gallery on this site?).

I like the graphics - the models and animations are very good. The tokakamak physics work well (but you should use more of it) - and the ambience of the game is very nice. I can see this working well as a game for kids (ie. 7 to 11).

However - and I won't hold back, because I feel that this may help you make the game better -

1. The gameplay is awful. It's really simplistic. In the current day of gaming, you cannot expect gamers of any age to simply be satisfied with simply aiming at the enemy and shooting and moving. It's far too basic.

2. The voice-over is a really good effort - as an englishman, I think it's a very good attempt compared to anything I could try. But some of the words do sound heavily accented, and the spelling is bad in some places and needs checking as others have mentioned.

I think if you focus on the gameplay and try to provide some variation, you can have something that can sell well. At present, it is rooted in the 1980's in terms of gameplay. Create a good story, give it some love, and it will sell.
