The football manager game Anpfiff by Blitzcase Entertainment offers you in version 2011 the most important duties you have to take care of when being a football manager. There has been an optimization of the whole game - for more realistic scoring in games and more realistic change of transfer value. You can play the game in english and german.
The game in version 2011 gives you some new editing features like inserting original team logos and player names and player photos. You can even copy the music you want to hear into the game folder.
The game consists of Newscenter, Formation, Team, Stadium, Exchange, Statistics and the simulated matches. Start your career as a manager of a soccer team now!
