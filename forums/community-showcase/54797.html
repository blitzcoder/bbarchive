I've released my game "Blood & Plunder - Goblin Head Go Squish Squish" which is inspired by the classic Gauntlet arcade game.

25 Monster infested halls and caverns for you to fight through before facing the evil witch in her underground lair.


Download and screenshots available here:

http://home.swiftdsl.com.au/~gezeder/lloyd/Blood&Plunder.html

Music by Ajari Wilson, Graphics / Programming by me (Matt Lloyd), Vocals - Rynne Cowham and Howard Kistler.

[bold]Game Pad / Joystick Required[/bold]

(However keyboard support is possible by entering the word "keyboard" in the text field on the setup page).
