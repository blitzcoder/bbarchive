Stevie G(Posted 2009) [#3]
Some feedback after a quick play:

* Love the vector graphics and ship designs etc..

* Control was not bad. I came second in my second race on track 2 with a time of 1:50 on Easy. I needed to have the camera at it's highest though.

* The default key of left shift for the firing can invoke the old 'sticky keys' issue. I know you can re-define them so this is a non-issue.

* You need to store the cursor position before changing a key and reset it afterwards.

* Not sure if you support joypads but if not, may be worth considering.

* I think the track needs to stand out more from the background so that it's easier to see where you're going. It's too distracting for me to look at the map.

* I think you should bounce off the barriers better ( i.e. just reflect the velocity with the collision normals and reduce the resulting vector ). It's too harsh at the moment and can be quite frustrating when you get pushed back the way you came.

* Like the pit - same a wipeout if I remember.

* I had a quick play of the editor but wasn't sure what I was doing. Best to add some instructions on there as I just made a mess ;) You should be able to get the AI to automatically create the racing line, given the tiles. I find having to place these a pain in the arse and trial and error.

* Nice retro sounds & robo voices - can I ask how you made them?

All in all, pretty good.

Stevie
