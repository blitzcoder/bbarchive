Earth just got out of war (nuclear war that is), but the radiations are killing it. Out of nowhere came the unpredictable; an alien that is here to save the world, the Nuclear Nibble! Now, you are playing the nibble that is absorbing all the radiations and you must go through several levels all around the world. Besides the radiations, there are several special powerups that come into play: jumps, lives, points, level warps, deaths, wall busts, wall spawns, sharp turns, length and shrink. The goal of the game: collect 'em all, win 'em all and save the Earth.
Nuclear Nibble 3D is the classic (Nibble, Snake, Worm, etc) reborn in today's technology: 3D graphics. Nuclear Nibble 3D is the sequel to an old game I made a couple years back (1996) in QBASIC. Now it is remade in 3D, using Blitz3D.

It makes a year that NN3D is out and I want to update it! One thing that I would like is to add 10 new levels! This is where you guys come into play!
