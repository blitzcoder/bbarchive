Well, I've been working pretty hard on my new game, and thought I'd send it out for a little testing(for those who are interested ;-) ). It is playable from a single player deathmatch vs. the Bots. I don't have many options that you can adjust yet, but you can definitely have a little fun with it.

<end edit>


Click Here to Download W.O.R. (Wings of Rage: Tournament)

I don't know what kind of system requirements it needs (on the low end). I'm sure it needs at least a 32 MB video card, and probably somewhere in the neighborhood of a 600 - 800 MHz processor minimum, but that's just a guess.

To play, simply go to SinglePlayer Game, and click start in the lower righthand corner. Note that the "Genetic Attributes" panels are not functioning yet (meaning you cant control what abilities your ostrich will have). Everything is randomized for each player (including enemy AI, which produces a great range of behavior patterns). Your top speeds both on ground and in air, your bank rate in the air, how much you can bank, and probably most importantly, your flight endurance will be randomized. Some ostriches will not have a very high flight endurance, and may not be able to fly at all. This is determined each time the game is played (though in the future you'll be able to save the players for future use).

Your standard weapon is an Autocannon with infinite rounds.
There are 4 types of powerups you can get.
Health - Indicated by a Red Cross on the Crate
Armor - Indicated by a Grey Diamond on the Crate (2 of these will fill your armor)
Invulnerability - A Blue Diamond with a yellow center. Provides 30 seconds of Invulnerability
ShotGun - Marked by a White splatter pattern on the crate. Provides 60 rounds of a shotgun. It fires slower and has less range, but really packs a punch when it hits.


Controls:
---------------------------------------------
Movement = A, S, W, D
SpaceBar = Launches you into flight on level or downward sloping ground
otherwise it bursts you to fullspeed on upslopes
LeftMouseButton = Fire Weapon

F10 takes screenshots

F1 turns on/off the AI info mode (framerate will drop using this, but it indicates your "score" a bit compared to everyone else)

Frames per second is displayed in the upper lefthand corner.

Hope you enjoy, and I appreciate hearing your comments.
