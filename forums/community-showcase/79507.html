The goal of the game is to capture all the bases on a map.
For example you have 6 bases, 1 of them is yours, another belongs to another team. You spawn at your base and then you drive to another one to capture it. Now of course you have to defend your bases. When you captured a base you are able to spawn there also.
Currently working on a nightmode with nightsights and so on...
And I'm planing to do strategic weapons, like artillery, bombing etc.
