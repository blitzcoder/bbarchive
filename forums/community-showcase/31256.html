The MarForPac Rave was originally actually just an 'inside' joke. When I was in the Marine Corps (Marine Forces Pacific) in Hawaii they told us we couldn't go to 'raves' and if a party turned into a 'rave' we had to go. So, how were we to know if a party turned into a rave? Here it is. 'Warning, Warning all MarForPac personell leave the building, Rave starts in 5,4,3,2,1.....this is a private rave'
I did all the vocal effects and voices for this, and also the siren you hear at the beginning of this song is actually a sound I made with my mouth then tweaked out to sound sireny.
http://sites.gwala.net/tormented/ihr/mfprave.wma

Yeah, I thought getting Hawaii was a spot of luck but, it turned out only 3 people in our class asked to go to Hawaii so they actually ended up sending 7 of us. I was a field radio operator in artillery, I also did a stint my last year as a staff instructor at Jungle Warfare Training Center (like the SERES course on GI JANE)

For a lot of my stuff I use Fruity Loops for making loops/sequences and sound effects.

I use Magix Music Maker to arrange my loops and stuff and add some after effects like step echoes. Magix is a bad music maker but, a good arranger program drag/drop/slice/dice/copy/etc. music/video/images into one file.

I use Razor Lame for exporting/converting files to mp3.

I use my Turntables 2 sa2200 Gemini's for scratching/mixing/and remixing stuff from time to time.
I'm also pondering getting 'Final Scratch' or something like that for mixing/scratching my own original files.

I used to use Voyetra Wave Editor back in the day as well.
