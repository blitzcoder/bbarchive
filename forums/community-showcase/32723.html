Berbank(Posted 2004) [#23]
Thank you for your praise!

Changing the jump mechanics because of a close overhang might not be a good solution, if I explain why I think it's bad, you can then discuss it more if you disagree.

Changing the jump height because something is close and above the flea assumes you will be jumpin straight up. Unless we continuously predict the jump path and adjust height accordingly, the flea might do something unexpected, and therefore annoying.

Any automatic variation of jump height makes the jump trajectory unknown. At least at the moment, you know you will bump your head.

Also, the fixed height is very important for level design, which is one significant distinction between its mechanics and Bugaboo's.

So those are my reasons for leaving it as it is. However, if you do end up getting FleaFall Champion, you can make your own levels and edit the ones you have so that there are less little passages if you like.

Yes, changing the background a little more is something I'll look into after FleaFall Champion is out. In levels that you don't have in Apprentice, we've done other effects such as blurring for background plates. Generally, I prefer the black edge foreground and coloured edge background approach that is in the Chase level (the one with the cat and birds et.c), but each to thier own.

Thanks again, and I'm sure the weather can spare you for a day or two, besides the fleas can get lonely when they aren't exercised regularly...(is that a clue about a possible future feature?)
