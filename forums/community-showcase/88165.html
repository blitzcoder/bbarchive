RifRaf(Posted 2010) [#31]
I have a pretty big demo update ready .
This update adds classes. Much like TF2, but the difference with Tiny Tanks is that Classes dont limit your weapon selection. They modify weapon and other attributes. The classes in game so far are.

[Soldier] average class with no additonal pros or cons

[Scout] - Pros : Very fast, can always see enemies on his radar.
Cons : Takes double damage, and deals half damage

[Bomber] - Pros : Can take out many enemies at once with a single blow
Cons : Primary explosion is a self detonation also destroying self
Cons : Takes tripple damage

[Marksman] - Pros : improved sniper cannon does 3x damage when zoomed
Cons : Sniper cannon has 10 tick reload, other weapons deal half damage

[Pyro] - Pros : Mortar Cannon and Rockets leave napalm flames wich cause damage to enemy tanks that travel into them. Rockets leave more deadly flames in a larger area.
Cons : The pyro moves 85% normal speed, and has a longer reload on all other weapons.


[Juggernaut]- Pros : Takes 4x less damage, Improved mortar cannon hits for 2x damage and also sends enemy flying into the air when they are hit with it.
Cons : Very Very Slow movement, double reload time on all weapons


So if you havent tried TT out in awhile , please give it another look. These class variations are in game now and need testing.
