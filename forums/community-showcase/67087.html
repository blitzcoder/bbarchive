About
Here is the second alpha of my game Oil's Well Redrilled, based on the Thomas J. Mitchell / Sierra On-line original version. The object is to guide your drill around each of the levels to collect all of the oil pellets within the twisty tunnels. As you move along you leave a pipeline behind which you cannot cross, so you must retract the drill to take a different route. Also, enemies will appear from the side of the screen - if they make contact with the drill's pipe, you lose a life.

Screenshots



I'm still using ripped graphics for now - typical programmer art, eh :) - but before any final release, the graphics will be redone (I'm already working with someone to improve this). This version is just to see how well the game runs on a variety of computers (and fixes a few bugs from the first alpha).
