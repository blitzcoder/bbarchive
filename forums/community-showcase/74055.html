After 2.5 years of full-time development, Eschalon: Book I is finally released!

Read more about the game and download the demo here.







For those of you who want the beef on the tech side of it: it is made in 100% BlitzMax code, no 3rd party add-ons. It can be compiled with a vanilla BlitzMax installation.

The same code runs without modification on IntelMac and Linux as well (those versions are entering beta next week) and I just got the MacPPC port done (just a few LittleEndianStream functions needed to get it working).

It's a huge game with lots of areas to plunder and creatures to slay! Enjoy! :)
