No neural networks. This was more of a little simulation in genetics than anything else. The bots are all under one blitz type, and the type has 46 different fields. Most of those fields are loosly genetic based fields such as metabolism, speed, color, intelligence, etc. Those are all passed on to each's children, with the ability for slight differences. There is always the chance too (I think it was like .2% or .1%, I don't remember) that a new offspring will be a whole new mutated "strain" of bot, in which case the "genetics" would be much more widely mutated, and start a whole new color of creatures.
