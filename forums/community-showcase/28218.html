Elf Demo released

Community Forums/Showcase/Elf Demo released

Caligula(Posted 2003) [#1]
This demo represents one level of an upcoming 3rd/1st person action game about Elves and Orcs. Featuring medieval combat with sword, bow, and destructible armor. Uses several products by the Blitz community such as, FONText, Swift Shadow System, Treemagik, and Xlnt GUI. (The instruction manual is accessible from the graphics setup menu).

You can download it here: http://www.3rdheavenstudios.com/downloads/EOEv1.0.exe
(The demo is about 17MB)

Any feedback and suggestions are greatly appreciated!
