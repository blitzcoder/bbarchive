HappyCat(Posted 2007) [#8]
Hi, cheers for the feedback everyone!

Anyway, Gun wing is my baby so I guess I should respond :-)

Enemy Graphics: When I first started the entire game had a very abstract look (enemies and backgrounds) so it looked more coherent. As I expanded the game with more backgrounds I started to include some more "realistic" backgrounds to show a progression (from earth, into space and towards the aliens's abstract planet). Unfortunately that's kinda lost by putting the first two backgrounds in the demo. Perhaps we should choose different backgrounds for the demo so it doesn't look so odd.

HUD: I like it simple like that. Doesn't clutter or get in the way.

Mouse Movement: Mouse movement is the same speed as keyboard and joystick movement, which was the idea.

Difficulty: They do have increased firepower, but yes the main factor is their increased hit points. No ones else has commented on this, but will keep it in mind.

Enemies from behind: Yes that's intentional - there's not many of them, but I have to keep you on your toes :-)

Mouse out of window: Yes, I'm not 100% sure of that myself. Until very recently the mouse was confined to the window when you were controlling the ship. I do find myself getting confused at times with the new method - will see if anyone else has any thoughts.

Enemy shots: You can't shoot them, but the shield and smartbomb will get rid of them.

Anyway, thanks for the feedback :-)
