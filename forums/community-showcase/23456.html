You know how it is... You've got a bunch of reference images and they don't line up, they're the wrong size, they aren't quite straight and it's a pain in the arse sorting it... well... here's the tool to fix your problems!

Adjust offset
Adjust scale
Adjust Rotation
Define reference lines
Overlay milkshape grid to see the real size
Hit a button and it saves a new bmp for you to use.
