Some of you probably remember that spaceshooter I was working on awhile ago,
going under the quaint name of Project Alpha since neither I, nor my graphics designer
Mr. Ash could think of a name for it.

Well not only have we finally decided upon a name for it, but we've made some
rather massive changes to boot(And a great many more are on the way!)



So here is the first Shattered Earth Demo, which includes 1 level, complete with
boss, and a few rather spiffy powerups.



Use Arrow Keys to move, Spacebar to shoot all of your guns at once, A,W,D to
shoot your guns individually, and Enter to switch between Primary and Secondary
guns.
