NOTE: THIS IS A TECH DEMO AND NOT AN EYE-CANDY DEMO; LOOKS ARE IRRELEVANT! :)

I haven't posted any demos for ages, so I thought I'd share what I'm working on with you: It's a pseudo Geo-Mod system (not CSG or whatever the acronym is - my own smoke & mirrors method).

The idea for the gameplay is hybrid RTS/FPS, in which the player will be able to design new buildings on the fly during play (using a laptop or something), watch them built in front of them by their workers, go in and out of them, blow chunks out of them with rockets and more. Hopefully this feature will be integral to gameplay rather than fluff!

As I've said, the graphics are very ugly as it's purely a tech demo. And even that's in an early state and needs optimising... god, it's so complicated it makes my head spin! :)
