Ryan Burnside(Posted 2007) [#1]
After playing countless hours of the Ace Combat games, I had a great idea. I loved the way your flight was documented in those missions and wanted to take some of that simplicity and make a game out of it. I also liked the idea of making an arena shmup. Also after playing some racing games, I found that I had begun to regard the computer controlled players as opponents and not just computer controlled AI. These opponents fought not only you but also each other. Really I want to encorperate the theme of the computer fighting itself and you into a game.I wanted to let the player steal points from computer players and conversly, let the computer steal from the player. This is done in the way to Sonic looses his rings when hit. When hit opponents will loose "glyphs". These glyphs will be free for any player to pick up once dropped or spawned.

Driving themes of this project:
1. Individuality for the player and opponents
2. Stealing and Backstabbing during gameplay
3. Fast pace with skill and reflex involved.

Now, you fight up to 20 opponents at once and they are all intelligent and have matched skills. You need to collect the green glyphs and shoot down as many enemy craft as possible. When shot, a player looses all glyphs so don't hesitate to kill an enemy with a high glyph count.

Enemy Kill-100pts
Glyph pickup-15 pts


Z or space fires, arrow left/right change cruzing angle and up/down change speed for tight or loose turns.

If you die press R to restart. This needs a title screen and player stats screen but the gameplay is mostly intact. If you have time in your schedult please try it and tell me what you think good and bad.

If you have any ideas for scoring or level progression don't make yourself a stranger!
