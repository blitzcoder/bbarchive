Kuri(Posted 2003) [#7]
jhocking: yes it was in its previous form. But we decided that because we were so pleased with the story etc. and it was a shame to abandon it completely that we would make a lower poly simpler version, which is what we are doing. The original models were all 1500+ polys and needed realistic anims as they were more realistic characters so took about a month each to produce. These characters are sub 800 polys and can have more cartoony anims and only take a few days to produce.

There are only two of us working on the project, so it needed to be scaled down or abandoned. In the end, as I say, we went for scaling down :)
