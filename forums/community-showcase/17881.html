Its been a while designing this game as it is really huge but I can confirm that work is now underway.

Expected Release date: (Late 2003) or when it's done. ;-)

Overall features (for the initial version):-

1) As many human players as there are teams.
2) All English leagues. + Conference.
3) Promotion, Relegation.
4) All the cups (FA, European etc). + League Cup, Conference cup, div 2 & 3 cup.
5) Manager rating.
6) Talk to the press.
7) Sponsors. With logos, length of sponsorship, fee paid.
8) Ground improvements. Capacity, floodlights, seating and standing seats, big screen tv.
9) Transfer market. With search.
10) Player skills and stats.
11) Training.
12) Realtime match engine (with possible 2d/3d highlights ) including the ability to watch other teams matches as well.
13) Injuries and suspensions.
14) Job vacancies.
15) Team history page with best win, teams highest scorer etc.
16) Finance page, income/expenditure.
17) Manager history page including previous clubs, cups won, and leagues won.
18) Manager of the month, player of the month.
19) International teams with World cup and Euro cup.
20) TV revenue for some games.
21) Board comments.
22) Players’ performance for all games.
23) Show cup draws as they happen.
24) Player injuries
25) Fine players.
26) Player editor.

Is there anything else you would like to see added?
