fredborg(Posted 2003) [#2]
Hi,

You should use the gile[s] forum for all your questions :)

But here is the answer:

gile[s] does not support Blitz sprites, but if you make the leaves/foliage as flat polygon meshes (or any other kind of mesh) there is no problem with getting them to cast shadows!

The reason I don't support sprites, is that there is no standard way of loading them into Blitz, and (depending on your spriteviewmode settings) they don't have a fixed orientation which will make shadows look weird :)

There is a screenshot of some trees made by wmaass in the screenshots board on the gile[s] forum.
