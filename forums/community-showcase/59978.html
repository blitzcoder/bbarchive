Here's a link to my new game Asteroid Buddies:

asteroid_buddies.zip (1.9 Mb)



Controls: left/right for rotating, up/down for going forward/backward, and space or right control for shooting.

Be wary of the asteroid buddies, who will attack you when you shoot their beloved big asteroids. They cannot hurt you directly (and neither can you hurt them) but they will fly into you in an annoying manner.

I wrote this for a mini challenge on www.retroremakes.com , which is a warming up to the upcoming 2006 competition.

Source code is included! (Blitz 3D, but only using 2D commands)

The feature is that when you blindly destroy all big asteroids, the creatures will annoy you while you are trying to clear away all the small rocks. So the trick is to be a little bit careful, and not destroy all the big rocks right away. Maybe it doesn't work for everybody, the difficulty level could be too high or too low. It could be that because I made the game, I got better at it and set the speed too high. This means that other people can have fun playing it after practicing for only a few days. :)
