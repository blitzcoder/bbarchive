NameGen is my latest Blitz release. It is a random name generator that is geared toward pen & paper RPG players and writers. It generates thousands of unique names for dwarves, elves, magic items, spaceships, taverns, towns, and more. It can speak the names to you as well so that you can have an idea of what they sound like.

It's for Mac OS X only and is available in the Mac App Store.
