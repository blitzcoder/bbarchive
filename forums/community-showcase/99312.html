Possibilities:

Loading of the animated models and textures, multitexturing, loading of sounds and music, insert of images from a clipboard, the miscalculation of cards of shadows, creation and generation of the matrixes, the built-in effects of the chamber, own editor and the generator of textures, preservation of the worlds in an initial code. Export to other formats is supported.
