Controls:
WASD - move protagonist
Mouse - move target
Left mouse button - fire
Right mouse button - Force
Mouse wheel - zoom
Space - teleport

The goal is to collect 10 energy sources (they look like stars with black edges and you must break crates to get 'em)

Some advices:
* Try to avoid the kolobok crowds
* If you are taken in cricle - use Force and and immediately get out of the entourage
* If you have invulnerability, you can safely teleport anywhere away from the crowd
* Try to take speed and firepower at the same time, so you'll beat more enemies
* Before taking bombs, wait until koloboks cover it around
* Do not hurry to take firepower if koloboks are far, wait until they come closer
* When using firepower do not go deep in the diffuse mass of kolobkov - when the powerup expires and they move to you again you can be trapped
* Use zoom to determine where more boxes are situated, and then try to break them

Kolobok is a hero of russian short child fairy-tale. It's spherical like head without body, so our spherical personages can have the same name.
