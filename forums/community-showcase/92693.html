A new version of Beat Ball 2 (v1.2.0) is available:
http://www.beatball2.com/files/BeatBall2.zip

Some of the new changes to the new version:
* The internet high scores require this version
* Internet high scores are no longer limited to 100 entries
* Two new in-game songs added
* Internet high scores are submitted even if you don't qualify to local highscores
* Alt-tab from windowed mode does not lock mouse
* Alt-tab from windowed mode automatically pauses game
* Game performs a health check and can provide a warning if internet high scores cannot be reached

Feel free to drop by the brand new web site for the game:
http://www.beatball2.com

jfk EO-11110(Posted 2010) [#8]
Imphenzia - if you really want to sell it, go out and do some guerilla web marketing. Ok, it isn't really something people like to see. I mean, Admins and so on. Usually Ads cost money, and when you drop in somewhere and tell everybody about a product - you will be kicked often. It's a matter of finding a good balance between community contribution and Advertisement. For example you may put Videos on youtube to show people some special moves and tricks in your game, that are not explained in the official docs or so. But youtube alone wont be enough. You really should offer your game on as many shareware servers as possible. There is a certain percentage of people who actually buy it after the download. It's up to you to spread it. It would also be nice to get some kind of sensational mentioning in news media, or get a top rank on youtube,eg. by your dog or cat, playing trough level one, or something like that.

You may also offer paper magazines with cover disks to publish your trial version for free. Just tell all of them about the possibility. Usually they only publish things when a company pays for ads in the magazine, but sometimes they have to fill the disk.

And never forget that publishing it on blitzbasic isn't really marketing, that's more of a elite ;) community thing. So think big, think masses. The Web is, unlike TV, Radio and Papers not monodirectional, but bidirectional, that's a chance.
