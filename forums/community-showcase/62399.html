This is the test of the game I am currently working on, 360 Space Mayhem. The point of the game is to destroy all enemys, and enemy forts. In order to destroy all the enemy forts, you must first track down the forts using their bullets. Enemy forts spawn enemys and just sit and spin, shooting out random bullets. The enemys are programed to lock on to you and chase you intill they are a set distance from you, then sit and shoot. Once you kill a certain number of enemys and get $300 in money, you can buy your own forts. Your forts just sit in one place and spin and shoot.
Note: Some enemys are programmed to lock onto your forts, not you.
