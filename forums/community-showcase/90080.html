Well, this is another "space" shooter in 3d . It is a pale copy of similar games like "Blazing angels" or "jetFigther 2015" , "Rogue Squadron".
coded in Blitz3D + FastExt libraries.
It is my second attempt to mess with blitz3D.
Mainly developed for my nephew's next birthday as a surprise.

You take control of different ships from the Valkyrie squadron (light fighter , heavy fighter, bomber , etc ...)


it features :
Arcade, and campaign missions
full mission editor
complete universe
30 + terrains and space quadrants (space station , asteroids fields, planets , etc )
Mouse , keyboard, and joystick controls
missiles and range of weapons


project is now over, and stable release 1.0 is available


It is released for free, (but I keep all the intellectual property and rights as it is not open source)
