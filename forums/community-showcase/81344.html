Taron(Posted 2008) [#9]
VERY nice start! The reasons for why it seems "ephemeral" (in essence not like an steady ocean) is that you have a visible stream direction along the beach. Instead you should consider a motion toward the beach, which - as a compromise- may well be as continuous as you have it there, but the idea of introducing slow but larger waves could help a lot. The other thing is a less frequent push of white water and contract it as the water pushes into the beach. The last, but probably coolest trick, would be to turn the sand darker in a slightly offset timing to the white water, much like as if the water made it wet, you know...

But altogether: Kudos! Looks great!
