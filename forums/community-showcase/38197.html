I took a break from my main project (Battle Pit 2) for the last week or so to see if I could create a good 3D version of an old 2D game I made called Titan:The infiltrator. Its a top down shooter in which you have to clear the room of all the alien baddies before you can go onto the next room. It's only a test room and has only the lowest type of baddie and one weapon type(so it may seem easy).

The controls are -
"." pivot and turn while shooting
"/" Strafe
"shift" Shoot
