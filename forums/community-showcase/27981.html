I'd say don't use black as a background colour. The standard colour for model renders is a middle grey (128,128,128) Then people can actually see the outline of the model. The picture is dark as it is so the background doesn't help.

I think the model looks a lot better than your first version. The only thing I really don't like it the mouth. It just look stupid, how on earth is he meant to close it? His teeth would never meet so there is no way he can bite or chew things. I also think the feet look a bit like clowns shoes but a lot of that has been sorted out with the new texture.

Of course a lot of this is my opinion. I'd say that you should be your own judge. If you are happy with it then go for it. However i find that when I work on 3d stuff I become more objective if I take a few days off a model and then look at it with fresh eyes.
