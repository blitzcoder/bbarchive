Update!

I know that you have all been waiting for some screenshots,
so here they are!
Along with a brief description of the gameplay.

Battery Man is played from an overhead perspective, and Batterys,
are a very important part of the game play.
Since Battery Man runs on Batterys, He is constantly in need of more.

As you play the game, his Battery Power continually drains, and as it
drains, your view of the screen gets smaller. So when your at full Battery
Power you can see the entire screen, but when your Battery Power is
low, you can barely see any of it.
Your Battery Power is also your health meter.
As you get damaged by enemys, your power will drain.
If your Battery Power ever reaches 0, and an enemy touches you, you
will lose a life.

The're lots of Batterys scatterd throughout the game to replenish your
supply. Along with other pickups such as extra lives, and shields.

Below are 6 Screen Shots, and a close up image of Battery Man.
