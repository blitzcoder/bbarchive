Najdorf - your games are cool.

I released a graphics library called Mildred on the Amiga, written in BlitzBasic 2, to verson 1.52. It worked with CPU-processed bitmaps/sprites in `fast ram` (non DMA non graphics memory), in `chunky` pixels format, 256 colors, using a chunky-to-planar technique for AGA display, or direct GFX card output. I believe it was used in Paul Burkey's `Foundation` game in part, but was mainly going to be used in Dafel:Bloodline from Pagan Games. That game, originally written in BlitzBasic 2 still isn't finished afaik but they're working on it in C or something. Mildred isn't exactly a game but it's a released portion of potential games ;-)
