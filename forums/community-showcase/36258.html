Hello, just learning 3d and have made a start on MargZarch based on the Acorn game Zarch.

There's not much to do at the moment but you can fly around and shoot. There are some 'seeder' craft aswell.

I'm really after people reporting back on the time in the top left corner of the screen and their system specs.

Download Here

It is 614Kb.
Mouse controls the craft. LMB - thrust, RMB - fire
TAB - toggles the HUD (a bit faster with the HUD off)
A - Zoom in
Z - Zoom out
Keypad 8 & 2 adjust the sea depth
Escape twice to exit
