Ninja Massacre is a game I developed in the Blitz3d engine for about a few months now. The game revolves around you, an unnamed ninja, fighting against an endless onslaught of constantly respawning enemies on a small platform. The goal of the game is to knock as many of these baddies off of the platforms using punches and kicks before you get knocked off yourself, to which the game would be over. Please keep in mind that this is my first finished game (I completed a prototype called "Bloody Cowboy Massacre" about 6 months ago.) and I am not a master at the Blitz3d engine.
