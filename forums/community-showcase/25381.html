Yoda Soccer is a new soccer game which intends to give a continuation to the funny and easy playable series of Sensible World of Soccer (aka SWOS).
It's written in Blitzbasic 2D.
The source code is available under the GNU general public license.

Download the latest version (beta 0.66) from the official site (we moved recently from geocities):

http://yodasoccer.sourceforge.net

Have fun!
