Heroes' Fate is a party based game, max of 5 players per party. Other then the main towns/taverns the entire game is instanced for you and your party.

Many skills and spells await for you to learn! This is an action combat RPG, so dont be afraid to swing away as fast as you can, but beware, you will get tired, preventing you from inflicting damage and your ability to run!

Good and Evil are in play, the things you choose to do will effect this!

Soon is in the next month or 2...... as for an mmo kit?

Not really, sure you can have lots of players on, but the code restricts players to a max of 5 in combat areas, an instance is created. This is to restrict the load on the client.

Where there is no combat, towns for example, then there is no cap on players, these areas are where players can gather to basicly lobby around and find other players to go on the quests with them.

Also, there is a cap of 200 players per server. I may or maynot change that in the future, as i have yet to have 200 players on to see the results. Most i have had on at 1 time was around 30, and everyone had 0 issues.

200 was just a guess on where to cap the server.... could be 100... could be 350... idk until i get there.
