Rob(Posted 2003) [#4]
lol... yeah, I feel that all scenes need an ambient light for mood - this is a sort of overall wash,

a defined light for atmosphere, this is sunlight, torchlight, and a complimentary light. The complimentary light should be quite different to the above lights, experiment, but is for "rounding out" the shadows.

For example, boris vallejo will use green on skintones to accentuate flesh, don't be afraid of using a very soft weird light in dark places to give mood.
