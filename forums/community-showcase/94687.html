A spaceshooter for Windows and Mac OS X. Download the demo now or buy the full version.
Save the solar system and fight the Geoms!



Geolaria is a solar system with the sun Geolar in its center. Decades ago, the five planets of this solar system have been attacked and conquered by the aggressive nation of the Geoms. Numerous subspecies – each of them has the same objective: Kill you. But a ship and a strong laser cannon are helping you to wipe out all Geoms once for all.

Dive into the world of Geolaria and experience a quick gameplay with challenging laser battles between you and the endless masses of Geoms. Luminous colorful explosions and roasted parts of your enemies will mark your way of destruction and accentuate your triumph through the solar system.

The constantly increasing difficulty forces to improve your weapons with upgrades. Multiple piercing, powerful laser shots will begin the carnage. But the opponents keep up with their endless mass against you – will you manage to overcome them and establish your reputation in the online high score?
