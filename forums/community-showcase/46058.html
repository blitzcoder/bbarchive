You know this gallery screenshot?
http://www.blitzbasic.com/gallery/view_pic.php?id=94&gallery=&page=7

this was my old project... some people asked me for source-code, but now i decided to publish it!

combobox is a bit unfinished, but it work... this GUI work on bb2d/bb3d. On bb+ not so perfect (keycodes changed in bb+ - and you must use LOAD(ANIM)IMAGE-commands with parameter 2 for faster graphics)

For BB2D/BB+ you must remove 3D-camera-object and modify few lines of code (removing camera-code).

This GUI is opensource - you can modify code - and you must publish your changes... you can also use this code in your commercial games/apps... you must display a link in game/app and in doku to my website http://www.miragex.de.vu (-edited-)

here are 300KB of GUI-codes
100KB sample-codes (15 files)
1 year of development

i publish this source and i hope this nice project do not die - i have no more time for this - and sorry i can't help you if you have dev-questions...

http://www.blitzbase.de/miragex.zip
