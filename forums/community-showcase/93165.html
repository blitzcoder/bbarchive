"The Night Before Christmas" is my second Christmas game and is an original retro-inspired Christmas themed game where the object of the game is to collect presents and put them under the Christmas tree.

The game is inspired by Mission Elevator but the gameplay is very different and will have you rushing around to make sure there’s enough presents under the Christmas tree and not too many kids sleepwalking around at the same time.

It has taken just under a month to develop and is made in BlitzMax.
