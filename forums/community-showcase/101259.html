Legions of Ashworld is one my project which has been in work (on and off) for too long. I am now pushing it into the final stage of development.

Legions of Ashworld is a turn-based strategy wargame set in a large fantasy world. The player leads various characters like warlords, kings and wizards with an obvious goal - to defeat enemies.
Unlike most strategy games which are map based with a top-down view, my game uses a "3d" view (like The Lords of Midnight). There are also some RPG elements in the game, like exploration and character traits.
