Hello everyone, I'm new here in the community, and I thought I would share my first project with you guys. I plan to sell this game once it is done, but of course, it still miss a lot of features and better graphics.

In Lunar Defender you will control the defenses of a moon base against hordes of alien attacks. It'll have three gameplay modes: the main campaign, which follows the story of some scientists in this base trying to find out what is going on; survival mode, where you are at the center of the screen and has to defend yourself against increasingly difficult waves of enemies; and puzzle mode, where you have to clear the screen from enemies using just the weapons and objects you have.

I'm posting all the progress of the game to a blog I created for this purpose, and I'm releasing the first betas as public. I will stop releasing them soon, and only make them available to a team of beta-testers(there are still a couple of spots for anyone interested, just contact me and we'll talk). Anyway, this game is still just 3 weeks old, and like I said, a lot is left to be done and improved. But since I'm new here, I thought I could post it here anyway to get some feedback from the more experienced users in here :) So if anyone has any praises or criticism, feel free to post them here. You can download 0.3b and 0.4b at the blog.

http://imakegames.wordpress.com/ (Yes stupid name :P )

You can contact me by posting here, or adding my MSN or e-mailing me: mothmanbr at gmail.com

The game will basically be an action game, the likes of Crimsonland and Phobia, but with some strategy elements.

Thanks in advance :)
