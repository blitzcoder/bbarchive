ST3D was born from a set of ideas and sub-projects, and aimed at the creation of an application, probably a VideoGame, based on the world of Star Trek. At the moment the 'puzzle' of ST3D contains following 'tiles':
1) A Simple Engine for spaces combats (Done)
2) A 3D Models of a new & original ships (Done)
3) A realtime 3D reconstruction of the set of Star Trek: The Next Generation (Done).
4) A realtime 3D reconstruction of Enterprise - D (W.I.P)

The most probable goal is the realization of a 'crossover' VideoGame like 'ST: A Final Unity' recycling a great part of the elements developed for these, and others, sub-projects; Sub-projects that at the moment must be considered a stand alone projects.

Star Trek 3D project still NOT an official Star Trek/Paramount Pictures/Viacom Software, but only a hobbystic tribute software. (See Disclaimer)
