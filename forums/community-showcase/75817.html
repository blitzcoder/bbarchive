At Last, My First Game.

ROBO ATTACK is something like pac-man, in the sense that you have to collect all the dots to win, while avoiding the enemy robots.

Something unique about this game is the walls of the maze. They are split in to two sides, a left side, and a right side. Which are colored purple, and blue.
Each side has 100 hit points, whenever a enemy robot touches a wall, the wall loses hit points.
when a walls hit points reach 0, then the side of the wall that was taking damage, will disappear, and then reappear somewhere else in the maze.

This effectively splits the walls, over time, into their two seperate sides. What this means is that instead of a purple and blue wall, you will have several just purple, or just blue ones instead.
And not only does this reform the maze over time, but also allows you to go through the walls, and reach dots that might have been hard to reach before.
With the purple walls, only the left side of them acts as a wall. There right side, you can just walk through.
It's the same with the blue walls, only their right side acts as a wall, the other side you can just walk through.

The controls are as follows.
For player 1
RIGHT ARROW: moves your character to the right.
LEFT ARROW: moves your character to the left.
UP ARROW: moves your character up
DOWN ARROW: moves your character down
ESC: exits the game

For player 2
A Key: moves him right
S Key: moves him down
D Key: moves him up
F Key: moves him right

This game can be played on 2 player, but both players must use the same keyboard.
It can be slightly ackward, but it works.
Have FUN!
