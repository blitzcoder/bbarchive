Xelev Games is creating a online RPG called BloodBane Online. You may have seen this appear a couple times before, and it has been re-created from scratch several times...Hopefully this is the last time.

BloodBane Online is a small online rpg made in Blitz3D from scratch. The models are free models from different sites, except for the terrain, which I made myself with T.ED...


Choose your alliance.
Interact with other players.
Explore vast landscapes as you fight your opposing enemies.
Order or Chaos, which will you decide?

The Server Selection Screenshot is just a Test I am not running 5 servers:)

Incase some do not know, a server selection screen is where you pick the instance of the world in which you want to play on. Most games have this because it would be very laggy if so many people were on at the same time, so they must divide the worlds into different server, containing a seperate copy of the world of the players who picked it.
