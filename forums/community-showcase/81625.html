Hagatha's Halloween Havoc for H+Soft Arcade



Here's a game I released awhile back for H+Soft Arcade around Halloween.

It's kinda crazy, but fun. You guide Hagatha (the witch) through 30 levels collecting candles and placing them in her Jack-o-Lanterns.
Get the magic wand on each level to shoot stuff.
Collect witch heads to earn continue credits.
Collect potions to replenish health.

Controls:
Use your left/right arrow keys to rotate Hagatha.
Tap: Left CTRL to fire (after getting the wand)
Tap: Left ALT to Flip (helps you with left/right rotation)

As all of my games you can also use a Game Pad.
