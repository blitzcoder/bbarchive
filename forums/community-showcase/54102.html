1-4 Gamepads (or joysticks with at least 4 buttons) required to stomp through 25 dungeons of crawling beasties.

Changes include - music (by Ajari Wilson)


New title (still subject to change though)
Better frame timing (still not perfect though but should be more playable on lower spec machines)
Camera adjusts if any player is difficult to see.
In game display panel replaced - press button 3 on your
game pad to get an overlay of your character's score,
number of keys held (yellow dots), number of potions held (blue dots).
Health bar prominent above character to allow you to have
a better idea of when things are getting rather dire.
New effect:Blood stains on the dungeon floor when killing
enemies.
Minor graphical fixes.
Small number of additional sound effects. I am still in need of character vocals for various effects.
