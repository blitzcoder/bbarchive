Hi slenkar, thanks for giving it a try! first-off the window mode I give-up on it was not fast enough for it. I think a game is Ready to Load by simply left-clicking Load, but if its been overwritten you can use the module installer "ChooseModule" then restart WarTactical & press Load, but Repeat-Right-clicking-Load after youve loaded a proper map, will give you many differrrent setups to try.(you could try the Scenario editor if your brave:)

You move by Left-clicking or LEFT/RIGHT keys to select your Troops , at the Moment Green is your Troops! once selected Make sure its not already moved it tells you on the dashboard, then a line from the unit to the mouse pointer appears(If your within the chits range & you havnt moved), if the Line is there you still have to make sure youve not moved it before! then Left-click destination where the Line allows(you will get further if you keep your troops with the HQs)

when your close to the enemy and a line is drawn from your selected unit to the enemy Right-click! to attack, when the confirm checkbox pops-up you can press LEFT/RIGHT for other targets that are close to where your attacking.
