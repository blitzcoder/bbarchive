Caff(Posted 2004) [#5]
Firstly -

Presentation is spot on. The graphics of the intro and the main titles is very good. I expect you'll add a main menu of some sort, to indicate a new game is starting, perhaps some options, player names etc.

As for the game itself, it's a fairly simplistic affair. I didn't go too far into it, but I would say if you could make the player move around, it would certainly add a new element of interest. Again the presentation is top notch, but the alien models are a bit basic and need some variation / change of attack pattern. I found the limit on ammunition a bit annoying too, a game like this is crying out for unlimited ammo and full on, frantic fast paced action (ala Serious Sam)

I didn't like the fact the game tries to connect to the internet after you die - the game froze, and because it takes up full screen I couldn't see the firewall message - therefore I had to ctrl+alt+del out. Very annoying. Remove the automatic connection and make it a manual option for the player. (If you are trying to contact an online hi-score server, as I suspect you are)

You should warn people before they download a demo like this, as they and I can't be sure of whether you are trying to access personal data and upload it to your site, or whether it's purely for stats / hiscore data...

Anyway it's a nice effort, add some variation in to it and it could do well.
