My current game (3D Breakout) has entered alpha stage and I would like to ask all you kind people here if you could please give me some feedback. Any form or fashion will do :)

A few pointers about the game:

System requirements:

500 MHZ Intel or AMD Processor
Windows 98/ME/2000/XP
DirectX 7.0 or newer MUST be installed
Direct3D compatible video card

Press 'spacebar' to switch between you/computer playing.
Press 'c' to change camera modes (used for testing)

You'll notice in the upper left corner a number (60/6754). This represents FPS/TRIS (Frames per second / triangles per frame)

Try to give me a detail analysis about the game. What you liked about it, what you didn't like. How it ran on your computer system. How did you like the sound effects, music, general game play and any other suggestions.

Thank you in advance!
