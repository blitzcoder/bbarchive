You, over there. Yeah, you! Do you have trouble finding partners in bars or clubs? Do you feel like there's an infinitely large hole in your life you need to fill? Do you have a desire to memorize pi to an excessively sad number of digits? If you answered yes to any or none of these questions, PiMorize is for you! Impress your friends with your incredible and useful knowledge of the world's favourite geometrical ratio using PiMorize!
[/end horrible intro]



Basically, PiMorize is a mostly complete improved clone of something similar someone made in Gamemaker. Increasingly large numbers of pi digits are read out to you in my beautiful voice, and it is your job to enter them over, and over again, until you either give up or stab your monitor. My current record is 40.

All the main features have been implemented, but there are still a few things left to do, such as create a way of changing settings (e.g. change the digit you start at) without going in to the ini file. I've also been told that the buttons don't look very nice, but I think I'd prefer to get a few more opinions before I change them.

You can download it here, or look at the latest source here.
