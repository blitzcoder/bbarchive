Since 23 December 2007, the beta demo version of AnoD or Angels of Dreams : Freed of the Captive ones will soon be available...

Theres altmost 4 class in the game: Angels, Humans, Demonites,Rulers

Angels:
Smart winged human based creatures developped in a terraformed Venus planet of the year 3700 ac...

Demonite:
Pathetic human sorcerer -but very evil- who polluated the Earth... they are descendant from real humans from today...
They cant fly but they have great powers...

Pacific Humans:
From the garden of Altara(3700's eden) they evolved from good humans from today and are blended with angel: they can fly...

The great King of Earth:
Leader of the human

Sphax:
The kings's councelor and the secret leader of the demonite

Xylvaniel the Great Archangel:
The leader of the angelic crew has Basically destroyed all evil human being...

Story
in the Year 3700 the king of all creation received a gift from angels: ten Sphere... One of those Sphere were empty so the king decided to base the Earth on it...

In that time, the genetically created race of Angels were fighting a war with Demonites... Sphax the evil king's councelor enclosed the King of Angels aka Xylvan into this Sphere and exilated the king into death...

Sphax became the evil leader of the earth and used the Great power of the sphere to enslavering the Humans and Angels... he tried many times to conquer and pervade human and angels earth but no human became evil so he felt asleep and Xylvan Saved the angels by sending his son to save him from its captivity... in the Spectral Sphere...

Angels of Dreams : Freed of the Captive one An epic struggle in the void of the Angelic Era, where everyone of the future will be councerned... Fight with us to free Xylvan and destroy Sphax the sleeping evil for the eternity...
