I thought a game where you could jump through time and change stuff could be quite fun. To see if I could get the basics working I made a little demo thing in which you can save a mans life. This is the first thing I've got to a working stage for ages so I thought I'd share it.

ZIP or RAR

Instructions:

The thing in the top left is the clock.

Left/Right Arrows to move
Hold Up Arrow to freeze time
While freezing time hold the Left Arrow to move time back
Press space to "talk" to the guy in red which makes him stop for a little while.

Currently you can only jump back in time, jumping forwards will be my next task.
