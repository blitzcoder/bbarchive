Today is first sunday of advent :-) Some will remember my small demo from last year. This time I put all things together in a single executable with new sampled music based on free MIDIs, grab it here and enjoy the peaceful show. Again - press Space to switch between the two modes, in firework mode press LMB for a new rocket and RMB to reset the rockets.
