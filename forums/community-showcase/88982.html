If you an owner of a WDTVLIFE System this is usefull for you.
A little app for osx to control your WDTVlife system without the IR Remote
Both Files must be in the same Directory.
Edit the Stats.txt with your Ip and Password.
Edit the Delay number for slow Networks or using Wlan.
Starts the App and remote with your Mac or Macbook the WDTVLIFE.
