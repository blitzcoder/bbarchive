This years soccermanager Anpfiff 2012 has been released. It supports German and English language. Anpfiff (means Kick-Off in German) has realistic game results and the most important features you need to manage your team.

You have a complex editor to change nearly everything in the game.
You can add original playerphotos, teamlogos, fansounds and pictures of stadiums.
