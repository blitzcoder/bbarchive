Greetings, mortals.
Welcome to Battle Pong!
A game that follows the same general rule as pong,
in the sense that you have to get the ball past your
opponent to score, but that is where the simularity
ends.

In Battle Pong you can also attack, and use powerups.

The keys are as follows.
Up and Down Arrow Keys to move up and down.
Left and Right Arrow Keys to attack, and use whatever
powerups are in your inventory.

Download it at http://hosted.filefront.com/werdnaworld
Enjoy!
