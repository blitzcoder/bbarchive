TartanTangerine (was Indiepath)(Posted 2005) [#12]
@Genexi2, thanks for the link, where did they get those old screenshots from?

@Everyone, although Caboodle may look similar to Lumines and has groups of 4 blocks that drop that is where the similarities stop. We have three types of blocks that are dropped, Lumines has two. We have two types of Powerups, Lumines has one. We have Gems that you have to collect that are essential to scoring bonus points, Lumines does not. Lumines gameplay relies on a constant sweep bar, we do not - block destruction is activated by you creating the first block. We introduce blocks from the bottom of the grid to increase difficulty, Lumines does not. We have a level based system, Lumines does not. We have pulses that are charged and remove complete lines of blocks, Lumines does not. The grid does not even have the same dimensions! Shall I continue?
