I'm proud to announce the official release of Chrysalis-Dx . Chrysalis-Dx is an evolution simulation game. You control the environment and let evolution do its thing. Chrysalis-Dx is not a fast paced game. It's a game you play in the background while you do other work. Check up on your experiment from time to time.
There's a feature which allows you to post your screenshots on your Facebook page directly from within the game.

A demo with one evolutionary path is available for download.
