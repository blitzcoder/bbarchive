Right Mouse to Thrust in the direction your pointing.
Left Mouse to Shoot at your target reticle.

There are about 10 levels, the level.txt file holds the level data its all in plain text so it should be easy enough for you to play with it. If you want more info on the way this file is made up just ask.

5 - 6 and 7 As the fire type, Pulse , Magma and Plasma

Pulse Fire is low in damage and scatters about a lot.
Magma is stronger than pulse but is more focused.
Plasma is Spread out and does the most damage.
