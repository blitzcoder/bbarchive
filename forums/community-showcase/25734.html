Hehe. After working with Blitz for nearly 3 years now I figured it was about time I actually released something which resembled a game. What started as a burst of inspiration has now taken form. My puzzle game, Trouble is now available to download from my website. As always, comments and ideas are welcome.

I would also welcome anyone with a creative mind to aid in level designing. Email me for further details and a copy of the editor.

To avoid confusion, V1.4 is the main game. The earlier version is difficult to play as blocks move more freely, making it tough to get them into place. If you want to play the older levels (included with V1.4 as well) use the parameter '-compatible'. It will automatically load in the older maps with the friendlier interface of the latest version.
