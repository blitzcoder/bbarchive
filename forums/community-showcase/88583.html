I decided to enter my first compo ever, the TIGSource Assemblee Compo. Using my knowlage of BlitzMax I made a whimsicly pixelated 2D platformer! I was trying to go for the classic-Gameboy look, and it even has the exact same resolution as a Gameboy. (160x144) Here's some of the readme:

A fun-tivity for the whole family! Quest through 3 levels of varing difficulty as you seek a stolen Item from the Dragon!

Feature!
* 3 Select Class!
* Slay 6 Types Enemy!
* Collect Several Points!

# -%%%%- #

Assets Used!
1-Bit Sprites by Oryx
Musical Stylings by zpxlng, Saros, and valzi.
Sound effects from Atuun and Saros.

# -%%%%- #

Control!

Arrow-Keys --> Movement
Z --> Primary
X --> Jump
Spacebar --> Secondary (Unlock after Only!!)
Escape --> Flee Buttan


Because of the wonders of BlitzMax, this game is available on Windows, (Intel) Mac, and Linux!

Here's some links:
Gamejolt Quickplay!
Windows || Mac OSX || Linux

The only problem I encountered between the platforms is that "StopChannel" apparently is applied different between the Windows and Mac platforms... but it's fine now.
