I found my dusty old projects (about 6 years old) on a dusty old CD and thought some of you might be interested in some of this. Most of them have source with it, so beginners might get some hints from it, though i haven't commented my source, and by that time i wasn't very good in programming either (about 16 years old and had occasionally programmed for 5 years). There are games and (pretty useless) programs.
Some of them give MAV at the beginning, but mostly they work.

Some screenshots of the games:

* HLP Game - just a bloody game where i tried apllying red colour to grayscaled images
* Bloody Ride - my first "3D" game, using Carmaggedon Splat Pack sounds
* Hool 2 - tried to do some simple physics (blood), otherwise just shootem up
* Bouncer - This was my first AI project, try it with Ai players. I've even thought this has been the best (most addictive) game i've ever made
