I have finished my game Mano Trooper, a remake of Paratrooper. It was programmed in Blitz Max.

Direct download link: manotrooper.zip (3.6 MB)

You have to shoot down helicopters, paratroopers, jets and bombs. Each shot costs 1 point, so in order to get a nice score some careful aiming is required. There are two dogs who will attack landed soldiers but after each attack they will sleep for a while. Four landed soldiers will start creating a human pyramid, and after that it's game over! The only way to take out a landed soldier is to have another paratrooper fall on them.

Controls are left and right arrow keys for aiming, and Z (or Y or space) to shoot.

The music is by coda (coda.s3m.us).
