The company I worked for, released it's AVITool DLL module for the price of a large burger meal. (Taxes might apply to some people though)

You can load/save AVI's in most installed codecs, but without sound.
Frames can be grabbed almost directly to pixmap for max speed.

(Used to be an inhouse tool for video capture of application for promo material/testers etc.)

There's full BlitzMax example source, but the DLL should be usable in other languages such as BlitzPlus and Blitz3D.
(Untested however, but we're happy to try to help out with the porting)

To see example source + avi output (as well as other info) go to http://www.nhsoft.net/?page=avitool
