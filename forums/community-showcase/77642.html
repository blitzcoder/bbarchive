MadJack(Posted 2008) [#9]
hey NA

The difference between V1.21 and 1.00 is pretty massive ;-)

V1.21 includes all the levels that were in V1.00 but includes three new levels featuring a new playstyle involving capturing hardpoints. This brings the total number of levels up to about 20, which gives around 6-8 hours entertainment.

The existing levels were also reworked to make them play better and address issues players had with V1.00 (e.g. shortened some of the walking levels, added a sprint function etc, tweaked difficulty levels, extra on-screen info added, etc..).

The core gameplay was reworked to give a faster and smoother experience and extra features have been added in the form of new cutscenes, skimish mode and new purchaseable powerups such as dropturrets.

So you won't get a totally new game, but you will get a game that has been both expanded and greatly improved!
