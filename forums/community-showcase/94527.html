Eternal Crisis(Posted 2011) [#12]
Art wise, no, this isn't the best as seen from the LE website/world. However, those screens you normally see are merely demonstrating art and the rendering power/quality of LE. All of them, or at least most of them, have zero game mechanics beyond basic camera control.

The game being posted in this forum is demonstrating game mechanics, not art, and demonstrating those mechanics built within a days work (plus some by now, and by the time that screen-shot was created). It's demonstrated well, I should say, as it does more than what it seems. More than just barrel physics.

Basic AI using a navigation mesh, AI sound/health/weapon, weapon effects/sound, basic movement, basic GUI, basic radar GUI, day/night cycle with clouds, none of that comes packaged with LE and is written from scratch by Marley's Ghost. He did an excellent job, especially with his limited amount of time, and I wait to see further progress.
