Foppy(Posted 2004) [#2]
This looks and sounds very good! The title screen of which you have a screenshot is impressive, for instance, and also the sound effects during the game and the incoming meteors, really great.

I have two remarks/ideas about how the game plays.

When aiming, movement seems a bit "jumpy", as if jumping along points on a grid, instead of moving smoothly. This makes the game more difficult than it would otherwise be: when the groox are coming in from one side while you still have to detroy them on the other side this can be a bit frustrating, I think it would be more fun if the aiming was easier. (But I don't know if you can decide this, or whether this is a technical issue.)

The second remark is about the radar. This radar is obviously a very important aspect of the game. The player will constantly look at it, then rotate the gun and shoot at the enemies, then check the radar again... since the radar is at the bottom left of the screen this can be a bit tiring on the eyes. Maybe it would be nice to use the aim as the radar. You would plot the radar blips as usual except that you don't plot them at the lower left of the screen but around the aiming device. That way the player always has the information on incoming enemies at the center of view, which would make for more direct and intuitive game play.
