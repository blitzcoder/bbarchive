John Pickford(Posted 2005) [#33]
This is more of a gameplay thing than a visual change: Standing on a building claims "ownership" of that tile + all connected building tiles. Enemy units cannot stand on tiles owned by the other side.

Whilst standing on a building you own, your units have virtually infinite movment distance. So you could theoretically defend all four sides of a large castle with a single unit.

The building returns to 'neutral' status when your unit leaves.

You'll also be able to enter\leave buildings by the ground floor. So you can get to the top of towers by entering the base.
