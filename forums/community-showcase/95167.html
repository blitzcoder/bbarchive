I wrote the game logic in BMax and ported it to c#.
Mini Puzzler is free and includes 15 puzzles.

Download Mini Puzzler

Controlles
---------
Click on a puzzle piece and click on another piece to switch them.
Hover over the preview to show a guide.
Press "P" to pause the game
Press "S" to solve the puzzle
Press "Escape" ingame to go to the menus.
