Hi All

Another Super-Quick made project by me, Cygnus Logo v1.0.
What is it? Best way i can describe it, is "instructing a robot with a pen in its ass where to go and what to draw."

http://damiensturdy.pwp.blueyonder.co.uk/CygLogov1.zip

Supports the basic Logo commands, Repeat, Functions, variables and Maths Evaluation.

Oh, and It can be made to use a 3D-View, Simulating the Logo Robot used in schools years ago.

Give this a test, and any bugs you find, Let me know? :D

Controls:

F10: Editor/Immediate mode. Use Editor mode to create your functions. Immediate to execute them
F9: Toggle 3D Mode.
F8: Toggle Itteration (Lines are drawn slowly, rather than all in one go)
F7: In 3d mode, toggle view angles. (Needs work)

You can click/Drag the logo Drawing Area with the mouse in 2d mode.
