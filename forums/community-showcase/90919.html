Hi everyone. For all you gravity freaks out there, here's a test program I wrote a while back that simulates launching a spaceship to the moon.
Be warned. This simulation is VERY HARD! These are the steps

1)You first start in Low Earth Orbit
2)Accelerate to (earth)escape velocity+
3)Coast towards the moon
4)Adjust velocity so that you're captured by the moon
5)Orbit the moon a few times to prove your success
6)Accelerate to (moon)escape velocity+
7)Coast towards the earth
8)Adjust velocity so that you're captured by the earth
9)Orbit the earth a few times to prove that you've returned home.

Keep in mind that while you're on your journey, the moon is also orbiting the earth.
