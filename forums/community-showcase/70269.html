Overview:
Puzld1 is a sort of puzzle game, wherein the player tries to fill up the board whilst gaining the most possible points. After a board is finished, the player is given a letter grade; "A", "B", "C", "D", or "F." Multiple boards gain in difficulty for fast play, and the boards.info file can easily be modified for additional boards and play time.
