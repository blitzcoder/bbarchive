Maybe you know that problem. You want to upload some pictures to a webspace. But the wespace hast restrictions (e.g. 200kb per file). But nobody want's to resize 150 pictures manually! That was the cause for writing this tool! It reads all pictures from a directory, resizes them and saves them to another directory!
Since Version 1.1 there's also the facility to pack all pictures into one.
From 1.2 on you can load BMPs to!

The tool is crossplattform..well..it supports only Linux and Win, since i have no MacOS.

The download can be found here. (Sorry german site, but that site is in english)
