This game use 2 very cool feature, AStar Path finding and 3D Quads ( both written my me ).

This is 1 of those puzzle games that people like gamehouse.com popcapgames.com and realarcade.com seem to like to make/publish so I've jumped on money train (I hope).

Objective is to line up 3 or more same colour counters in a vertical or horizontal line, this will score points and you will see your point-omiter (Green bar on the right) increase, when this bar fills up its up to the next level where you have to score more points to win the level and so on until you either get board or the board fills up.
