Looks good though, how have you done the controls?



Cheers! :)

Well, I've tried a few things and settled on the auld inertia approach...

To raise the dart, you keep the RMB down, to lower the dart, you let go of RMB, to move left and right, you 'nudge' the mouse in the respective direction and the dart sorta sweeps from left to right (Or vice versa), to chuck the dart, you simply press LMB.

It feels awkward at first, but after a little practice, its a pleasant feeling swinging the dart around the screen, because you have to think that little bit ahead on when to sweep the dart back (Which in essence will slow the dart down first, before going in the opposite direction)
