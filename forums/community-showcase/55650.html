I just thought I'd show you all the new version. As yet I'm still undecided what I want to do with the engine but there are quite a few possibilities.

Some screenies ...





Firstly, you'll need to configure your joypad. Load the file JOYPAD from the Config folder. Use the mouse to click on Up or Right on the appropriate joypad and then move the stick in that direction. If the guide spot goes in the opposite direction you expected, click on that axis again and move the stick in the opposite direction. Note that you will also need to set up 3 buttons. To do this just click on the button number, then press the button on your joypad. Joypad dead spots can also be amended.



Current set up is for a PS2 joypad, where button1 = SELECT , 2 = TRIANGLE , 3 = R1.

CONTROLS

SPACE = Reset Demo
BACKSPACE = Alternate Scene between playground and race track.
Pad Button1 = Change Car
Pad Button2 = Change Camera
Pad Button3 = Hold for a rear view

You can change your screen set up / depth etc... by editing the file "Setup.cfg" via a text editor. You can also set the demo up for 2 players. Screen is split vertically for best view.

THE VEHICLES

Major handling improvements .. at least I think so. A wee bit of sound for engine and skidding. Collsion Improvements.

Hovercraft

KNOWN ISSUES

Camera View needs to automatically adjust to ensure no geometry obscuring view.
Need more camera angles and better system in general. Cameras are a pain!!
Vehicles sometimes get stuck under things. Unavoidable with the playground scene.


I'd really appreciate any feedback. I'm mostly interested in your FPS , how you found the joypad setup and how you found the vehicle handling?
