I played through stage 3-8, and quit -- While I like Tetris & it's derivatives like Qwirks a lot, this one just felt... Boring.

I don't want to sound overly negative, but here's my initial impression:

1) the gameplay isn't smooth enough. The eggs drop a full line at a time, and pressing the 'down' arrow still takes forever to drop an egg all the way to the bottom. It will probably feel much smoother if you lower the eggs a partial line at a time -- less choppy.

2) Some of the graphics are too similar, especially the yellow egg with the greenish edge and the yellow egg with the brownish egg

3) Too easy. Any time you get some kind of built-up (2/3 full?) I kept getting several bombs in a row which cleared a substantial chunk of the screen again. I never even got close to feeling I was going to be in trouble.

After playing this for a bit, I felt that there's a good reason that the various tetris-type games use blocks that consist out of at least two segments, vs. the single egg you are using here.

Overall rating: "Meh."
