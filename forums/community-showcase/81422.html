Hi there,

I just wanted to show you my last game (& the first I promote to this forum): Razoon

Razoon is a 360° spaceshooter with the only focus on making as much damage to other ships as possible.

I've been developing this game since 2006 and I hope you like it as much as I do ;)

Features:
- 4 different types of Rockets
- a buddy ki ship that helps you
- many different Enemies
- big explosions
- lots of secrets in 10 (+ 10) Levels
- onlinehighscore




Controls:
W,S - Accelerate, Decellerate
A,D . Strafe Left/Right
Spacebar - Hold direction
Mouse: Steering and Turret direction
Mousekey 1: Fire primary weapon
Mousekey 2: Fire current selected Rocket

R - auto-rocket selector module on/off
if off: choose rocket-type with 1,2,3 or 4
C or Mousekey 3- choose closest enemy
T - auto aim help module on/off
LAlt - Screenshot
Escape - Resign / Destroy ship

You get hitpoints by dealing damage. You can destroy incoming missiles by shoot at them (your on bord auto-mg will do so by itself)

Enjoy!
Firstdeathmaker
