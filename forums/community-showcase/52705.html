xlsior(Posted 2005) [#25]
What, in your opinion, would be better - if game speed will be saved when player exit the game and restores when he will continue the game next time. Or set it to default each time when he starts the game?


I would set it to default when you start a new game, unless you also use multiple player profiles -- if you have multiple profiles, each player could remember his/her last used settings. You can tie that to the username entered.

As far as the speedup is concerned, you could also make it temporary, to be reset at the end of the current wave to give you time to build new towers, etc... Or give the player a checkbox or something to lock the speed change so it stays the way it is until the player chooses otherwise.

Another suggestion: You may want to consider revamping the scoring system a little -- I hate to drag warcraft 3 into it again, but I do like how they implemented it: You get a small amount of cash immediately after killing each unit, and a larger amount as a bonus after completing the wave. That way killing a bunch of units early on in a level may give you some extra money to upgrade your defenses further down the map, rather than having to wait for some cash inflow at the end of the level.

But again: this game looks very professional the way it is already. Great graphics, and good to play.
