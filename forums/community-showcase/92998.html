Just played through the demo, and now I'll give a short review :)

Graphics 8\10
Very good graphics, I quite liked them. I know that some of it was done
with TimelineFX, because I recognized those explosions, but where did
you get the enemy sprites? Those are really cool looking.

Sound 7\10
Good music. Not spectacluar, but it fits the game nicely, and sounds kinda
cool.

Controls 3\10
I found the controls very odd. My fingers felt cramped being so darn
close together when using the arrow keys with one hand, and 'm' ',' '.'
and '/' with the other.

Gameplay 5\10
I can see quite a few Defender inspirations here, which is pretty cool.
However I personally found the gameplay a tad too hard(I kept blowing up), and a bit one note. Although it was only the demo I played,
so perhaps the later levels of the game add more variety.


All in all, I'd give the game a 6.5 rating.

Cheers Playniax, I look forward to your future work :)
