Demo version limited to 60 mins play time.

**Cartoon Skin** Others to follow.

Features 3 different modes of play.

- Classic - Constant Block dropping action, score well to progress and unlock new scenes.
- Casual - Match the blocks and capture the Gems to progress to the next level. (My Fav)
- Puzzle - (Only 7 Puzzles in Demo) - Remove the puzzle blocks from the game grid.

This is our first product using BlitzMax so there may be a few issues. Most likely there will be issues with sound. Please report any issues, or general feedback here.

The highscores are handled by the new IndieNet dll, if you experience any issues with the highscore uploads or download please report here.
