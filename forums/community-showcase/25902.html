Just wanted to announce that I've finally completed my first shareware game, Defensor!

It's a 2D Defender-type game in the demo version, but it's much more than that in the full version, with new backgrounds, new alien types with individual characteristics, collectible bonuses and power-ups, end-of-level guardian, etc....

Thanks to Jason Brasier, Marco Janssen, and Paul Green for all their suggestions, and to Marie Isaac for the soundtrack.

Screenshots, plus a fully playable trial/demo version is available from www.gamerblitz.com/Defensor.htm .
