TeraBit(Posted 2003) [#8]
Thanks for all the feedback. There are a number of interesting ideas here.

The lag is probably caused by the undo system making a copy of the current texture when you begin painting. This can be a little slow depending on your system specs I suppose. I've never had a problem with is personally.

a) The controls are a bit flakey I must admit. It's been the aspect of the application I've disliked the most.

b) At the moment, you can only change the shape and attributes of the Airbrush tool. I only intended the Solid brush for places where you need Hard lines and so is very basic.

Screen res control is coming too.

Thanks again,
