Yes, after what seems like an eternity, I now have my first complete Blitz game, which I am now sending out into the commercial world.

Rockfall - The Return, for those that haven't seen it, is a classic puzzler where you have to collect gems while avoiding falling boulders and dealing with other nasty surprises from various creatures.

Both the demo and the full game are available from my website (link in my sig). The full version offers:

- More than 70 levels (demo has 10)
- 100's more downloadable from the site
- More music
- More graphics
- Savable high scores
- Ability to save progress through a game
- Built-in level editor

Special thanks go to all involved on the project, especially Feo for providing the excellent graphics, plus Krylar, George Bray and Robleong for their advice, guidance and play testing.

Please enjoy and post any comments here.
