History:
Dated May 31st, 2007:
Chris:

Let me first start out by introducing Hopping Stone Games. HSG is a fledgling company, named so because the games we are making are our 'stepping stones' towards bigger and better things. For now, we are focusing on making many small productions to expand our horizons and increase our understanding of BlitzMax, and possibly other languages as well. HSG currently is Mike Wojtkowski and Chris Arendt.

Now, the game...

Metalord is a small two-day project that was officially the first Hopping Stone Games production. Because it was written and playtested in only two days, of course some things may be lacking here and there. The point is that it was made in two days, and that we were, at the time, trying to make as many games as possible. However, I think it's a fun little game with enough gameplay to keep you occupied for a half hour or so, and a good addition.

So, more on the game. It's a top to bottom space scroller, wherein the objective is to earn points. The player earns points mainly through avoiding asteroids, but is capable of earning through breaking up the rock formations with their trusty laser cannon. There are a total of four different pick-ups to be obtained, each with a unique result; A health pickup repairs/upgrades your ship, therefore increasing its lifespan, a cooldown pickup reduces the amount of time between shots, a gun pickup increases the number of shots fired each time, and a whipeout pickup explodes all nearby asteroids.

Please visit our website, www.HoppingStone.com for download, screenshots, how to play, system requirements, etc. We thank you for the support!
