JazzieB(Posted 2007) [#6]
Nice little game and well done. I got as far as the middle of the third set before I ran out of time. Just a few things...

1. Don't really like how it automatically starts a game as soon as you've entered a name for your profile the first time it is run. Should really go to the main menu ... especially as you have some help and the options screen there.

2. I tested this under Vista and I'm sorry to have to report that you have the dreaded 1 second delay on sound effects and music playing. You need to check and swap to the DirectSound or OpenAL driver instead.

3. And lastly, it doesn't un-install itself cleanly, as it leaves saves and player profiles behind. These should either be deleted as well, or the user given a choice whether to keep them or not. I fell for this one on one of my projects too!

Other than those comments, it's a really polished game and you deserve to do well with it. As it's a BlitzMax game, you should consider releasing a Mac version at some point in the future.

Good job.
