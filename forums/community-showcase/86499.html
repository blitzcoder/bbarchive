Brucey(Posted 2009) [#25]
Ideally, you wouldn't use a big image anyway. You'd render all the hex tiles on-the-fly, based on the terrain of the specific location. Then render the units on top of that.
Then the windowing problem wouldn't be a problem any more, as you could decide which parts of the large map you wanted to display, and only render that section to the screen.

Otherwise, you might get away with changing the ViewPort before drawing the big map, but this may have issues with some integrated graphics drivers.
