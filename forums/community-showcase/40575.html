The beginings of a tiny racing game:


http://www.frecle.net/misc/TinyRacers.zip (~470Kb)

All the physics are done in Blitz and uses the infamous verlet system and a couple of hacks.

Features:
- Fully customizable cars
- 4WD/FWD/RWD
- Steering on Front/Rear/All Wheels
- 'Realistic' physics
- Golf hole, loop, jumps and bumps!
- Up to 4 players at once!

Key Layout:
PL1 - Arrow Keys + PgDn to flip car / Joystick 0
PL2 - ASWD + R to flip car / Joystick 1
PL3 - IJKL + P to flip car / Joystick 2
PL4 - Numpad 8456 + Enter to flip car / Joystick 3
