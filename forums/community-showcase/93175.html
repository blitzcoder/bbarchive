Free Holiday Mini game: Santa's Xmas Havoc 3D/2D

I was bored with some time off work and made this little free-bee Xmas mini game for my 3-year old daughter - and rest of the fam. It should be entertaining for all ages. A simple side scroller, you have 60 seconds to help santa collect the presents while avoiding the bombs and hitting warp zones for speed to save time. Just use the Up/Down arrows to move santa up or down. Or, use your game pad/joystick. My little one loved this with the goofy santa voices and music.
