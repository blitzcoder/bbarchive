After 2 years the third game in the Robocop 2D trilogy is now up for download.
First started as a Blitz Basic program like 2D 1 & 2 but then ported to Blitz max.
2D3 now lets the player walk in all 8 directions more like a SOR/FFight beat`em-up,
now faster & with new graphic effects and easier 3 button controls.

Pc, Mac(Intel) & Linux versions are all in the Robocop2D3.zip
