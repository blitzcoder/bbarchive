I thought I said, Anyway its a worms clone. but with sheep.

Its already got a lot of work done on it, so its already semi playable. its got a random environmental type thing with weather like rain,snow,thunger&lightnin,sleet, random wind direction and speed ( all in real time )

Its also got 2 player controlled teams, Our goal was at the time to make it networked with a lobby.

Where we wanted to maike it stand appart from Worms was in the multi player mode, it was our intention to give the player a team of sheep, each sheep has a set number of lives, when its been killed say 3 times it dies and that player either has to play his next game a sheep down or spend some of the cash(won from game wins) and hire a new sheep.

the Grave Yard at the menu was then going to fill up with the graves of your fallen heros.

Anyway thats just some of what was planned and whats in it just now, it still needs some work but given a healthy intrest from the community it could be finished in a matter of weeks.
