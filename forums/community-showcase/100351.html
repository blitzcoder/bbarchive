Hey guys VOD is intended to be a sandbox/adventure game heavily inspired by Starflight 1/2

I started learning blitzmax many years ago and for one commitment or another did not continue. I recently started back learning late last year and decided to have another go at creating my idea for this type of game.

I do not have a suitable public build, but here are some videos I have uploaded.

When I do have a suitable public build, depending on if anyone is interested I will post it here. Also please see my topic in general discussion, I need some help crafting this into a game people will actually want to play.

Right now in VOD you can

Blow stuff up
Upgrade your ship
Explore an entire sector
Scan planets
Scan anomalies
Communicate with alien races
Dock
Buy Different Ships
Probably some stuff I forget

Some Stuff you will have access to in the game, through exploration but which of course can be done in a test build.

Cloaking
Jump Engine - because the sector is so big, flying everywhere would be excruciating so my idea of fast travel is a jump drive which you can acquire.
Pulse Shield - this destroys all incoming projectiles from enemy ships
Phalanx Point Defense: this shoots down all incoming projectiles within a certain range
Phase Modulator: When turned on enemy fire will pass through your ship.
Tractor Beam: what does this do? hehe
Nanite Device: Uses nanites to repair your armor
Ablative Matrix: Gives your armor and your hull a mitigation factor for incoming damage

All of those use energy, some use so much that your capacitor will be seen to be draining, some don't use enough to cause a drain more than your recharge rate, so there are some you can use more than one of, but some which you cannot use for very long such as the phase modulator.


Planets have interesting i information about them. Anomalies are dangerous, some are like wormholes where you can enter/exit a fixed point. Others are unpredictable, you could enter/exit a fixed point, be thrown randomly out somewhere unknown, or be seriously damaged + more.

I am trying to give you an idea of what is in so far, you can do so much, but I need to be able to use some of these features to make the game want to be played.

Like why should you explore? What is the benefit?

If you are familiar with starflight 1 or 2, you will know if gave you the feeling of small ship big universe, which is what I want to capture here, you are in charge of a starship/crew and can go anywhere etc. But I need some fun reasons for someone to click that .exe and decide to play.

There is a crew system, your crew will die if they do not eat. They get hungry and tired, and you can even monitor where they are on your ship, like the gym or the mess hall or the bridge, when they get hungry, they go and eat, when tired they go to barracks/crew quarters, and you can monitor your crew from a manifest and see all this info on them, health, hunger, fatigue, location etc...

You can even deploy mining drones to planets, and return and collect them when they are full. You can mine asteroids, or planets for precious ore to sell for interstellar credits to buy stuff to upgrade your ship.

Giving you more ideas of what is there, but I need ideas or help how to make them more compelling, or turn them into fun activities etc...

Since I will post a public build, this can stay here for reading for then but in the mean time perhaps people could reply to general or to here with the help.
