The project has started in september/october 09 and is still going on. This is a remake of a java game from funorb, originally completly personnal, that I then decided to share in june 2010. It's reuses a very similar engine I rewrote from scratch, with only a few differences, the most noticable one is the fact the game runs at 60fps, while the original was only 50. :)

Your aim is to dig in a volcano and escape from it with your pockets filled of valuables. But the volcano, as you might espect, isn't really a safe place to be! Sooner or later, it will ... icerupt.
