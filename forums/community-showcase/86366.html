Don't know if it's a bug or not. When an elevator is moving, Sunny can go right through it without any collision detection.


Jur(Posted 2009) [#10]
That is by design. I have tried different ways of how Sunny interacts with moving lifts (like being pushed away). There were always problematic situations with collision approaches. The "problem" is that this game has no "death". What happens if you put Sunny into the lift station hole and lift comes? In many games that would be solved easily - he get killed. I designed this game to be without "deaths" so I had to take care of all these "dangerous" situations the best I could.
