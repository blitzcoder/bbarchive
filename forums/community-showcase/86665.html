Luis de la Cruz(Posted 2009) [#12]
mkg, I use the FastExt libreries in addition to Blitz (www.fastlibs.com by Mikhail Vostrikov[the best]). This excellent library provides projective shadows for Blitz, realistic water, postprocess effects, bummapping,...and now it has a very good price. Now I'm updating the game with the new feature of Treeparty (by Gothasoft), tree real-time shadows.

Also I created a grass system seemed to GrassParty (BlitzDinamicGrassSystem).

I am a programmer but I have realized or modified 90 per cent of the models of the game, with his textures and 100 per cent of the animations. Also all the code in Blitz3D of the game.

Definitively a lot of work during several years.
