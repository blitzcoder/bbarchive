Rob Farley(Posted 2003) [#9]
JFK,

I'm working on a new version, I don't really like talking about it too much as I don't know how long it will be at the moment...

However...
Shadowing has been improved loads and sped up.
Lighting... um... well... will be better but it total pants at the moment... but my thought process is much better! In theory it will allow for multiple coloured lights and so on, just doesn't work yet!
Terrain Generation
Texture Generation
More power on the texturing side of thing, so you specify a texture between a height range and angle, this means you could have beach type rock on steep slopes in low level land and mountain rock on steep slopes at high levels.
A more modular way of working so the workflow is more efficiant. You create light maps, shadow maps, steep maps and texture maps seperately so you don't need to re-calculate things that don't need re-calcuating so everything runs faster.

Before everyone gets excited about this it's not just round the corner, there's currently no front end and a lot of this stuff is theory rather than practice... however, that's the plan and much of it is going well.
