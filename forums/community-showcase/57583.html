Hey everyone. I've added some more, basic, features to my editor. This includes:

The ability to lock movement to any axis.
The ability to force movement along a grid on any or all axis.
A rotation mode.
Manipulation space: Local, Global or World.
Local and Global are the blitz equivilant. World means, the the World axis. Say you have a mesh rotated at 0,45,0. You switch to world mode and rotate it along the X axis, it will turn it, using the World axis.

Not much happening i guess, but i thought i'd post a small update.

Controls. Pretty universal.

Left click: Select a mesh
Left click and drag: Manipulate item along the X,Z axis
Right click and drag: Manipulate item along the Y axis.
Middle click and drag: Manipulate the camera.

XYZ at the top of the screen, is the Axis lock. The X Y and Z at the bottom, is the grid lock.
