Chroma,

Project PLASMA is very much alive. Yes it is true that we have lost members, but, this is the nature of the beast (open source project) and it has been anticipated. All active Team Plasma Members have continue to show their undying commitment and support to this project.

My goal has been to provide a basic direction without micro-managing the development effort. The >>> About Project Plasma <<< provides a list of members and responsibilities, engine design abstract, and game design abstract. Interested parties should be able to use this information here to determine what part of the engine/game that they would like to work on. I post updates, demos, and news when I receive them.

As Project Coordinator, I have made many compromises in a attempt to satisfy ALL Team PLASMA members. But, this can be a very difficult task to achieve considering everyone has a 'their' way of doing things.

I will remain steadfast in seeing the project to completion. Completing this Project is ultimately the driving force for me, and in my opinion should be the same driving force for Team Plasma.

In conclusion, we are very far into the development cycle of the engine. Any and every contribution you make will be greatly appreciated.
