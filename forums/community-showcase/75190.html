Chugs40(Posted 2008) [#10]
Hey Grey thanx for playing.

Yeah about level 5 the game starts getting a bit more tricky and problem solving. Any unreachable ice or stone tiles are blown up with TNT, you just have to find the right detonator. As for the stone (grey) tiles (at foot level) santa can roll through them by pushing and holding down while moving left or right. As all of my games ESC exits the game and the SPACE BAR pulls up the game menu. I also want to add, keys can be tricky becouse they switch open and locked gates every time you pick one up. So make sure you get the presents or items behind open gates first.

Anyways, check it out a bit longer if you have time. I will be sure to check out your new game as well :)
