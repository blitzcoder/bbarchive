2 months has passed now since I started on this one and I've come to another fork in the road. Best way to solve it I guess is to let you guys try it out and tell me what you think is the best way to play it.
There are no tutorial in the game right now. Only 2 themes and 1 tune but hey.. it's a test :D

Instructions:
Click on a cube and drag while holding mousebutton to rotate the specific wheel.
Click on empty area and drag while holding mousebutton to rotate the whole scene.

Objectives: Remove all cubes from the wheels to release the wheels. Enough released wheels lets you go to next level.
