Here are some game details just to make your play a little better and to try and reduce your learning curve.

Left Mouse : Fire
Right Mouse : Thrust

There are 3 fire types at the moment, with missiles planned before its main release.

currently there 5 safe power ups, 1 for each gun type, 1 shield and 1 power cell. the power cells recharge your gun power, the more you have the less chance your guns energy bar will run out.

There are mines, that drop from time to time, they have a 5 second timer on them, get close and it starts, stay close and your dead, the timer will reduce as you progress through the levels.

The levels come in stages, each stage has 5 sets of asteroids to clear.

At the start of some stages comets will fly by, these things will aim for your ship, so if you plan to just pick a corner and sit there blasting its not going to work.

At the end of each stage, or each set of 5 levels, all of the blasting you have done has created a field of debris this is a level with around 20+ mini asteroids all fast moving, and all deadly with your guns or shield are not up to the job, there are also 3 comets, to make sure your not planning on just sitting still and twirling the mouse o.O.

The levels are basic at the moment, only the first 10 are really accurate, the other 40 are just copies of the first 10 with the type of asteroids changed, thats something I am still working on, fine tunning each of them to challenge the player.

As for the controls. I know some of you would expect a w,a,s,d and mouse to shoot setup, I did have this but found that the game was way to easy, something i might look at again in the future tho.

Anyway. thanks in advance for looking at this. its one of those life long projects you can never seem to finish so would be good to finally get the book closed on it.

@Starwar : thanks m8, yeah the shield has only 3 levels so after your shield drops your fair game for any rogue asteroid or comet. There will be 1up's floating around but yeah you will start with only 1 ship. any more will be gained through a score bonus or one of the mentioned 1up's that you can find.

As for the hud, I still need to do some help screens but it should be prety self explanatory, your ship bottom left, shows 3 rings around it, these are your sheilds.

The 3 main powerbars next to your shield are your left,mid and right gun cells, when you start only your middle gun cell will drop in power as you fire. as you power up to shooting two bullets, your left and right cells will degrade and then when your on full gun power shooting 3 bullets all 3 cells will degrade.

Your power cells can be recharged faster by collecting more batteries, which you will see floating around.

Quick Tip, any drop with a green indicator is safe. anything with a red one is deadly. an orange one shows items that could help or hinder your progress.
