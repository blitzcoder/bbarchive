JoshK(Posted 2010) [#7]
We're designing Leadwerks 3.0 to make it easy for people to make real games without a lot of programming. You can dive into programming as deep as you want, or you can just work with scripted components that are provided. We'll also provide a library of scripted 3D models you can just drop into your game and let run. I've got a pretty good idea by now where people run into trouble, and I think we can make version 3.0 both easier and more powerful.
