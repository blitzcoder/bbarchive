xlsior(Posted 2009) [#10]
The 'whatismyipaddress.com' will give you your internet address, but if you are behind a router you would also need to add a port forward in your router to point it to a PC on your internal network, otherwise the router will simply discard the incoming packets addressed to you.

If you are playing on your LAN, then you don't specify your external IP but your local area network IP --open a command prompt (start -> run -> cmd.exe) and type 'ipconfig'
It will give you your LAN IP.
