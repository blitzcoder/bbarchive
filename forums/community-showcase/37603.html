I posted this a while back, but have made a few changes, and since I've been spending so much time on other projects, and going back to school, it doesn't look like I'm going to get around to finishing this any time soon. So I'm making the 'alpha' version of this available for download to anyone who wants to play :)

Wings of Rage (for lack of a better name) is kind of a Joust meets Unreal Tournament type of game. The concept was to have riders collect/raise genetically engineered ostriches, and use them in tournament battles.

This program basicly tests the flight mechanics, basic combat with 2 different weapons, and bot A.I. (this was my first time doing this type of game and creating bot AI. I know it has lots of room for improvement, but as it is now, it's sorta fun)..

http://www.forestmarkstudios.com/wings_of_rage_page.htm

Instructions are on the website.

For anyone who wants (and while my bandwidth lasts), enjoy :)
