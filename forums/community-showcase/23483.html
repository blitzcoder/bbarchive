Wandering Knights is a RPG project I started in mid 2002, not a long while ago. It made a while since I wanted to create my own computer RPG, but was a bit afraid of undertaking such a huge project, especially since all the 3D stuff was new to me. This is where I started Nuclear Nibble 3D, got some experience, and finally undertook this project that is Wandering Knights. I also wanted to make Wandering Knights as unique as possible, with the less typical, clichés RPG aspects, giving out a weird hack and slash game.
Wandering Knights is based on a huge random and unique world, that is being created while you play. Wandering Knights starts when a deamon came from hell, terrorizing people out. You are a wandering knight, running in the wilds, and you now have to decide where you go, what you will do, etc. Will you go and hunt the devil, or simply run around the world, looking for oddities? In Wandering Knights, you are free to go and do as you please, the essence of a pure hack and slash game.

October 10, 2003 *Beta 0.3.0 Release

-Fixed a bug that exited when you reloaded

-You now see the few last messages when you die

-Keep dead NPCs bodies option
-Adjusted the shops model as they weren't centered
-Fixed some memory leaks
-NPCs clones no longer appear on map (this had been buggering me for a long time)
-You can no longer sell a weapon/armor to a shop and bring it to < 0 golds
