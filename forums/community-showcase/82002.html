Hello everyone,

Last week(end) I felt like making something a little different, although I have
made something similar once before.

It is kind of an interactive screen-saver. You can either sit and watch, or you
can interact - move the camera,change views.

Run the program FleetSelector0.25.exe which brings up a standard windows GUI allowing
you to set the number of ships (don't go too high!) and an optional number of minutes
to play for and whether to shutdown the PC when it is finished (type 1 into the relevant
box if you wish it do so). Press Start.

Once in the environment the demo will start in the cockpit/on the bridge of one of the ships.
Using the mouse allows you to look around (left/right).
Pressing SPACE bar takes you to an external free-look view. Move the mouse to
look around, press the left mouse button to move forwards, right mouse button to move backwards.
Pressing SPACE again takes you to a chase view. Pressing tab puts you inside of the
current 'chase' ship. Pressing the up/down arrows switches to the next/previous chase ship (both
in external chase view and internal cockpit/bridge view).

As I said, it's not really a game, just something that I made because I wanted to watch
some space battles.


Regarding the media - it's all my own stuff but I don't mind if you use it in something else of your
own.

(oh - particles.png is from particle candy, other than that it is all my own stuff.)

Press ESCAPE to quit.




http://home.swiftdsl.com.au/%7Etmalcolm/tom/fleet/Fleet.zip

(roughly 6MB)
