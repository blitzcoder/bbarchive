This is a quick little riddle game, that will be part of Ping Pong Battle.

Just thought it might be nice though to get some feedback on it, before I stick
it in the game ;)

http://www.mediafire.com/?qoriyr4hzin

The Book of Dao is a riddle based game, where you must answer a riddle on
each page of the book, to gain access to the next page. And so on, until you
complete the book.

Use Up and Down Arrow Keys to turn pages, and ESC to exit!
