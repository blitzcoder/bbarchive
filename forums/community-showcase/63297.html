Without seeing your code, I am going to take a stab in the dark and ask if you are moving Jack-O THEN checking collisions or the other way around. At the slowish speeds he moves, you should be able to avoid most sinking problems using something like this:

If (Jacko.x + Jacko.width + Jacko.xvel >= object.x) Jacko.x = object.x - Jacko.width

If, however, you are first moving Jack-O then doing

If (Jacko.x + Jacko.width >= ...)
^^ The difference being you are checking after the move instead of checking his position+velocity before the move.

That's just one thought on the matter and may not be applicable in this case.
