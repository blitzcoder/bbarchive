Hello all,

I have developed a little game called Pikgem.
You can download it at : http://www.dconcept.info/ftp/pikgem.exe

In this 3d arcade game, player takes control of a little guy that collects gems to complete levels.

It is based on retro concept with actual improvements. The originality : you can take control of vehicles like rocket or skate to collect gems faster. Another point is the very easy control : you won't waste your time to studying complex controls to get some fun.
PikGem is easy to learn and simple to play.

The features are : different game worlds from Tropics to Space with various traps,bonuses, monsters,vehicles and of course easy mouse or keyboard control,a learning mode,enhanced 3D graphics and much more...
Please if you can test it and say me if there are some issues or bugs it could help me.

Thank to all.
