Hello,

We want to introduce our latest game, "The Attack
of Mutant Fruits from Outer Space", an old school shooter game, done with Blitzmax.

The game is freeware and we hope you enjoy it.

ABOUT THE GAME
When Human Beings have been destroyed themselves ... who will replace
them?. Trying to avoid the disaster, The Earth Alliance sent to another
planets the main fruits and vegatables seeds to have an ecological
reserve, but maybe ... is late. The Earth have a new enemy, from outer
space, The Mutant Fruits are crossing the galaxy to conquest our world.

FEATURES:

- 10 worlds.
- 8 melodies
- 12 enemies
- 3 weapons and a laser shield
- Keyboard and Joystick support. (Ready for arcade cabinets)

sswift(Posted 2008) [#6]
The name and graphics reminded me of my own game Attack of the Alien Space Beetles, (Which is still in development) so I decided to try this out.

This has the potential to be really good, but right now it is impossibly hard and takes too long and is too confusing to navigate the menus.

The first thing I'd do is remove the fadeout on the menus, or make it go much faster. (No fade out would feel more responsive and more solid.)

Then I would add the ability to get out of any menu by hitting ESC, and confirm any selection by hitting Enter.

Then I would add mouse support, and put a button to go back and/or confirm in a consistent spot on submenus, down near the bottom.

Then I would make the ship blink and be invincible for a few moments after being destroyed.

And lastly, the enemies need to be slowed down quite a bit at the beginning. I think in order for me to kill all of them consistently on the first level, they'd either have to be like 4x as slow, or move twice as slow, and fire twice as slow. Also, slowing down the bullets couldn't hurt.

But I mean, other than that, it's pretty polished. It could be a really nice little game if I could actually get past the first level.

Hello,

Thank your for your comments, we appreciate it and we'll consider it to improve our games.

About mouse support:
This game was created thinking mainly for Arcade Cabinets, for that reason we used the keyboard as the main game controller. We will consider the mouse option.

MGE - Windows Mode
It's not documented, but pressing F10 from the main menu you can play in windows mode.

GREY ALIEN - Text Input
The game need a minumum of 3 characters to save the name. Use the button 2 to accept the character and button 1 to finish the entry.

ABOUT THE DIFFICULTY
We have made many changes about this. The actual difficulty is based on the experience of our betatester, a total of 16 persons.

We wanted to offer this game as freeware, but in the next months we are going to release a few commercial casual games, and we will consider your suggestions for to do better games.
