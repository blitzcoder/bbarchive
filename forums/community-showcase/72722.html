Picklesworth(Posted 2007) [#10]
That grass works surprisingly well with the dreamy, light feel. I am normally not a fan of 3D grass when it is so sparsely placed, but in this case it is not bad at all.

However, two things:

- It seems to be laid out in a very precise grid pattern. I can see in your second and fourth screenshot that a straight line can be drawn to connect each strand of grass. Try displacing each of the grass sprites slightly, perhaps with a random number, to get more attractive results.

-The grass is all the same colour, and rarely the same colour as the grass texture on the ground beneath it. This makes it seem out of place from the green ground beneath, and means that there is no variety in the grass. It is possible to tint it, using EntityColor, and I believe there are some good examples of that in other peoples' grass systems.
