So far, this is my best achievement with Blitz3D.

I put together almost everything I know about Blitz stuff.

This demo is using
- Lightmaps
- Several texture blend modes (up to 8 textures per surface)
- Normal-maps, Specular-maps
- Real time animated textures (with simple textures and cubeMaps)
- FastExtensions (DepthOfField, LightRays, Reflections, Refractions)
- Up to 4 full scene renders in a single pass with acceptable fps
- FastImage
- FastText (just simple on-screen text)
- PhysX wrapper (all the colision and movement is done with PhysX)
- ... and around 3 to 4 months of work ...

Land of Ozis
The concept is based on a 3D sequel of 'World of Goo' :)
It is just an early prototype but let's say the idea
would be using physics to solve objectives and puzzles... (no new concept, but is addictive :)

Anyway, it was lot of fun to develop this demo
and I have no idea if I will keep working on it ...
... but you can test the demo
