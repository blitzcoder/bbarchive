This isn't a Blitz-made game but I wanted to show it to my favorite group of people. I made it in Unity3D. It's not finished, not by a longshot, but I'm sure I can make quite a few levels with this idea. ;)


The controls are extremely basic and some can be changed in the Input tab. WASD to move and Space to jump. Middle and Right mouse buttons toggle the mouse lock. When you die, press R to respawn.

GIB3D(Posted 2010) [#5]
Thanks Nate. To get past the first barriers you have to slow down by holding S. The fake falling is a side-effect of me setting the player's physics Drag to about 2 or so. The drag slows the player down which is good for moving forward/backward/sideways but it also effects going up and down. I'll see if I can raise the gravity higher tomorrow. Also, when you hit walls and barriers, a few things happen...

1. A sound is played
2. A message shows up in the top left
3. The player's rotation is unlocked allowing it to roll around and you no longer have control over it
