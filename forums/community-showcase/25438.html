Tournment Blitz.com Pay-Per-Use Payment Service. Ideal for MMORPG, Non-Tournament Games, and Applications. A great payment method for users who would like to use software for short duration of time without purchasing the software at its full price only paying for the time they use the software.

The service takes full advantage of Tournament Blitz's Secured PayPal/Token System. The Tournament Blitz SDK Pay-Per-Play (TBSDK[P3]) can be implemented in a variety ways to charge tokens to include Time-Pay, Pay-Per-Save, and others.

Developers earn 75%-85% profit per use. The service authentication system practically eliminates pirating as authentication it required to launch the software.
