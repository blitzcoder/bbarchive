JA2(Posted 2007) [#11]


It's taken a bit longer than I had hoped but I've finished my scarecrow model and a couple of pumpkin lanterns. The scarecrow is fully rigged and ready to animate.

I've also added some random rain and lightning effects with changing intensity and sounds to match.

The gameplay is very simple as I said before. Basically, the scarecrow will hit random pumpkins (like drums) and you have to follow the pattern. It starts with a simple 3 hit combo, which you repeat, then the pattern increases by 1 hit etc. The more pumpkins you have the harder the pattern will be to follow.

Fingers crossed for a demo this week :)
