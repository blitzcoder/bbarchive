Warpy(Posted 2008) [#4]
Cool, but I think you're making too much work for yourself! You keep drawing every item that's been picked up, even after it's well inside the ball, and you perform collision on all of the items, making the movement very laboured, and eventually the framerate will start going down.
The way katamari damacy does it is to use a single collision sphere which grows as you pick stuff up, and only the last few things you picked up are drawn. I think they get away with it by packing things in much closer.
