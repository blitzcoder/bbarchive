Ruz, looks at the overview maps in the bottom right of the screenshots... they get much more interesting! I've written 10 music tracks for the game so there's plenty of music there. To stop coming off the top of the track you have to slow down on the corners!

How to get round corners fast: If you're turning left, get up high on the right hand side bank then cut down and across the corner. Once you get the hang of that you'll get round easier. Also, start steering early. If you're rolling towards the top either ease off the accelerator or indeed hit the brakes if necessary.
