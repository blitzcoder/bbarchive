Hi, I'm happy because, after many years of development, "Tompi Jones" is released. As many of you know, "Tompi Jones" is a platform game in 2.5 dimensions made with Blitz3d.

You can see the HD Youtube Trailer: http://youtu.be/_rx3XYS5cuE

Unfortunately the multiplayer option has been removed. After failing crowdfunding campaign has been impossible to get the necessary technical equipment for testing and finish the multiplayer mode with reasonable quality.

I hope to get enough resources to create "Tompi Jones 2" and include a multiplayer mode.

Thanks to all the blitz community for their support!.

I have also created "DreamsSoftGames Bundle 1" with 3 games to offer a lower price: "Tompi Jones", "Arkhelom 3D" and "Breakout Invaders".
