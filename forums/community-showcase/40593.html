I've started a sci-fi game set in 2015. I've been working on this for quite a while, with the basic plot changing often. I think I like this idea.

You are among the 600 remaining US soldiers in the middle of 2015, the beginning of WWIII. The UK has joined with the US to form "Army Overseas" (AOS), and the resurrected Soviet Union has joined forces with Vietnam, Korea, and the rest of Southeastern Asia to form the Asian Liberation Army (ALA). North Africa has formed the Global Annihilation Unit (GAU), along with the Middle East.

Fight to the death in many places all over the world. Every nation in the world is included in this war, directly or not. Face terrifying betrayals, victorious accomplishments, and humiliating defeats. But tower over all in another generation of World War.
