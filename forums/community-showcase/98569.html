LED Dancer Screensaver (V 1.0.0)

Hello everyone in the blitz community! I am at my random programming hobby again, but this time I am looking to actually complete a project. So, I come to you today to ask if you would like to beta test a screensaver I just finalized to version 1. It uses only Windows native commands, so there is no '.dll' to go with it, just the '.scr' file, and it also has a smart GFX and auto-configuration ability (More info on that below.) It is simple to use, easy to view, and it is free for use forever to all who want to test it. (It is a demo, but that is because I have not made a full featured version yet.) Sorry if this is in the wrong spot, you can move the post if you wish. :)

About:

This screen saver is a collection of LED lights that create complex patterns and designs that are as addicting as they are colorful. There are 10 modes for color, 10 modes for movement, and 10 special effects applied to that, giving you a total of 1000 possible patterns and designs.








Statistics:
-Uses Windows native commands so there is only one file, the screensaver.

-Allows for quick installation. Just download, right click, and install!

-Displays in the preview monitor while choosing a screen saver.

-Smart GFX finds the best resolution for your monitor, then adjusts all screen saver actions and detail.

-Auto-Config keeps track of the FPS and changes the detail as it runs, keeping it at the highest detail your PC can handle without killing frame rate. (Takes about a minuet to adjust.)


Specifications:
-Windows XP or later (SP 3 Recommended)
-3D graphics support via DirectX 7 or higher
-3D support for in gadget display (windowed mode)
-At least 32 bit support

Note: This screensaver supports all monitor sizes, but will pick the maximum size for that monitor. If you are running the desktop lower than your monitor's maximum resolution, you may experience a pause when the screensaver both starts and ends.
