PyraTrapped released

Community Forums/Showcase/PyraTrapped released

OscarBraindeaD(Posted 2011) [#1]
I'm proud to announce that BraindeaD Games has released his second BlitzMax game: PyraTrapped.
You must escape from the pyramid, dodging the falling blocks to reach the exit of each level.
It features physics (through Farseer) and nice torch light effects (with adjustable detail to keep the game performance).
The game has 24 levels in with increasing difficulty.





It's available for Mac on the Mac App Store:

http://itunes.apple.com/us/app/pyratrapped/id475606911?l=es&ls=1&mt=12

And for Windows in Indievania store:

http://beta.indievania.com/games/pyratrapped

www.braindeadgames.net
