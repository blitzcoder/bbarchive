RemiD(Posted April) [#12]
Cool feature for the fans.

You will have to learn about "texture atlas" (putting several textures in one large texture) and merging surfaces which are near each others (like the parts of a building), if you want to prevent slowdowns due to the rendering of many surfaces.
Or the alternative is to use copyentity of a hidden "prefab". (for each part, like you probably do)

(but merging surfaces which share the same material and which are near (the parts of a building) is faster than copyentity anyway)
