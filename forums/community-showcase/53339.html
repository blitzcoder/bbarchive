For those who didn't get to play this yet... here is - "Snowball Scramble".



A simple festive fun (Christmas themed) arcade action style game for 1 or 2 players (split screen) and three modes of play. FREE demo version available now. Registered users (£5.00) get no 'nag' screen and high scores and you can have a name appear in the game (if it were a gift for a child for example).



Features:
Select a character from 8 in built.
Cursor keys or joypad for player 1.
WASD for Player 2.
Select from 10 upto 80 parcels to collect from the magic forest.
Midi music, sound effects, and festive vocals.
Particles galore!
Collect and deliver parcels from the magic forest to the giant Christmas Tree in order to score. Can you beat your opponent to the last parcel? Steal parcels from your opponent, grab powerups for energy bursts and extra scores.
The more you carry the slower you move so beware!
Against an opponent or the clock.



The FREE demo version is available now from:
www.smartscreenuk.com/Snowball_SmartScreen_2005.htm
