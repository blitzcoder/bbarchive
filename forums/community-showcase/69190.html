I updated my post - its actually 15-minute sessions, not 20 minutes. I originally had it at 20 but the general opinion was that it was too long, given that the number of sessions is unlimited.

I don't consider that its crippled. The player has full access to 'Classic' game mode. The other two game modes are expansions of that. How far you can get in the demo depends on how fast you play - other than the 15 minute time limit there is no restriction on how far you can get.

I've also made the difficulty curve a little steeper in the demo, to give players an impression of the whole game, rather than just the start of it. You'll see things in the first few demo levels that you would not see in the same timeframe from playing the full game.

Reasoning? Oh God....
