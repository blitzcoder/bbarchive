Luis de la Cruz(Posted 2010) [#1]
After several months of progress in its development, Arkhelom 3D is nearing completion. In a few weeks I have prepared a demo version and the game will be for sale. I'm looking for distributors for the game.

Although initially the game was going to be a system of free megaupload downloads, I'm finally going to market Arkehlom 3d with the traditional system of sales, with competitive pricing and trying to reach people through a distributor.

Anyway, in addition to the game, periodically I will be putting free content for the game through the website www.dreamssoftgames.com, like new levels, ships or loops for the game.

I added two new items:

- Time Stop: slow down for a few seconds allowing time to escape difficult situations.
- Capture Ray: can convert enemy ships that shoot at allied ships with our craft and improve their destructive power. Each ship will inherit the characteristics and type of shooting the enemy ship captured.
