Sup everyone! You guys may have saw my old Space Invader Clone (2D Version) if not here is the link: http://www.blitzbasic.com/Community/posts.php?topic=35994

Anyways a guy named Yavin told me for practice it'd be a great idea to make a Space Invader game using 3d commands this time... So i took that into consideration and decided to pull it off... Of course i needed help because i had a lot of errors (i need to work on my debugging skills badly) anyways, after all that hustle and bustle i finall made it... Here it is guys Space Invaders 3D

Screen Shots:







In this game you are trying your hardest to save the planet earth from these vicious invaders... You don't have much time since they decide to attack you travelling at light speed... This mission is either a do or die, don't get hit once or you die and all hope has been lost... Don't let more than 5 invaders get passed you... It only takes 5 of these ships to blow up an entire planet, and unfortuneatly planet earth is in their way... Good Luck on saving us, your our last hope...

Controls: Arrow Keys to Move: Up, Down, Left, Right
Fire: Right Control

Download Game: http://www.nuclearfusionpc.com/razor/SP3D.zip

I hope you all Enjoy My first ever 3D game in Blitz3D!!!
