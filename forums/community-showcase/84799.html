Greetings everyone!

I have finally finished my very first 3D game.
I began this project some time ago shortly after I bought
Blitz3D but then dropped it when I got stuck :(

I recently picked up work on this game, and completly
finished it in about a week.
Expanding considerably on the original concept as well.
This was originally going to be just a remake of one of my
earliest blitz games, simply in 3D, but has since become quite
different from the original.

The goal of the game, is to move around trying to collect all of
the dots, while avoiding the enemy robots.
After collecting all of the dots, you get to fight a boss character.
You can also pick up Powerups to give you special abilities for
a limited time.

Heres some screenshots, and I hope you enjoy the game!







Download it at
http://hosted.filefront.com/werdnaworld

Use the arrow keys to move, spacebar to strafe while moving,
'A' to shoot, 'P' to pause, 'J' to take a screenshot, Enter to change
the view, and finally ESC to exit.
