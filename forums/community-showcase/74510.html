Currently just a preview (some features currently don't work - for example, Arcade mode isn't fully implemented), but Spots is available for playing.

Its a multiplayer game (for up to 4 humans or AI), where you have to remove your opponents spots by moving one or two spaces - moving one and you clone the original spot. Move two and you transport it to the new place.



Its based on an Acorn Archimedes game of the same name, written by Patrick Hinrichs, and uses some of the original graphics.
