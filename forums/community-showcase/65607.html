Speedup the characters : talking about the NPCs ? Yeah maybe.

Underwater effects : actually there is a bloom effet and a distortion of the view (by simply scaling the camera) but it's true that it isn't very visible in the video, adding fog seems a good option.

For the monsters, some of them are slow (the fire-golems for example, those you see most in the trailer) some of them are not :) (the flying ones are quiet hard to beat especially when in group) and even if the fire golems are slow, you have to move fast to beat them as they have to ability to block fury attacks. For the "pokemon stuffs" these are really old and I'm no more implied in it, I was 15 when I did these but as peoples keep enjoying it, I keep offering it.
