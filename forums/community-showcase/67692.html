Wu Hing is a strategy board game based on the five Chinese elements of wood, fire, earth, metal and water. The game pits two players (yin and yang) against each other, with the objective to create, destroy and transform the elements in play.

There are four different game styles to choose from and you can play against a friend (hotseat or network) or the computer with four different difficulty settings.

Please visit www.kudosgames.com for more information or download the demo from www.kudosgames.com/downloads/WuHingDemo_setup.exe
