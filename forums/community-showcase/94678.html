Hey guys,

Last weekend I entered the Ludum Dare #20 competition (write a game in 48 hours.) The theme this time was "It's dangerous to go alone, take this!" and I came up with a game called "Fish With Weapon"

In the game you end up bashing aquatic creatures with a mallet in an attempt to save your friends and defeat the evil "Shork".

It was a great experience writing it and I learnt quite a bit about myself as a developer (eg, what I can force myself to do with a proper deadline ahead of me!) Although the game was "finished" there are still quite a few problems with it. Obviously it's quite (very) rough round the edges, you can't die yet and there are nasty collision issues. but overall I was quite proud of it.
