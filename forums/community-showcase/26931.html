Well, with the closing of Binary-People, I think I've become a more professional coder. The look of my site has changed alot, from looking community-oriented to sales-oriented (even if I'm not good at making it pretty). It's not fun, but I think it's necessary.

Anyway, something I've been working on lately is called Cubix3D. It's a 3D map editor where you use cubes to build maps. You can drag the points of these cubes about however you require. By using multiple cubes, you can create almost any shape you need. Like this:

[img]http://storm.binary-people.com/projects/cubix/snap/example02.jpg[/img]

So, I've just released the shareware version, and I'm hoping to get people to download it. You can get to the shareware version by going to http://storm.binary-people.com/projects/cubix/index.html . Give it a shot, and let me know what you think.
