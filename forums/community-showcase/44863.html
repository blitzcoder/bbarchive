Greeitngs, I promised this little demo some time ago and just got around to releasing it.. It's by no means complete or optimized but it does utilize a number of blitz3d tools and resources.. I thought it'd be interesting to throw all these fines works together into a single demo.

This demo uses:

Blitztree3d - for trees
TreemagikG2 - for distance trees
Plantlife - grass and foliage modeling
ALE - for conjunction terrains with LOD engine
Cshop4 - light building modeling
Giles - structual lightmapping
Bryce - skybox rendering
Particle Candy - General particle effects
BlitzTOOLui - Editor gui routines
PaintShopPro - tacky texture editing
Psonic - player model
Tranz3d - modeling



There's alot of things that could be done to better this demo but for times-sake im releasing it as is because i no longer plan to work on it..


Hold down the mouse LMB and RMB for mouselook and movement - swivel mouse for lookaround.
download (12mb): http://www.aliencodec.com/temp/ademo1.zip
