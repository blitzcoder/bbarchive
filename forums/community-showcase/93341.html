xlsior(Posted 2011) [#76]
Could anyone give me tip on how get passed level 2?


- Try to get additional salvage yards as fast as you can, or you'll run out of money early on.
- When creating new fighters, I find it often pays to wait a while and then create a bunch all at once -- that way they progress in a tight group, and have much better luck killing incoming zombies together
- Make sure to use your 'yee-haw' specials when they are available
- After maxing out your salvage yards and trailers, try to save up some money for the other shacks, which allow you to build stronger units like the angry farmers, which kill from a distance and thereby can kill more zombies before they get killed themselves.
