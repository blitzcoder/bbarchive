I recently found an old post where I said I was going to make the opposite of asteroids.

Here's the beginnings of an attempt at that.
There are some asteroids, which I suppose the player will eventually control somehow, and some spaceships, which need to be controlled by the CPU. Thanks to some clever maths, I've made them quite skilful at navigating around the asteroids.

What would an asteroid do about the dudes in the spaceships if it could? I suppose the opposite of the player breaking up the asteroids is the asteroids joining together, so maybe that's how this should work?
