This is a game I made for the last Shmup-Dev shootemup contest. It tied for 11th place. Although I made it a while ago, I never took the time to post about it here until now.

You are the candy cane, and you must kill all of the other Christmas objects to proceed to the next level. Use WASD to move, and click to fire in the direction of the mouse. Gather the presents that appear on the screen to get upgrades.




All feedback is welcome.
