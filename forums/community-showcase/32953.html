Rob Farley(Posted 2004) [#19]
Ronnie,

Here's the low down on the terrain:
It's a 256x256 heightmap with a 512x512 texture map on the top.
The texture map is 100% procedual textured (texture map, light map and shadow map) then as the objects are placed onto the terrain additional shadows are written to the texture map.
This texture map is streched over the whole terrain and there is a 'noise' texture repeated over the whole lot to give more detail.
The texturing is done with my new version of terrain shader which I should have finished at some point soon.
