King Dave(Posted 2005) [#12]
Thanks for the compliments :)

Currently we have about 12 people on the team, although only about 5 of us are active on a daily basis (e.g. we have music and sound guys who will do work occasionally when we need it, not everyday).

Third party tools... well, I basicly don't do anything outside of coding and the website (I'm useless with anything media wise), so I'm not 100% sure what the other members have been using.
I know some of our more recent dungeons have been made using Maplet, apart from that I haven't got a clue! lol

I originally started with a 2D version but that only lasted about a month in roughly October 2001. So the version you see now has taken a bit over 3 years so far (note that all of us are doing this in our spare time, not full time).
Over those 3 years we've made:
+128,232 lines of code (3.63MB - game, patcher, server, editors, etc)
+3,888 items (armour, food, weapons, spells, etc)
+148 characters (including playable, monsters & npcs)
+62 maps

I can't wait till this is done, lol
