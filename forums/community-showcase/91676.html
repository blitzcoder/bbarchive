Be warned!

This is the kind of game that comes about when it's a long, boring day at work,
with 0 customers and nothing to do. My brain was slowly frying in the extreme
heat, and thus the idea for this bizarre little game came about!
(It only took me 2 days to make it)



This is an RTS game(Sort of) where the main brunt of the strategy is in upgrading
your troops in between fights. I actually had a blast play testing this, lol.

Use ESC to Exit, any key to skip through text scenes, and Left and Right Arrow
Keys to move your army left and right(Not that you often have to).


Have fun ;)
