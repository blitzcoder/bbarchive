After 2 months of waiting for it to be released, I was emailed Tyler about every month or so asking when... no idea why it took so long... apart from "isnt a typical release on our site":

They bump games they consider not to suit their target audience for games more in line with their "key demographics". Since they get a LOT of casual game submissions, this means that games like this - which with all due respect are not casual games in the sense that BFG uses the phrase - can keep being bumped because new games keep queue-jumping above them.

They did this with Kick Shot Pool. I think Kick Shot Pool - which also was not a casual game in the sense that BFG uses the phrase - took about five or six weeks, but that was a long time ago and a lot of BFG's rivals have shut up shop or died on their arse since then, so BFG are likely getting a lot more submissions now. They're certainly more fixated on their core audience than ever.
