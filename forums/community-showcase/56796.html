Liberation Games is proud to announce our latest game Kingdom Elemental!

Kingdom Elemental takes the winning combination of exciting tactical action and adds in a multitude of exciting features including a dazzling, vibrant 3d world, tons of characters, monsters, bosses and a level of customizability not seen in other tactical games.

The game will feature:

-Over 40 waves of fast-paced, action-packed tactical combat!
-Dazzling spells, characters, and special fx!
-Easy to learn interface has you fighting battles in minutes!
-Fully-integrated tutorial teaches you everything you need to know.
-Addictive unlocking systems keeps you coming back for more!
-Skirmish mode for unlimited gameplay!
-4 vibrant worlds, full of life and special effects!
-14 unique heroes, including: Rogues, Beserkers, Milittia and more!
-14 unique monsters including: Zombies, Ogres, Kobalds and more!
-A gaint, flaming bear!









The game is being published by Chronic Logic (www.chroniclogic.com) creators of the popular game Gish. Kingdom Elemental is scheduled for release early May.

For more information including unit biographies, and storyline check out www.liberationgames.com

All questions and comments are welcome! :)
