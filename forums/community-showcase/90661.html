I was at work the other day and had a wierd idea ... pencil drawn games on plain old notebook paper! So I created a new series for H+Soft Arcade - Paper Games Series. I plan on doing a few different retro type games for this series.

Paper Games Vol. 1 - Pencil BiPlane Battle


This is a little retro shoot 'em up, shoot the enemy planes on each stage while avoiding the mountains and hot air balloons. Find and shoot the blimp on each stage for bonus points.
