I'm working on Aquablox, a tetris clone using 3D for visual appeal with a little twist. Every time a line is completed, the glass cubes shatter and water is released from the orbs held inside. Harvey the fish is stuck above the water and you only have a certain amount of time to get him back in. So, you have to release a certain amount of lines in a certain amount of time to raise the water level over his head. Take a look at the screenshots and download the one level demo and tell me what you think!
