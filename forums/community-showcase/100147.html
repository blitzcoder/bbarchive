Luis de la Cruz(Posted 2013) [#1]
Hi, blitz community:

Today DreamsSoftGames presents a new Blitz3D project: "Breakout Invaders".

“Breakout Invaders” is a funny fusion between two game genres: "Breakout" and "Space Invaders". Play through 100 variety levels, with a lot of original game styles. You can enjoy destroying enemy ships and blocks structures like castles, temples, towers, cities,... You can customize and improve your ships, collect the enhancers items, fight again final bosses and much more.

Oberon Media has published the game, which is already present in the games portals of Iplay, Yahoo or Msn and soon others like Big Fish Games.

We have had to discontinue development of our game "Tompi Jones" for a few months, to try to get funding with "Breakout Invaders".

After completing the sale of "IFK2" (Indie Face Kick 2 Bundle: "Be in the game" in May 2012) with our games "Tompi Jones" and "Arkhelom 3D", we have not yet cashed nothing. It was all a fraud and any developer has received anything for copies sold.

We hope to have more luck with "Breakout Invaders" in casual gamers portals.
