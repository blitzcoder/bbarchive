Filax(Posted 2004) [#20]
i have made this scene with my new tool TerraED :)

http://www.blitz3dfr.com/tempo/TerraEd2.0.zip

With this tool , you can make easily terrain and paste any
object on your terrain ! like house, trees, plants etc
generate forrest and bush.

next you load all your level (terrain, object, with all
parameters) with one line of code like :

TER_LoadTerraEdMap("Myscene.ted")

and , that all :) you can use you level for demo, fps ...

The rendering method made by eole for drawing objects is
very optimised, you can have 3000 objects on your terrain
with a good FPS rating. (we draw only what the player see)

Good bye LoadTerrain and position entity :)

I think post the first release in one week.
