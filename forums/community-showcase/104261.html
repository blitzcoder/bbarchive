Yasha(Posted 2015) [#5]
You should read this and this, if you haven't already.

What colour Mars is is a difficult question to give a straight answer to. What colour you should use probably depends on what you want to represent - as the second article says, a human on Mars would probably find their vision ended up recalibrating for the different sunlight, and end up seeing something quite different from what a robot-mimicing-human-vision would see, or from what a newcomer or Earth-based viewer would see. So if your simulation is of a remotely-controlled rover, your current balance is probably OK, but if it's of human astronauts landing on the planet, it should probably be more blue.
