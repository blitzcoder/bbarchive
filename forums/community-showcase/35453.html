RE: Challenge Mode Helpers : That's not a bad idea, but there's a big drawback. If the way I solve the challenge is not the easiest, I may actually be hindering more than I'm helping if I make the helper show the method I used. Most of the challenges, I'm quite certain, can be beaten in multiple ways. Plus it doesn't really address the bigger problem, which is that they're just too hard. I'm toying with the idea of making the challenge game playable in Arcade mode as well as Sim. The extra speed that arcade mode generates, coupled with the ability to steer the ball after release ought to make the levels a bit easier, without reducing the difficulty of the challenge itself.

RE Difficulty : Hard to say. I've also had a couple of complaints that it was too hard. It's basically dependant upon how fast the bars move, at least for control method #1, and i was using a faster speed, more difficult setting before I released the beta. I figured the fact that I'd been playing it for four months straight had probably given me a skewed perspective on the difficulty so I easied it up a bit. It's trivial to make it harder if the general thrust is that it's too easy.

BTW, the CPU players on the hard difficulty setting regularly score around 230 and are capable of scoring 300. They also have some simple learning AI in there, so technically they'll keep getting better if you play well.
