Boiled Sweets(Posted 2005) [#3]
The galaxy is made up of...

2 huge spheres with 2 star textures with different scaling and half alpha'd so the tiny background stars kinda twinkle.

Then I have a 'nebula' bitmap that is applied on top.

Then I have an array of galaxies (the blue spirally things). I have added red ones too now and have angled then and I spin then gently too as they drift about.
See 3rd screenshot above!

Then have a star array for the bigger stars.

Then I have an array of asteroid entities that spin / rotate.

I then have a comet particle effect.

And a sun particles effect.

I'm gonna add intermittant shooting stars too!

This is just the background for the game!!!

They were done in PhotoShop 7
