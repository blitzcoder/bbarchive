u2o(Posted 2007) [#1]
Have a look at my first attempt at a game using Blitz Max. It's a little pointless, but was a good learning curve to figure out Blitz Max. Some Blitz Max do's and don’ts, what works good and also some things that are not so great (ViewPorts! (But I found a great workaround!!!))




The idea of the game is to guide your crystal around the arena and avoid hitting the other balls, pick up the powerups / powerdowns and survive for as long as possible.

I am sure there are many things I could do to make the game better, such as high scores (even net scores) but maybe in an update? This game I feel was my intro to Blitz Max.

Have a play and let me know if you find any bugs. Suggestions all taken on board for when and if I do an update.

Download it here: http://www.plusnetu2orange.plus.com/blitzmax/Boing.zip

Now... on to my next game! :)
