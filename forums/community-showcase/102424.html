Hello,
I have published my first game, "3 seasons" in my website
http://www.blue-bit-entertainment.com/

The game reminds of Columns: The objective is to get at least 3 blocks of same color horizontally, vertically or diagonally in a row. Though in "3 seasons" there are both horizontal and vertical bricks and the size of them varies and there can be many collapsing sequences, before the next brick appears and there are some bonus blocks, too.

The game has background themes from 3 seasons, autumn, summer and winter, hence the name.

The game itself is coded in Blitz3D, the startup menu of the demo version is coded in BlitzMax.
