Ok, i downloaded this and had a shot. It's a decent effort, but it seems like it's built for you personally. The controls are very strange. You should allow the camera to be moved whilst your moving the mesh, or have a key or mouse button you can hold, to temp move the camera.

If all of the issues are addressed above, this could become a good tool. World editors aren't easy to build, so keep it up man :o)
