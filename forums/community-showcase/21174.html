Hi Rob - no need to let you know what my comments are, as you already know that I think it's great ;o)

Zawran - there are two main ways of selling your game from your site. The first is via a publisher, such as Idigicon or Binary People, and the other is via online distributors like BMT Micro, ShareIt and others of the ilk. There is no short answer and some investigation into which is more suitable for yourself is needed. You'll find plenty of threads on the subject here and at BlitzCoder if you do a quick search, but ultimately be prepared to do some research first - both in terms of where and the sorts of deals you're looking for.

Defender is always a tricky one, cos people who played the original have a fairly set idea of how it should be.
