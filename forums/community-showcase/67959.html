Desktop Forest Screensaver

My biggest screensaver yet, and biggest finished blitz project.

Features
- 4 Seasons
- 4 Times of day (Morning, Midday, Dusk, and Night)
- Squirrels, butterflies, and fireflies (At night only)
- Seasons and times can be based off of your PC clock. So the first day of spring at 9 PM your screen will be dark with spring trees.
- Weather effects Rain, Thunderstorms, and snow able to turn on or off, or set frequency.
- Display directly on your desktop or an image
- Ambient forest sounds depending on the season and time of day.
- Set the overall number of trees, quantitiy of grass, rocks, butterflies, squirrels, and fireflies.
- Set the speed at which the trees grow.
- Set the overall detail and resolution to get the best performance possible.

Made with Blitz 3D and Visual Basic.
