RifRaf(Posted 2007) [#1]
Hi,
Im not really trying to show this off as much as I am looking for artistic help. Im working in a mesh creation system based on blocks and dropped scenery. Blocks vary in shape, and you can specify the textures to use per side ect. Entities are optimized in real time to allow you to place far more in the scene than you otherwise could, faces that are not seen are removed immediatly, and are not exported with the final model(s). Lighting is very simple, so far its done on a per block basis, no real lightmap creation. Scenes can vary in size from 8x8x8 to 64x64x8. Everything is a snap to grid type of flow. Scaling Placing ect.


You can save raw data to recreate at project runtime, or export a multisurface mesh. This creates two models for you one primary, and one with scenery you have flagged to not have collisions in game. You can also drop data points wich can have any text you want assiciated with them for custom effects and things to be parsed at level load.

The help i need is with textures and low poly scenery objects that fit the basic "toon" appearance of the shot below. I would also like a few people to test it and give me critical feedback as to its interface and ease of use ect. This would be a pretty thankless position I cant pay someone to give me feedback , but I can pay an artist if any are interested and have the talent to put together some good model and texture packs for me.
