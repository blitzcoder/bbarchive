Hi,

In my spare time I'd been flying model RC Helicopters quite bit and thought it would be fun to make a little game using dual analog sticks on a pc game pad based on the control sticks of the transmitter. This game is a two player VS. game where you activate (remove) all your landing pads before your opponent does. Meanwhile, avoiding landing in pits(open spaces), your opponents landing pads, walls and your opponents missiles. Let your shadows be your guide while flying and shooting - this determines actual locations for collisions. You can play with one game pad (1 player) but is not nearly as fun.

Use your left analog stick to control altitude and rudder (up/down and left/right spin). Use the right analog stick to control the elevator and aileron (fwd/back/left/right movement). use buttons 1, L1 or R1 to fire rockets. If your controller has an analog button make sure it's turned on or the controls won't be correct!
