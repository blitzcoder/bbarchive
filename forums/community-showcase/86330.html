Sorry I keep being the one to post here, I'm surprised no one else has tried it.

After playing the game my suggestion is to really sit down and think about what you're trying to accomplish in this game gameplay wise.

Obviously this game has Arcade style aspects; the high score, bosses, levels, powerups and a bit repetitive gameplay. This is fine, but for your game to really be appreciated today, it needs to really look stellar. Particle effects, stereo sound, etc. In todays gaming world with short attention spans, these are crucial.

However, you may be trying to create a more Action/Adventure style game. In this case, you need to add more to the game than just collecting balls. Some sort of in depth storyline, puzzle solving, or a strategic aspect. Perhaps rather than a ton of enemies, have fewer, stronger, and above all else, smarter enemies. Work on solid AI that requires the player to think about how to conquer enemies.

In short, your game is in a No Mans Land when it comes to genre, and I think this should be sorted out so you can "fill out" the game gameplay wise.

I hope that makes sense.
