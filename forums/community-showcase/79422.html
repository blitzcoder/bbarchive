This is my latest retro game coming soon to H+Soft Arcade - Jack-o's Revenge Pinball.
Some may remember a couple years back when I had released the original platformer Jack-o's Revenge (which is included in the H+Soft Arcade Demo).
This game is not a sequel to the original but one of several spinoffs with Jack-o. I have 2 'actual' sequels planned for the original game which will come later.
Here are some screen shots >>


Collect magic and unlock the 'Bonus' boards, Item boards and Battle boards from the Main play board.


The 'Battle Board' Destroy the crystal balls and all the witch's 'pets' to escape!


The 'Bonus Board' (1 of 10) Bash the blocks to hit the '?' boxes fors bonus points and magic.


The 'Magic Item Board' (1-5) Jack-o's about to grab the scissors - One of the five items needed to reverse the witch's spell!


This game (written in B-Max) will be available (fingers crossed) in one to two weeks for H+Soft Arcade.
