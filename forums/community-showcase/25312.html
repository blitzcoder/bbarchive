Hi

Harmony blocks is just about done so I have decided to post a demo for you guys to play

http://www.fire-bat-software.co.uk/hb_demo.zip [5 meg]

Instructions:
Its like Mahjongg / Shanghai solitaire. Match 2 blocks that are the same, they must both have a free side (left/right) and have no blocks on top of them.

Keys:

TAB - skip music track
Hold Right Mouse BTN to rotate the camera

If you want music, stick some mp3s in the music folder.

Still a few things to do, but It is basically finished.

Kinda funky that its finished on my Birthday =)
