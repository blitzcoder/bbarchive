As BlitzCoder's showcase system keeps deleting my entry, I will put this here for people to see, and hopefully enjoy.

My latest game is out as of several weeks ago, but there is an updated version as of a couple of days ago. lol

TransVersion EX is a trippy collect-em-up where you have to grab all the 80s retro items off the screen while avoiding being shot and going for the highest points multipliers and also trying to attain survival bonuses and dropzone bonuses.

Give it a go, its under a meg and I've had a lot of positive comments about it - including getting a post from the author of the original Spectrum version on my forum, which I was dead chuffed with.

If you plan to try and get on the online scoreboard, you have your work cut out for you, and should not be attempted by those of a nervous disposition.

Watch out for level 8 and level 10 as they are the ones that people seem to have the most trouble with.

There are 20 levels, and after that they loop and the game gets faster, but also each item collected earns you more points.

Enjoy and let me know what you think. :)

It's on my site, goto the projects section. Cheers.
