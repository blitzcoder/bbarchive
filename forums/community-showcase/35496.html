I have written a fighting game in Blitz 2D. It is called "Machine Gun Karate". Here's a picture:



And here's another picture!



The controls are a bit like International Karate. The music is by Nebula (that's his Blitzcoder name), the sound effects were created by me using a microphone. I plan to add some more features in the future, like more sound effects and perhaps background animations like in IK+. Adding defensive moves might make it more fun to play too.

The game can be downloaded from the Blitzcoder showcase here, or directly here (approximately 3 Mb).

Instructions are currently in the README file. If you don't want to read it I can at least tell you that to turn around you have to press just the space bar. Other moves are performed using the arrow keys, and using the arrow keys plus the space bar. You can make an opponent go KO by hitting them quickly 3 times, and the goal of the game is to score three KOs.
