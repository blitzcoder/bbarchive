With internet tank games appearing to be all the rage recently (apparently), may I present to you Tanks Online. To my shock and astonishment it's featured on page 98 of this month's Retro Gamer magazine. It's a top-down multiplayer tank game along the lines of Combat (Atari) and Tanks (Wii Play, Nintendo Wii). Note that this game is and will remain freeware.

The current version (0.2) is far from complete but is fully playable (and hopefully fun) and has been stable on my own tests. There is no centralised management of active servers yet, so you'll need friends, one of whom will need to host the game. Everyone else then connects to his/her IP address. More details are included in the readme file.

Download it from http://www.bankie.com/games/tanks/tanksonline.php

Please let me know of any problems or general comments.
