Mark1nc(Posted 2004) [#14]
I'm using an isometric map (200X200)
Each 'cell' has a base height and one of 256 tops that have individual corner heights.

The top block image and corner heights are totally changeable in the editor.

On top of that, there are fixed shadow blocks (I was toying with the idea of having the shadows cast on top of moving objects - but it was too much work and too cpu intensive)

There are also tiles for ice, goals and tiles for the shadows on walls.


The game is 2 player.
I can't get 2nd mouse to work on W2K or WXP, but it should work on WinMe and W98.
It functions like the original - if you fall behind, you get transported to where the other player is (incurring a time penalty) - but unlike the original, mine scrolls horizontally too.

I'm still working out some sfx. There are about 60 sfx in so far.
