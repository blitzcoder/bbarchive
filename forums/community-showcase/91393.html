Sung(Posted 2010) [#18]
Current feedback and what will be changed/added:

So far we've got already some nice feedback/balancing which I want to summarize here.

Movement

* Submarine can move "through" other ships (just dive)
* Destroyer will move in 8 directions (additionally diagonal)
* Battleship and heavy Cruiser will move only 1 field per turn
* Light cruiser will get an attack range of 1
* Patrol boat will get a move range of 3


Damage modifications

The following attack combinations will make 100% damage:

Carrier against destroyer, heavy cruiser, light cruiser
Battleship against carrier, battleship, heavy cruiser and light cruiser
Heavy cruiser against light cruiser, battleship, carrier and destroyer
Light cruiser against destroyer and patrol boat
Destroyer against submarine and patrol boat
Submarine against carrier, battleship, heavy cruiser and light cruiser
Patrol boat against submarine and carrier

All other constellations have a modifier of -50% for attacking.

Please feel free to comment this and/or add own ideas and feedback, it's very appreciated!

WERDNA(Posted 2010) [#24]
Just played it.

Very promising indeed!

Story 1\10
Currently lacks Story.

Graphics 8\10
Very good graphics!
Looks like something you'd see in a commercial game, although I had some difficulty
in telling the units apart. They need to look a bit more different from one another, but
overall very nicely done!

Sound 7\10
Not a lot of sound in there yet, but what's in there is solid stuff. Excellent explosion
sounds :)

Gameplay 7\10
Pretty fun. Kind of like a rock paper scissors type thing, in attacking the right units
so you deal 100% damage. The units are diverse enough so they all have a purpose,
and I can really see this game being quite fun to play once you add some scenarios.
I'd probably not include a way to build more units, since that would totally change
the game mechanics. Just have it like it is now, with different sets of ships for different
scenarios :)

The only thing that I found annoying, was that the computer player seemed to always
get the first attack ;(
No matter how I moved he'd always be just out of reach by one square, forcing me
to move into his range if I wanted to start combat. Not sure what you could do
about that one though, lol.


Keep up the good work!
