is there going to be any movement inside the 'pit when you're controlling the ship around, or is it glued to the camera?

Nope its not glued to the camera. Its rocks and roll, and shudders when you get hit. There are also two other view modes (Cockpit, No cockpit and outside view).


Joke aside, there going to be any joystick support?

I really hope I can get this working nicely. I bought a game pad just to test it, and I have also bought the Force Feedback plugin to add in. I did some tests a few weeks ago and work nicely. If you get hit buy the enemy my gamepad rummbled, and hit a tower you get a different effect. So all going well and after a few tweaks it should include a joystick routine. However I very much doubt I will include a digital joystick or keyboard routine as the it really doesnt suit this game.
