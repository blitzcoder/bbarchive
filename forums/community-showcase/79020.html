Game aim: Do wheelies, stoppies and burnouts with a motorcycle to get
as many points as you can. To make wheelies, accelerate and use clutch
and rearbrake to control your angle. To make a stoppie, simply press front
brake. To make burnouts, press front brake and accelerate.

Game modes: 2 minute time attack, free roam and online multiplayer.
-2 bikes
-4 maps
-7 driving positions/tricks
