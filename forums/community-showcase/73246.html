Hi I've just re-released the Demo version of NeoFlip my first (commercial) game, please feel free to give it a try and let me know what you think?

How to play:
1. Remove all the blocks to complete the level!

2. Single click the block you want to move then click on where you want it to go!

3. Or click and drag the block where you want it to go, note you can drag a chain or snake of blocks at once!

4. Connect 3 or more blocks showing the same colour to remove them from the grid!

5. Watch out though if you are left with only 1 or 2 blocks it's game over!



Keys:
Mouse to select and move blocks, click-select click-move or drag
[Escape] - Exit Game
[M] - Mutes Music
[+] - Increase Volume
[-] - Decrease Volume
