I have put a few finishing touches to my game Saucer Attack, made in BlitzMax.

It can be downloaded here: saucerattack.zip (1.1 MB, for Windows)

Sounds were created using DrPetter's SFXR.

The aim of the game is to shoot the enemy flying saucers. They will also fire at you but only when they are colored red (at which point they'll start rotating their guns towards you). After a while they will turn green again. There are two bonus objects that may drop from destroyed enemies: a health bonus and a fast fire bonus. Collisions between saucers are not dangerous (a well known fact), only the bullets will hurt you.

Controls are: arrow keys, space (or Z), and Escape to quit.



Thanks for having a look!
