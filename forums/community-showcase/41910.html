Hi Blitzers,

finally completet my first game written in Blitz Max and my first game written for the Mac. (Windows version is compiled, too but I'll have to wait for the compiler to be released, before I upload it)

Blocks! is the sequel to Block Out!, I released for Windows a year ago. It's a nice litte addictive puzzle game (what else ;)).

The goal is to erase as many blocks as possible from the board. You can erase blocks with same color in vertical and/or horizontal row (at least two of the same). Use the questionmark jokers to recolor some blocks around them.

Blocks! offers two game modes (classic and nick of time (against the clock)), different blockstyles and backgrounds, a funky main title, sounds and two highscores for the two game modes.



Try it, it is real fun... and of course free!
