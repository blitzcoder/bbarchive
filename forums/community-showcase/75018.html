Here's a new demo of my 2d physics based game. I'd appreciate it if you could give it a go. Same venue as before. No goal as yet although you do win if you're the last team standing.

http://www.steviegoodwin.plus.com/TD3.zip

Controls are in read me. I've speeded it up a bit, improved the friction and most physics aspects. I'm going to scrap the idea of worms gameplay and opt for a single player .. kill everything within a time limit, collect things within a limit + a few more good ideas.

It takes a while to master but if you hold kick you can get the guy to do rolly polly's or spin if in the air. Great for high dives.

I've had great fun playing around with it. Please let me know how you find it.
