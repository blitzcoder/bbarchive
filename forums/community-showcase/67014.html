Started this on Saturday after my friend STompy ripped the graphics for me. Thought it would be a nice simple easy game to get a bit of coding practice on.

Originally a PD game for the Atari ST by John Gymer, it's a very simple collect-em-up probably more suitable for children.

Avoid the baddies and collect the eggs (and fruit if you are quick enough).

Hopefully it's pretty much bug free, but less than a week ago it didn't even exist, so who knows if there is anything nasty lurking in there.
