Graphics are lovely. General look of the game is great.

Music is utterly dreadful... never ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever ever use a keyboard to do a guitar solo, it just sounds like crap. Use a guitar.

Gameplay is dull, you get no feel of driving the car, no feel of sliding round corners. No physics when you bump of sides or mines or other players.

When you're going the wrong way it doesn't tell you.

Too many keyboard controls, I would have select weapon and fire or something. For an arcade driving game there are just too many controls.

I think it's got great promise, presentation is great, but you've really got to do something about the gameplay, it's just not exciting.
