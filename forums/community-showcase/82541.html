Hey everyone!

I'd like to show off my latest blitzmax project here.
The game is called "ChaosSpace 2" and you do right if you wonder about the "2". Yes, there was ChaosSpace 1, but it wasn't worth being released in the english community (Yepp, I'm from Germany ;) ).

Some screenshots first (click on them to enlarge):


About:
ChaosSpace 2 is a spaceshooter mainly based on action and fast reactions. You controle a little spaceship in a 2D World and fight against hundreds of enemies, which assail you in any formation and try to kill you by collision.
There are overall 13 different types of them, each with unique properties. Your weapon is upgradable (e.g. 2,3 or max. 4 shots at the same time).
The scripts use an own language (unfortunately there is no tutroial on how to make them on your own. if there is enough feedback with "omg lolz plz make teh fuckin tutorial! ;)" I'll think about this.
If you figured it out on yourself, you can upload it on www.chaosspace.de (scripts). The Script then will be shown in the online section. Playing "online" means to play a whole script to save the score in the highscore afterwards. In training mode you can disable/enable levels by clicking on them.

Powerups:
red: 1,2,3,4 shoots.
green: the shot flies through 1,2,3,4 enemies.
blue: activates shield
purple: explosion around you.

The Controls are easy:
The ship follows your mouse. You can shoot by pressing the left mousebutton.

Hope you like it!

Download here:
www.chaosspace.de
To get english language, just click on "Optionen -> Sprache: DE".
