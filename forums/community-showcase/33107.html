Hi there,

So this is how to get BlitzTree and TerraED working together..

TerraED Player
------------------

Include "Inc_TerraEdLoader.bb"
Include "bt3d_include.bb" <------- You will need to stay for Aliencodec release it's or not for the moment your Trees are not at the good spot.
...
Tree_Cam=camera
..
Ter_LoadTerraMap("Demo.Ted")
LoadTreeColorMap("tree.bmp","mytreelist.txt",Terra_Map,Terra_Scale,0) <------ This one will be called BT_LoadTerraEDColorMap in the new bt3d_include.bb
..
Repeat
..
Ter_UpdateTerra(camera)
UpdateQuads()
..

Hi Kendo, send an email to Sabata (AlienHead). He not put them online yet.

We fixed them last night and they work perfect.

------------
Edit the TerraED Player.bb
----------------------------------

Include "Inc_TerraEdLoader.bb"
Include "bt3d_include.bb"
Include "blitzgrass_include.bb"
...
Tree_Cam=camera (the camera of TerraED)
TREE_SEASON=1 <--------- Season *check in your Btree and Bgrass include for more informations :-D
...
Ter_LoadTerraMap("Demo.Ted")
BT_LoadTerraEDColorMap("tree.bmp","mytreelist.txt",Terra_Map,Terra_Scale,0) <---- For Trees
GR_LoadTerraEDColorMap("grass.bmp",Terra_Map,Terra_Scale) <---- For Grass
...
Repeat
...
Ter_UpdateTerra(camera)
UpdateQuads() <----- Don't forget this function for your Trees ! (animated leaves)
...

------------
