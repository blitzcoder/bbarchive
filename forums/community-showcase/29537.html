rsbrowndog(Posted 2004) [#19]
Bad news peeps,

I am not going to continue with this Cannon Fodder clone.

Why? I sat down today to make a list of things I did and didn't want in the game and other additions I could make to it. What I ended up with wasn't really anything like Cannon Fodder anymore, only the viewpoint and controls were similar and they are standard for just about any RTS these days anyway.

Also, at the end of the day, I really didn't want to infringe on someone else's IP, even if the company that owns it is doing nothing with it whilst turning out tripe like "Pop Idol: The Game". Grrrrrr!

So watch this space (well, not this space really as I will probably start a new thread) for new shots of a revamped version of this engine, with new models, textures, etc.

Mind you it's not all bad news, hopefully this thread will give Sterling the nudge he needs to go back to work on Troopers, which looks, in a word, awesome. If you haven't checked out the trailer he linked to above then take a look!

Cheers,

Ryan
