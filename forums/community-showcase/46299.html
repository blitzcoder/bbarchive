I have taken a break from blitz for the last few months for various personal reasons, but here I am again ;)

I decided to crack on with my DeguDegu puzzle game, redoing most of it from scratch,
I have uploaded an early version for you all to test. No animation yet, no scores or anything like that,
I just want to see how it runs.

Basically get from the bottom of the screen to the top using the correct sequence (Red, Green Blue, Yellow)
In the actual finished game this sequence will be given to you briefly, a bit like the old 'simon says' game.

If you touch a 'bad degu' you will have to fight using the Degu's age old tradition of 'Rock, Paper, Scissors' I trust you all know how to play that :)

Press Space if you get stuck, C to change cameras.



You can download it here ~ 1.50 meg
