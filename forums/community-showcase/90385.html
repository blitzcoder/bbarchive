Neat graphics, how's the game work? At first glance it looks like a match-3 game or something similar, but looking closer I notice one pair of scissors cutting a sheet of paper.


Anatolie(Posted 2010) [#5]
Main task to remove all objects.
There's only three types of objects - scissors, ball of strings and paper.
You select place to drop current object. Scissors cut paper, paper removes ball, ball removes scissors ( like stone,scissors,paper game ).
There's no time limit, but may be new lines appear ( from the bottom )
