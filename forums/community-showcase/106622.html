2 Unlimited(Posted 2016) [#1]


Featuring:
- Multi way scrolling graphics
- Arcade action...except the coin slot!
- Alien patterns maybe never seen before in a shoot-em-up

INSTRUCTIONS

Each destroyed alien gives a score of 50. Some of them may require more than one hit to be destroyed. At the end of each wave a boss forestalls you and if you destroy "it", 5000 points and an extra life are awarded. Controls are LMB to shoot, RMB to move, P to pause/resume, Esc to quit. You can adjust game speed with the mouse wheel.
