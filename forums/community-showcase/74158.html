DreamsSoftGames, spanish independent games developer, presents a new project in advanced phase of development. It’s Sword Fighters, an action game and adventure in 3D with ROL components based in a mythological world of fantasy. Toth, a warrior king with angelic skills, will face the challenge of reconquering Pedriel's Kingdom of hands of the Owner of the Shades. He explores wide scenes in 3D fulfilling a series of aims or moving with freedom of action for his villages and cities, learning new movements with the sword, magic assaults, use of terrestrial and aquatic vehicles, items and potions, throwing weapon, powerful enemies and much more. You can see it in: http://www.dreamssoftgames.com
