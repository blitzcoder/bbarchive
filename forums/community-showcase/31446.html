I was transferring a load of stuff from my old computer to my new computer, when I came accross lots of old Blitz game demos I've downloaded over the years, and started wondering what had happended to some of the games I'd been really impressed with. So, in no particular order, here are the games that caught my eye.

Ollie and the Dream Keys - Not sure who made this one, but it was great fun and showed lots of potential.
Elite Multiplayer by Edzup - Always loved elite.
Winter Games Gnome Edition by Yan(I think) - International gnome hurling was great fun and I'm itching to play the other sports.
Lander by Rob Farley - The screenshots on the mental illusions site look fantastic, but not heard much about it in a while.
Simple Soccer by Matt Sephton - Loved sensi soccer and this one looked like it was going to do it proud.
Skid Marks Construction Kit by Simon A - The public beta I've got is in a pretty stable state, but again there's been no news in a long time.

I know most of the people mentioned above are still around, so come on guys lets have some progress reports.
