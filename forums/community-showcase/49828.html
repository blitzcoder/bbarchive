Hello everybody....

JumpingClown is a easy, little game. I made it for the JumpingClown-Contest in the german forum... ( http://www.blitz.inarie.de/viewtopic.php?t=299&start=0 )

You have to move the "jumping base" with the arrow keys. The Clown has to burst the ballons. The Clown jumps higher or in a other direction, when he lands on an other place on the "jumping base"!

If you have enough points, you get a place in the Onlinehighscore....

All the text are german, but I think, you should understand it ;)
