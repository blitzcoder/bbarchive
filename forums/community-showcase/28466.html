what antialiasing is that, realtime or postedited?
It has been rendered in Blitz in realtime, with no post-edit effects. If you mean the black edges around many objects, they have been drawn on the texture itself (we saw that in XIII, and gives cool results). It can't be done over all textures, because of texture tiling, but some textures that are not tiled look very good with this effect.
