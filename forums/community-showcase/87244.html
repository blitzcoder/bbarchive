Sauer(Posted 2009) [#8]
A tile based RPG offers the advantage of having a conceptually simple engine yet allowing a lot of flexibility of the final product. Also, because I'm using pre-made graphics and designed a simple UI, I can spend my time working on the story, building puzzles, and composing the music.

My overall goal is to create a smooth playing, entertaining game, even if graphically it's not the prettiest. I want it to be puzzle based but I feel it also has action potential, especially when you get ranged weapons and the enemies have more complicated AI scipts.

Thank you for the kind words, it helps motivate me to continue. Any suggestions are welcomed.
