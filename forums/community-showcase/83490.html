now i want to make the displays lcd screen... the right must to display all kind of data, and the main screen board, only speed, and car alerts.

any advice to do that, is very welcome..


The way I did it may not be the best, but worked for me. For example in my motorcycle game, there are 3 LCD numbers for the speed (0-299), so I made first 10 different textures for each LCD number (0..9), then loaded the textures in game. Then I made 3 different boxes, that I attached to the motorcycle (in your case the car of course). Then it is easy to change the textures in each little box to display the correct speed.

The other as suggested earlier is to draw the LCD screen itself in one texture, which might be easier for your game..
