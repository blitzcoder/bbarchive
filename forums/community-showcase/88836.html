JoshK(Posted 2010) [#1]
The evaluation kit for Leadwerks Engine 2.3 has been released:
http://leadwerks.com/werkspace/index.php?app=downloads&showfile=98

The evaluation kit allows saving and script editing for a period of 30 days. BlitzMax programming is not possible with the evaluation kit. An NVidia GEForce 7000 or ATI Radeon 2000 series graphics card is required. An NVidia GEForce 8800 or ATI Radeon 3870 graphics card is recommended. Please ensure you have the latest drivers for your graphics card.
