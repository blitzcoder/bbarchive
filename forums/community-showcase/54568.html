1.66e

Villagers now coming one by one during next location. After final location they are just added to scores automaticaly.
Towers Range now can be upgraded up to 12 level. (it is neccessary for 6-th location)
Added alternative Death animation for monsters. It is puddle of blood. Can be swithed only in Settings.vdd file. Must be DeathMode=1. It may be usefull for hardcore portals, while default animation(DeathMode=0) is good for kids and casual portals.
