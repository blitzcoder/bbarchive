I really only get time to code at weekends and some weekends are busy. On top of that I have 'real life' to deal with too.

Regarding is it like a real helicopter, having never flown one or indeed been in one it's hard to say. However, I'm pretty sure it's nothing like a real helicopter, not even close. However, it swoops around, and is fun. You don't control the height, thrust or anything you just rotate left/right and accelerate forwards/backwards. The control system is mind melting simple, I did this on purpose so it's not a simulation, it's a game. Too many games use exact physics and flight models and this can sometimes detract from the game playing experience, this is simple and fun.
