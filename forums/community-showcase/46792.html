agent4125(Posted 2005) [#1]
Be an invincible action hero, trashing 120 unique waves of enemies.

- 20 weapons - as an action hero, just about anything can be used as a weapon
- 10 stages - some familiar and not-so-familiar worlds to do battle in
- 120 waves - each with a unique enemy character

This is, by far, my largest project to date. This game began as an entry for the One-Button Competition on BlitzCoder back in March 2004 and I have been working on it ever since. There is still a little polishing to do, but it's essentially complete.

Enjoy! =)
