CyBeRGoth(Posted 2007) [#5]
Hehe aye thats the old DeguDegu, there are quite a few elements of that in the new game.

Had to take an extended break so its not really been two years :P

@ Genexi2, the gameplay style is a mix between Tetris, Columns and PuyoPuyo. You try to arrange a series of colours vertically to free the degu (or anything else in the tiles) as its a hexagonal map there are loads of permutations and the game gets harder as more colours are added. To help you there are some spells you can collect to extend your tile swapping range, destroy tiles etc.
