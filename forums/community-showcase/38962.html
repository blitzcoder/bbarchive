Just thought I'd post to let you all know my Dizzy 1 Remake has now been released. It was made for the Retro Remakes Competition 2004. Use to love these games when I was a kid, thought it would be a good idea to remake one for the competition.

My version has the following improvements over the original;
- Now scrolls, no more flip screen.
- Updated graphics
- Updated sound
- Extra challenge of finding 30 coins throughout the map

You can view the screenshots along with the link for download here.

Please I would love your c & c on this along with any bugs you may find! I kinda had to rush it in for the deadline so I didnt have much time to playtest it :)

I cant think you all enough for the great comments! Im really glad you like it.

I do agree with the Codemasters comments though, it would be a mighty shame if they did stop me from releasing the game. Although I did hear that the Oliver twins are not really bothered (as long as there is no profit being gained), they are quite proud that their creation is still living on!

Just to let you all know, Dizzy has been updated to version 1.02. Heres whats been changed...

CHANGE: Totally rewritten the Redefine Keys, so no more selecting the same key twice for different directions!
ADDED: Joystick support.
BUG: You no longer get stuck between the two huts next to the Grease Gun.
BUG: Fixed a strange bug where the game would crash if you killed a spider.
BUG: Birds and Bats now don't get stuck in the scenery
BUG: Corrected the timing of the gamma fades on the intro.
CHANGE: Slightly changed the spider graphics, now you can’t see the background through his eyes!
BUG: Numerous slight bugs on the audio changing, all now are hopefully gone.
