HEAS is a action computer flight simulator of a modern air combat aircraft. The game models combat in the air, on the ground and at sea. HEAS is a try of continuation of great simulations of the early '90s.

- Player-flyable aircraft: AV-8B Harrier
- Instant Action Mode
- Detailed low poly flat shaded landscape of Ullung-Do
- First simulation written in Blitz Basic





Manual:

W : Thrust up
Y : Thrust down
Cursors : Stearing
Guns: D
E: Change Weapon
View : F1 - F4
H : Cockpit on/off
S: Gear down landing/ max80 mph
