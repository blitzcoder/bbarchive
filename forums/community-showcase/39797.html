Hi all,

Just my little give back to the community, my first full game release, its called Freakbank, it's addicitive (So im told) and easy to pick up. Based on the old classic Westbank.

You play the part of bank security guard, protecting the bank from robbers, your job is to shoot anyone that pulls a gun. You collect money from the innocents which translates to a score inbetween levels. Dont be fooled, people arent always what they seem! Suicidal cops, perverts, drug barons with automatics, dodgy geezers, all make a bunch of Freaks.. hence the title. :)

You can blow peoples heads off to score higher points, the game has a high score, (no name entering) and also a basic setup file to change from fullscreen to window and 16/32bit.

It's got an original sound track by me, I did all the code, models/skins n textures... the lot basically :) Along with some valuable input from a few others.

The program has lots of inluences from code here and within the B3D demos, so without any of you guys it probably would have never happened. So I thank you all who have helped with any problems along the way.

If anyone wants to use anything graphically or would like the source for their own purposes, then I dont have a problem, as long as you let me know and drop me a credit ;)

DOWNLOAD FREAKBANK

Progs used.. PSP, Milkshape, CharacterFX, Acid and B3D
P.S Check the readme for any more details.

Regards,
Paulo
