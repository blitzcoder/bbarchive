Hi all,

I'm close to having my building demolition game ready for release. Playtesting found a few bugs, one of which meant some late nights recoding fairly core components of the framework.
End result however is better, more detailed buildings, more scope for design and faster smoother framerates.

Some videos :-
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=NtqpE8s6W3U
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tW_j7hIJ6s8
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=v10jAYd2WjI
http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Y0StMTHOjHQ

Last one is the most interesting I think, it shows the building creator and explosives editing.

Many thanks to all the people on here who have helped me, especially VIP3R and also Scribbla for his lovely explosion animations.

Oh and as is in the topic title, I changed the games name to 'detonate' from 'kaboom'. This was mainly because the more research i did on the 'kaboom' name, the more stuff came up with that I wasn't really bothered about being associated with.

Goes to show its important to research everthing before you start eh ? nevemind perhaps I'll learn for next time.

cheers,

Mark
