You know the old "hover!"?
I loved this game, but the graphics are really super-low. So i decided to make a game with the gameplay of "hover!" comic-graphics and physx engine and some other features.
It will be playable by 2 players.
Here are first screenies:

The green hover standing it its base. If you bring a flag from the enemy in your base, you'll earn one point.


A hovercraft's landing near the base. If you are falling down you need to use the parachute to protect yourself from damage.

Why "BEANS"? Well, uhm my mother thought that the hovercrafts looked like beans. Think it's a nice name for a game like that :P

Sorry no demo yet:(
Working hard on this!

Sorry, forgot about the people who don't know hover^^

First, both players will grab their flags, and place them all over the map, so that the "enemy" has to find them and get them back to his base. As a player reaches his base with a flag he will earn a credit. The first one who has got all the flags is the winner. Its a improved gameplay, in the old hover it is a bit different. Read more about hover here:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hover%21
