Loader is a small game with more focus on gameplay than, say, fancy 3D graphics. The game has a nice retro-feeling to it, and that's of course made on purpose.
Loader is set in a storage room on a space station. Though you won't see it in the game, spaceships come and go, and they deliver or pick up crates. You control a small truck, and It's your job to handle these crates. You have to get the delivered crates out of the way to make sure new ones can be delivered. You also have to make sure everybody gets the crates they want.

More info, downloads and more: http://www.snarl-up.com/showproject.asp?loader
