Al Mackey(Posted 2003) [#6]
The control scheme is like a car, but the treads do move independantly to the best approximation of the input. Typically the left hand moves the tank (either with the WASD keys or a joystick) and the right hand rotates and fires the turret (with the mouse or another joystick). PS2-like joypads with dual analog sticks work great for me, but people used to FPS-type games seem to prefer the KB/Mouse.

The first moving enemy is in.. a bumbling patrol droid.
