KEYS
------
'A' = rotate left
'X' = rotate right
'Left Cursor' = fire
'Down Cursor' = thrust
'Right Cursor' = turbo thrust (in combo with thrust)
'Space' = smart bomb

On the menu screen
--------------------------
'Space' = restart from level 0
'Return' = restart from highest level completed

Left Bar is shield energy
Right Bar is Smart Bomb energy

The Right Bar is also affected by thursting etc.

Anyway have a go there are 5 levels.

Cheers
