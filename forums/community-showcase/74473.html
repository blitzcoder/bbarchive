Hi!

I just finished this game, Match3D... which is and isn't exactly a match-3 type of game ;)

The game was made in Blitz3D, and it's basicly a cylinder that you can rotate. Left click to remove blocks (must be groups of 3 or more), right click (or drag) to rotate the cylinder.

Here's a screenshot:


Since the "pieces flying out of the screen" looked cool, I also implemented some "red-cyan glass" filter, to give it a somewhat 3D effect (of course it can be enabled or disabled)

You can all have a look at the demo here: http://match3d.icongames.com.br
