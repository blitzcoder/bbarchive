GameCoder(Posted 2004) [#6]
It worked perfectly here.

Pros:

The graphics imo were exellent. They are nice and bright and the game has a good quality cartoony feel to it. The character animations were beleivable and didn't look robotic. The control system was easy to use and it would be nice to implement joypad or joystick support. The level design was good.

Cons:

The enemies got stuck in trees or were forever stuck and trying to walk through walls. It seems like the path finding system is not coded properly. Also, some of the enemies were impervious to attack. No matter how many times I shot at them they would not flinch or seem to die. There were alot of closed doors. It would be nice to have doors that open allowing you to go in all the building. A view from the mill would have been good.



Overall the demo could have been more polished with things working properly but aside from that it's looking very promising.
