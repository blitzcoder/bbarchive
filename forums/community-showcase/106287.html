Wanted Not Alive, is a turnbased luck of the draw gunfight game, I made it for a western style RPG when I needed a shootout redolution

This is a Turn-based Western Gunfight game, the object of the game is to claim the highest bounty you can from capturing wanted cowboys before your 3 possie members are killed, .

Tour Possie are the 3 left figures from the old west, when you get into a gunfight, your Possie will engage in a gun battle with the wanted cowboy on the right.

The gun battle is simulated by a moveing slider, you have to get the slider to your opponants side on the right, Wgen a slider gets to the right you will capture your enemy and gain the reward, but if the slider gets to your side the left your possie member will die(and you only get 3 in your possie.

there are 3 types of gun bonus in the game, shotgun gets one pip on the slider , pistol get 2 pips on the slider and a rifle that gets you 3 pips head start on the slider, but your opponants also get the same bonus from their weapons too.

there is a dice at either end of the slider they oppose each other , they try to push the slider towards victoy, each shot throws both dice, then the result is applied to the slider.

All you need to know is you want to bank gold. you can also auto simulate the battle. and there are 2 plus buttons if you want to bias the slider in favour of one side or the other


Ok I did the aiming and cover badly last version, Ive totally redone and redisgned the aiming and cover, You place tiles to cover different parts and you aim at different locations you wish too.
