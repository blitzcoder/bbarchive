AntiWar is a simple shooter.
Aircrafts of evil imperialists are attacking you and your brothers. Now it's about to defend yourself.

In order to get the whole thing a bit more interesting, you can upgrade your people and rockets (e.g. stronger/faster rockets)
Controls:

Left mouse button = left turret
Right mouse button = right turret

Important note: In order to be successful, it's necessary to target aircrafts, until the crosshair turns red (about 1 sec.)
This time may be reduced when you buy an aim upgrade (rightmost).




Website + Download:
http://awar.de.vu

Please give us some feedback, either here or in our forum
