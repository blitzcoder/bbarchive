Hey everyone!

After looking over some of my older games recently, I thought it
was time to update one or two of them :)

Here is the first one, a massively updated version of Battle Pong.
(I changed the name to avoid pong clone legalities)


This is only the beta version, and does not include the online play
that the full version will(I already have the basic engine for the
online play done though), but does include a nice single player
mode.

You can choose from 4 characters to play as, each one with their
own unique stage and music(And in later versions, unique special
attacks).

(Heres 2 'Retro Hippies' Facing off.)

Use the Arrow Keys to navigate menus, Enter to select things,
and space bar to skip the opening logo sequence.

While in game, use the Up and Down Arrow Keys to move, the
Right Arrow Key to fire your standard attack which will briefly stun
your foe if it hits him, and Left Arrow Key to use whatever item
is in your inventory.


Have fun,
