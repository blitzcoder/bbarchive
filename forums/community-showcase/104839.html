I've been working for the past days on a battle engine that calculates the damage taken by every shield vs warheads impacts, and it's more confuse than i thought.

For every Captain\Ship there is a NEURAL NET for the player to evolve. Every time a player levels up he gains a ammount of points to spend on perks/abilities.

At the moment i'm just working on Shields and Warheads, so i ask for your advice on some cool names for the above perks/abilities:

SHIELDS
S.1 - adds a % of max hit points shields;
S.2 - reduces a % of the cooldown (shields will only regenerate HP after some time without beeing hit);
S.3 - adds a % of hit points regeneration;


WARHEADS
W.1 - adds a % to max warhead range;
W.2 - adds a % to warhead speed (it travels faster);
W.3 - adds a % to warhead damage;
W.4 - adds a % to hit precision;
W.5 - adds a % to critical chance;
W.6 - adds a % to critical bonus damage (shields take no critical damage).


Feel free to suggest another perks/abilities if you want.
