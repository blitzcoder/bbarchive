jhocking(Posted 2003) [#5]
The first model looks nice but very inconsistent polycount. You used very detailed cylinders for the side arms; you should redo those with less detailed cylinders. At the distance your game's viewpoint is (from the screenshot) there will be no visible difference anyway.

Also, you may want to change the texture colors to something which will stand out. For example the black side panels will just blend in with the blackness of space. Bright blue solor panels might be better.

Tranz(Posted 2003) [#12]
you could save yourself alot of texture space by overlapping the wings on that first texture.

You have eight parts that use what looks like the same texture, just overlap the UV's and you can use just one area of texture for all of those parts.

There are a several other areas that look like you could do the same.

That would leave you MORE room on the texture for important things, like a larger area for the cockpit.
