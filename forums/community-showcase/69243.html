gman(Posted 2007) [#9]
thx for the comments :) its about time i actually _did_ something! this baby has been in my head as my starter project for a few years. over the last few months my vision is really starting to come together and i am nothing less than overjoyed on how its turning out. with all the beauty of todays graphics i really wanted to capture the simplicity and charm of a bygone era and by the initial responses im hitting my target :)

im shooting for a July release but will have a playable multiplayer demo hopefully late May, early June. the final will include the must have goodies such as bouncing shots, invisible tanks, and multiple arena layouts. also (obviously) the 70s color schemes :) while the original was seemingly random colors, i will have 20 preset schemes that will randomly be selected.
