Thanks so much for trying it. I think the aerodynamics are fairly accurate. It was quite a challenge to fit your plane and enemy planes or balloons on the screen at the same time -- thus the multiple zoom levels (pageUP/pageDN). Larger screens help but I recently tried it on a friend's XP net-book and it ran fine. Forgot to mention, frame-rate is the lower left number.

As for a plane sneaking up from behind (from offscreen) and crashing into you, there is no score penalty. Good-guys and bad-guys each lose one plane (a tie) but unfortunately does require a mission restart.
