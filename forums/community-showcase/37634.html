Game Tutorial

This is Kjell - The Main Character, the "Pipe" in his hands is a Shotgun [i think]
To fire The Shotgun you have to Hit [or press] the "V" button. He is also capable of throwing grenades, by
hiting/pressing the "C" button.
If you want to hold you position and turn around to shoot enemies or whatever, press the "Z" button (you will be
able to move while not pressing this button)
Kjell will Run if you press "X" button (you will be
walking while not pressing this button)
Oh, I forgot to mention that you use the arrow keys to walk or run.
The explenation above is pretty bad, but you will understand once you try the game.


You can only walk to the next map/area by following the roads that ends on the edge of the current map.


You have to kill all enemies on the current map before you can walk to the next map/area, otherwise it would be too easy.


Find out how to get the green key, It will be too easy if i tell you how to get it. Maybe its already too easy.


To open doors you need the right key to the right door, stand as close as possible to the door and hit "B" button.
The door is the green thing on this image, it might not look like a door.. but it sure works like one.


The last thing is the Status Bar, I think you understand it pretty clearly. Above the Hearts Coins and Ammo you will see a line wich says 'Four Little Enemies' this is the name of the area. And on the Right side you will see all the Keys you have collected.
Below the little image of Kjell you see a yellow line, wich displays the Stamina you have left.
But i thought its very boring to walk when you have no stamina left(although it refills all the time) so you can
continue running even when its empty.

I hope you understand everything I wrote, and that you will try this updated demo. I dont know when/if i will finish this game. Hope you enjoy it anyway.
