Boiled Sweets(Posted 2004) [#1]
YES! Finally and with much anticipation we have released the first ever public version of our new game, RetroSphere. Download it here!

RetroSphere was inspired by the awesome C64 game Trailblazer but has been updated for the 21st century. There are now 21 track types, some good, some bad and some downright ugly!

We have also shipped the track editor (designed, build and used by us) so you too can build some wacky tracks to fox your friends. Send 'em to us for inclusion in the next release...

NB: We have had some reports from beta testers that playing RetroSphere will reduce your sleep, diminish your food intake and can cause embarrassing puddles near your PC. Be warned.
