caff_(Posted 2007) [#7]
I tried it. My initial impressions (or criticism):

1. I had no idea what I was doing, but it did remind me of the original Elite. But I can't comment on gameplay.
2. I could fly through the earth (it took about 5 minutes to get there! But I made it)
3. The grey cockpit looks a bit ugly - less cockpit and more space would be nice, and make the instrumentation fairly transparent to give it a slick feel.
4. The stars aren't very interesting.
5. Flying - when you turn, it's very rigid - most space games have a more fluid feel, like you turn gently at first, then when you stop pressing down the keys, the turning slows down.
6. Mouse control - surely this game is begging for primary mouse control. I was a bit surprised I had to use the keys. But I see you are planning to add it in - I would make it the default.
7. Earth looks quite nice at distance but not close up, maybe you can 'switch' the planets with a high res (more detailed sphere) when you get close enough.

It could be good as an online concept - I'd love to see a MMO Elite-style trading / combat game done properly. So good luck with it.
