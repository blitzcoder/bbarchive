Dear all !

It's been some days ago since my last game.

So ... the waiting is over - here comes "Snake!ing"

Background:

I always wondered how a Snake-Game is been made off ... coding and logic. So I decided to try it by myself. As the result the idea of "Snake!ing" was born.

You can download the demo here: http://www.softworxs.de/download/snakeing/snakeing_demo.exe





How to play:

When you know Snake-Games you're nearly know everything about the gameplay. But ... wait - there something new. The movement is limited and you have
to collect gold coins to complete a level.

Enjoy the game - critic is welcome :D
