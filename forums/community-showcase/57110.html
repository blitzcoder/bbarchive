Hi, i wasn't sure where to post this, so i'm sticking it here ;o) It's the beginning of my editor for the project i'm working on. This editor will allow waypoint creation, placement of entities, rotation... etc etc, give entities custom properties.

I just wanted to get some opinions about the basic controls of this.

www.rosscrooks.pwp.blueyonder.co.uk/main.bb

Oh, you use the mouse to control things :o) Click and drag with the left mouse button, to move along the X and Z axis. Click and drag with the right mouse button to mouse along the Y axis. Click and drag with the middle mouse button to move the camera.

Space bar locks the movement to an axis.
