[edit2]Very interesting find here. Turns out the fullscreen problems were being caused by a bug in version 1.39 of B+. I use a created image for my collision layer and with 1.39 when you switch to full the entire image gets filled with garbled pixels, that's why the character would rise to the top of the screen. I downgraded to 1.37 and everything is back to normal now, even the scrolling seems smoother. Please see if the updated zip fixes your problems Gen.
