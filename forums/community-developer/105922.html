I wonder if those interested in "sound" are aware of Resonic software...

I came across the Resonic Player (freeware) in my search for a "music manager" that would not immediately scour my hard drive for MP3 files, build a library, favourites, playlists, online database search etc.

The Resonic Player simply has an old-style split-pane file-tree interface, and support for many file types. Perfect.

Resonic Pro (which I have no experience of) offers itself as a "powerful sample manager / browser and creativity tool for audio professionals"

Because the freeware is so good, I thought maybe the pro software was worth a mention...
