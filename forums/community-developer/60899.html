3D World Studio version 5.48 has been released. This fixes a few minor issues, and adds a "Run Map" menu item, so you can export a .b3d and launch it in Blitz3D straight from the editor.

You can download the new version here:
http://www.leadwerks.com/index.php?page=downloads.htm

July will be the last month for Cartography Shop users to get the discounted upgrade to 3D World Studio.
