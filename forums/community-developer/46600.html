if you''re not especially after havok for blitzX and more after how to get a version of havok in a more or less cheap way than you do have two chances:

- In Macromedia director there is/was havok support included (since version 8.5). MM did not renew their licence for the actual version. Anyway it still works but this is not an actual version of havok - no havok 2 (so slower, no softbodies, ...).
