jhocking(Posted 2006) [#2]
First off, I assume you mean "b3d" not "w3d"

Second, b3d does not support any particle effects, so unless your fog effect is done as/converted to an animation style that b3d does support (either heirarchy or skeletal animation) then you can't export that.

To do particle effects in Blitz you need a particle system. You can download one from the Toolbox to plug into your code.
