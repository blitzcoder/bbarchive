Lightwave Scene Exporter to .X

Community Forums/Developer Stations/Lightwave Scene Exporter to .X

nuclearglory(Posted 2004) [#1]
A new plugin to export your Layout Scene directly to an optimized .X file. It preserves smoothing, UV's, and even copies the needed textures to the same folder with your output .X file.

The output .x files are in the 8.1 format.

This tool plugs right into Layout and is not an external converter.
