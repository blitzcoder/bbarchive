I think its worth getting FragMotion regardless. These are my reasons:

a) its very inexpensive
b) it supports B3D well
c) its still supported (CharacterFX isn't)
d) it lets you do lots of other things: mesh painting etc.

It's probably best to keep using it, ask some questions on the forum. If you prefer it to CharacterFX then switch over.
