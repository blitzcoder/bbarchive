c5ven(Posted 2004) [#2]
iirc Rhinoman is just a plug-in meant originally to export to BMRT (the Renderman knock-off that everyone loved). when BMRT was alive, Rhino v2 had a direct link to it for rendering. since the developers have since added Flamingo to Rhino (a slooooow renderer from all reports) it's discussed much less on design forums.

there's something called Bongo which is a keyframe animation add-on that's supposed to be in the works. nothing you'd want for games. Rhino is very much an industrial design tool (alot of people in my line of business use it) and i believe Bongo is intended to show simple anims for design presentations.

Rhino's more like a low cost Alias Studio modeler than a Maya. nice program. great bang/$. excellent support for CAD/CAM applications. but doubt a B3D exporter would help many people.
