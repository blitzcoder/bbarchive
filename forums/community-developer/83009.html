For individuals and companies with less than 2 people.

It's a great node based shader editor for 3dsmax which supports HLSL and CG. We use it with the Ofusion exporter to export shaders to ogre. Very good indeed if your an artist who isn't into coding, or anyone that wants to get a general shader library made quickly.
