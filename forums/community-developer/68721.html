fredborg(Posted 2007) [#2]
Negative scale in 3dsmax is my guess. Try applying a Reset XForm and then export again.

If it is already skinned you need to save out the envelopes from the skin modifier. Delete the skin modifier. Reset XForm. Apply new skin modifier, and load the envelopes again. Save/Load envelopes is located in the Advanced Parameters rollout of the skin modifier.
