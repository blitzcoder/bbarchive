What version of 3ds Max are you using?

Try grabbig B3d pipeline http://www.onigirl.com/pipeline/index.html and use the material selections supported within that to add the colours, then export as a .b3d model. This is the best art pipeline from Max into B3d.

To load into B3d simply change the load code in your .bb from .x or .3ds to .b3d.
