Ricky Smith(Posted 2004) [#2]
Its great for easy-listening accompaniment - you would need to get the mega version with all the style disks to get the best selection of styles. You can use as many styles as you want within a song and its very flexible.You can create your own styles too or even create a style from a midi file.
Jammer Pro does better solos and drum-breaks but BB is much more versatile and easier to use overall and has lots more styles.
