Well, assuming that the sound is not too different sounding at the beginning and end, you just have to zoom in on the sound and clip it at the beginning and end where the sound crosses the 0 line. Also if the beginning starts at 0 and goes up, then you want the end to start below 0 and go up to 0, so that you have a continuous wave.
