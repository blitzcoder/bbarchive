Depending on the scale of the assets you want to design, and assuming they're "engineered" (i.e. not people or other organic stuff) it should be pretty good. Certainly not on the level of a professional suite with full lighting control etc. etc. but it should make up for that somewhat by being CRAZY easy to use by comparison :0)

If you want to go heavy duty though I'm a fan of Blender (blender.org). All the muscle, none of the cost... but with a lot of head scratching to get started.
