jhocking(Posted 2004) [#5]
It usually helps to first select all verts and assign them to the root bone to make sure you have everything attached to the skeleton. After that, yeah, make sure "ignore backfaces" is off (it's in the right click menu I think) and assign vertices to the bones you actually want them attached to.
