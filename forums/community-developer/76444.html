Ricky Smith(Posted 2008) [#2]
Hi siread,
You can load the low poly model and then 'rip' both the skeleton and the animation from the high poly model. You just then need to re-assign the vertices to the new skeleton.
For low poly models you don't always need to use vertex weighting - you can try just assigning one bone per vertex and then make minor adjustments later if required. You can also start of by using the 'Assign to nearest' button after selecting all vertices and then just reassign those vertices that don't look right.
Vertex assignment is probably the most difficult part of skinning and quite a bit of practise is required.
