To be honest, I always had UV problems with Lee's LW converter. My suggestion is Ultimate Unwrap. In LW I export in .X format (using FUI's free exporter) load it up in Ultimate Unwrap and export it to .B3D format. It's a little bit of a pain but at least the UVs stay intact that way.

As Scribbla says, if you're having UV probs then best to keep the skellys out and triangulate the model before saving. I've found working on one copy, then exporting a second when you want to convert the best practice.

The .LWO format is pretty complicated (It holds a lot more information than most Model formats I've come across) and UV information is in a particularly 'Far out' format.
