I'm a huge fan of Wings3D for modeling but I know little about either anim8or or Quill (in the former case it doesn't run on my system and in the latter case I was never interested in its modeling abilities, only other stuff.) Do note however that you cannot texture map in Wings; I highly recommend you get Ultimate Unwrap no matter which tool you use to model since it has very robust b3d support (including import of animated b3d models) and that is a fantastic tool for texture mapping.
