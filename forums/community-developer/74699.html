jhocking(Posted 2007) [#8]
I have CharacterFX and it works great for animating b3d files; I've used it extensively for lots of projects. Certainly the fact that it isn't actively supported doesn't mean it suddenly is no good any more. It still runs just fine, saves b3ds just fine, and I still favor the Keyframer window in CharacterFX over equivalent tools in other animation software.

That all said, if I were buying something right now I'd probably go with Fragmotion. Although I do not regret at all buying CharacterFX, that was like 4 years ago so it's had plenty of time to be useful to me. If, say, the software stops working in the next update to Windows, with Fragmotion that would probably be fixed, but with CharacterFX you're just shit out of luck.
