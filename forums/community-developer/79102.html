XSI 7, no more Foundation.

Community Forums/Developer Stations/XSI 7, no more Foundation.

Reactor(Posted 2008) [#1]
XSI 7 has been announced for those who weren't aware, and the new ICE tool looks quite amazing. However, the cheapest Foundation version is being discontinued. So, that leaves $3k for Essentials and $5k for Advanced (which I doubt too many game devs will need).

The good news is, Foundation users can upgrade to Ess for about $1000. The offer is good until the end of September. Considering what's included, that's a huge bargain, and well worth taking advantage of if you're a Foundation user.
