jhocking(Posted 2004) [#2]
I don't know of any tutorials off the top of my head but don't just search for Milkshape specific tutorials. Creating low pol trees in Milkshape is the same as creating low pol trees in pretty much any other tool, so search generally.

The gist is to use simple solid geometry for the trunk, like a vertex manipulated cylinder, then do the branches and leaves as masked/alpha textures of leaf clusters on quads.
