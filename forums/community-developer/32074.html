Garion(Posted 2004) [#2]
In the material editor colour selection box, right click and choose the purple Texture Map image with Caligari written on it. This loads a place holder image for yer texture.

Click the little red triangle in the 'Colour' properties and then load yer texture from there.

The second texture can be done as a material rectangle (a decal)

Load the texture as before, then choose 'Material Rectangle' from the UV options.

I hope this is of help to yoo.

Garion
