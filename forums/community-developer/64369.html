AdrianT(Posted 2006) [#9]
The SDK was frozen entirely since V6 till now, the new SDK and has largely been rewritten and includes a 64bit version.

It has a lot of speed optimizations, especialy with really high poly scenes since previous versions it refreshed the entire scene when you modified it, now it just refreshes the vertices you are moving.

As far as I can see its mostly speed optimizations, but it does mean that it is MUCH faster in viewports than it used to be with large scenes.
