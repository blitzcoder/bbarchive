Community Forums/Developer Stations/Cartography Shop 4.1 released

JoshK(Posted 2004) [#1]
CShop 4.1 is ready. This fixes a problem that Intel and some other built-in cards had with the perspective view. It also uses an installer instead of just a zip, so people will be less likely to extract it to their CD-ROM and wonder why it doesn't work. New features include the reimplementation of constructive solid geometry, addition of "vis groups", and support for export of Half-Life .map and .b3d models.

Upgrades will be available some time today at cartographyshop.thegamecreators.com.
