Started work on a MP3 file manager / ID3 tag editor a while back for my own needs and now have got it to a stage where I am happy to realise it to the world. It's not Blitz but sits nicely along side my various Blitz games and screen savers. No it's not a rivel to iTunes or the awesome Tag & Rename but it does have some really cool features that those applications don't have. If you don't like it then fine, just thought I'd share with you...

Moose is a free utility to help manage your MP3 files.

Moose is like lots of other MP3 file managers, it allows you to edit the ID3 tags held in the MP3 file. However unlike most other MP3 managers it can also separate your MP3s out into \Artist\Album folders!

Moose is ideal for those iTunes power users that don't want iTunes to auto manage their collections. Moose gives you the power to control your precious MP3 library!

Moose is constantly in development so feel free to request new features. We'll do our best to add requested features...
