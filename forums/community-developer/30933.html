Wayne(Posted 2004) [#6]
Here is the feature list.

SDS modelling, with sharp edges.
Symmetrical meshes.
Blob modelling, with ellipse, 3D splines, 3D meshes.
2D/3D curves: mix of straight lines and splines, Read TTF font, Boolean operations.
Toolboxes on meshes, faces, edges and vertices, texture projection.
Four Texture projectors: plane, cylinder, sphere and uv.
Procedural texture generated by scripts.
Morphing and skinning.
Export to: 3DS, OBJ, Direct X, DXF, VRML, Virtual Light, SunFlow.
Import from: 3DS, LWO, OBJ, VRML, DXF.
Export to POV, RENDERMAN. ·
All scene data (meshes, animation, texture...) saved in one '.3de' file.
