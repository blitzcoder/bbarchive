Before B3D Pipeline, the established method of getting from 3dsMax to Blitz3D, was to export to Ultimate Unwrap using the 3dsMax plugins available from the UU site, then convert from U3D to B3D in UU. It won't have support for the extension stuff that Pipeline had, but it did support skeletal animation, vertex normals, both UV channels and I think you could set up brush properties and texture layers in UU.
