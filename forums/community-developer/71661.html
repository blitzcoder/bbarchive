AdrianT(Posted 2007) [#11]
you use bakersfield to make lightmapping in max easier. B3D pipeline won't work with max 9 and I havent used B3D in over a year.

Adding lightmaps in B3D pipeline is simple, you just add an extra texture layer with the lightmap on, and set your blendmodes. B3D pipeline has its own Max material type that supports most if not all B3D fixed function material functions.

The only alternative to B3D pipeline is Unwrap3D, which I havent used for exporting for several years now.
