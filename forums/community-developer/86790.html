Ricky Smith(Posted 2009) [#4]
You can use Pacemaker to save, load and share animation between models with similar skeletons.

http://www.goddysey.com/pacemaker.htm

If you want to do it in real-time you just load both models, hide & animate the source model and then copy the joint rotations to the other/visible one.
