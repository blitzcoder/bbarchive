chi(Posted 2009) [#4]
hi kryzon,

1.) paint vertexalpha within vertexpaint-modifier
2.) click "Tools" and select "Channel Info..."
3.) rightclick ID 0:vc -> copy
4.) rightclick ID -2:Alpha -> paste
5.) add b3dbrush to your model and tick "vertexcolors" and "vertexalpha"
6.) export and have fun ;)


cheers, chi
