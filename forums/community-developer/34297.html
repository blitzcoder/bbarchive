fredborg(Posted 2004) [#5]
To avoid confusion:

Here's how to get an ALE landscape to work in gile[s]!

1: Load your landscape
2: Delete the model 'Land_7'
3: Delete the material 'Brush7'
4: Open the 'Lighting Methods' window
5: Set the 'Default lightmap' to use 'Flat Packing' and disable 'Average polygon normal'.
6: For 'Brush2' through 'Brush6' disable 'Cast Shadows' and 'Affect Global Illumination'
7: Use 'Recalculate Normals' from the 'Tools' menu.
8: Add your lights and other objects. For example some nifty FONText slogan :)
9: Render.
