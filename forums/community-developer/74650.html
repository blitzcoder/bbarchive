puki(Posted 2007) [#21]
This thing is really very low-poly. Many of the buildings are just cubes of different sizes and dimensions.

There is a lot of variety.

There is also a variety of the texturing quality - some buildings are clearly better looking than others.

There are 3 pre-made city sections. The one I pictured is the larger of the 3.

The buildings all have complete roofs, some with extra detail - meaning you could fly over the cities - ie in a helicopter, etc.

There are errors in the pre-made cities though - some buildings seem half-buried in the ground - I've seen some buildings out of position (ie half inside another building) - nothing major though (you'd have to look hard) - plus, it can all be fixed.
