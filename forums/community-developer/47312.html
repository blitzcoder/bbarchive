After 8 months of development and tons of input we have completed and released TreemagikG3.

Low poly - high quality 3D tree creation has never been easier! TreemagikG3 introduces new breakthroughs in the tree creation industry! With TreemagikG3 you'll be able to create some of the most beautiful 3d Trees we've ever seen.. And all within seconds.

Based on four years of 3D tree mesh creation tools, TreemagikG3 boasts a feature list unlike any other. Design and shape tree trunks down to the very vertice, import branches and limbs into your creation then cover the joints in leaves! It's that simple. TreemagikG3 also allows the designer to import and mold root system on the base of their tree creations.
