Ricky Smith(Posted 2006) [#5]
Yes PaceMaker can do this - even if the proportions of the skeletons are different - as long as the joints are named the same you can share animation between different models.

Using the Skeleton and Sequence Import/Export feature in PaceMaker 1.34( wmv 20mb)
