The answer will be "no", for many reasons:

1) its illegal to use gmax for non licensed games.
2) its very difficult to do exporters for it (or at least very different to doing them in 3Dsmax).
3) b3dpipeline (the great b3d exporter for 3Dsmax) is a big project and is no longer in development.
