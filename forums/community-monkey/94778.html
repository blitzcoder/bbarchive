For those not on the monkey forum, I have released add-ons for two popular editors to enable Monkey support.

They support syntax colouring, keyword expansion and completion and building/compilation. TextMate supports a few more things such as context sensitive help.

TextMate (Mac OS X)
- this also works with E! editor on Windows, and others
https://github.com/gingerbeardman/monkey.tmbundle

Sublime Text 2 (Windows, Mac OS X, Linux)
https://github.com/gingerbeardman/monkey.sublime

More information at the links above.

Let me know what you think.
matt
