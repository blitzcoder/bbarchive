This is our first game developed with Monkey and available for Android.

SANTA NEEDS HELP



HISTORY

Santa Claus needs your help!

Santa's helpers want extra money. They have stolen some gifts from Santa Claus to resell in ElfBay.

Your mission is to recover all the gifts, so no one is left without gifts on Christmas Eve.

Armed with countless snowballs, and with your aim skills, you must try to truncate their intentions.

Enjoy the Christmas Spirit with this fun time management game.



BONUS

To advance in the game, you must pay attention to the bonus. Green items add extra seconds to your game and golden items freeze the time, giving you a break ... It may seem simple, but the elfs are agile and fast, and you must demonstrate your skills dropping snowballs.

Hours and hours of fun guaranteed for all.
