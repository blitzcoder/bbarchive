Never even got as far as the iOS simulator - I dread to think how slow that is. Some people have also reported bugs in compiled programs, so it might not be a good option for compiling cross-platform code either.

If you intend to make money from your Xcode projects, save yourself the trouble. Better to get a cheap Mac off eBay or from a refurbished-Mac store.
