therevills(Posted 2012) [#19]
explaining what the advantage


Heres a few:

* DirectX Target
* Proven stable Windows and Mac Target
* Linux supported (Monkey does not offically support Linux)
* Debugger
* Easy to add code to change resolution/fullscreen etc

With GLFW you can not go between Window and Fullscreen modes and GLFW is just OpenGL.
