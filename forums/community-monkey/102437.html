Hello!

I recently released my first game on Android and IPhone. I originally wrote the game in Java intending to port it to Android, while working on the Android port, I remembered MonkeyX and decided to give it a try. It worked even better than I imagined!

The game pits you against the enemy, either playing as Sammy the Slammer or as Bella the Basher! You control the speed of your puppy with one button and the elevation of the bone with another button, trying to match the random speed and elevation at the time of the impact! The closer you are to matching the two values, the better chance you have of winning. If you match the speed and bone elevation perfectly, you will knock your opponent off their cat mount!

The game stores your statistics and will load them into the highscore board hosted at YellzBellz Puppy Jousting High Scores!

You can see the video at Puppy Jousting on YouTube!

You can get the Android game at the Google Play Store!

You can get the game at the Apple App Store!

If anyone can leave me some feedback, input or face bashing, I would really appreciate it!

Thanks!
