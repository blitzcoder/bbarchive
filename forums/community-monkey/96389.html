Matty(Posted 2011) [#27]
If I can give a little advice, that maybe you already know, but from my experience with the Android market - releasing a game that is relatively unknown will do better in terms of downloads if it is released early on a Saturday morning so that it has the entire weekend in the "Just In" list...there are many more people who search for 'games' and similar on a weekend than there are during the week. (EG have a look on "google insights" and look at the trend of searching for 'games' over the past 30/90 days - definite trend there).

Also - your game title is very important. For those people who do searches for games if the title has a relevant word in it then it will show up at the top of lists a lot more often...

Hope you don't mind these tips (you may already be aware of them).

Also - an 'update' to your android app will only go back into the 'just in' list if at least 8 days have elapsed since the previous update. If you do update it before that time it won't appear in the just in list again...not unless you update it again outside of that 8 day range.

from Matt
