@Blitzplotter:Congratulations on joining the simian brigade. What ever you do remember that mobile devices aren't PC or Game Consoles. So don't expect something that you have written for an xbox to work out of the box on a iPhone.
And do use image atlas's so as not to have too may performance hits.
And be aware that there is a gap of silence when looping MP3's so you'll have to find a tool to remove it (there a couple of topics on monkeycoder.co.nz about it) and also be aware that at the moment data loading and saving is done as a string.
Otherwise have fun.
