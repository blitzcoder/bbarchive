Yasha(Posted 2011) [#9]
I wouldn't even call Monk an IDE. It's a text editor which happens to have an integrated compile button.

Coding with a proper IDE (that manages your project and actually bothers to read your code rather than just highlighting individual words) is quite a different endeavour and takes a bit of a mental switchover.

So it also depends quite a bit on what you want from your tool, and how much you want done for you vs. a box to type in and feed text straight to a compiler. Many people prefer the second.
