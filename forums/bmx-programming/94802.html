col(Posted 2011) [#8]
Works on this :-

SuperStrict

Local M:TMap = CreateMap()

MapInsert( M , "Key1" , "Value1" )
MapInsert( M , "Key2" , "Value2" )
For Local n:TNode = EachIn M 
	Local key:Object = n.Key()
	Local value:Object = n.Value()
	
	Print "Key :- " + String(key)
	Print "Value :- " + String(value)
Next
