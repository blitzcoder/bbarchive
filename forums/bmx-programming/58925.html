	Function ShellExecuteA (s1:Int, s2:Byte Ptr, s3:Byte Ptr, s4:Byte Ptr, s5:Byte Ptr, s6:Int)

Const SW_SHOW = 5
?

ShellExecuteA (Null,Null, "www.webappstogo.com", Null, Null, SW_SHOW);

Synchronise the mods.
Restart the IDE.
