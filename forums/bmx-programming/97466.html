Easiest route is to just go windowed rather than full screen. If you go windowed under maxgui you can position the window wherever you like. There's no inbuilt support for multiple montiors. There area few modules that will let you get info on additional screens, but I *think* you would have to modify the graphics driver module to let it target other screens...
