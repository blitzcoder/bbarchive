You'll probably need a graphics canvas

canvas:TGadget=CreateCanvas( etc ...

Look at the example program for CreateCanvas() it shows you how to add a canvas to the window and animate it. Just remember you have to call Flip() and also RedrawGadget(canvas) to see the results.

Then, anything you can do in Max2D or GL/DX you should be able to do with your logo.
