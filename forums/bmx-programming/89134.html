Otus(Posted 2010) [#3]
Local f:Float '= blah
Local arr:Byte[4]
Float Ptr(arr)[0] = f

Edit: Oh, or the string version:
Local f:Float '= blah
Local s:String = String.FromBytes(VarPtr f, 4)
