You could use Rnd() to offset the images current location by a small amount

const RANGE#=2.0
Global ox#,oy#
Global imgX#=320 , imgY#=240

Repeat
 ox=Rnd(-RANGE,RANGE) ; oy=Rnd(-RANGE,RANGE)
 DrawImage myimage,imgX+ox,imgY+oy
Until KeyHit(KEY_ESCAPE)
