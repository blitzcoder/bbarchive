Dance ejay and ejay programs can do this. You can create from some small sample wav files or loops and then connect them to create a complete music.
Also Fruity loops does this too.

The idea to do this with blitzmax , is to create some interface with audio tracks.
Your tracks will be pieces of small seamless wav files and then play them continuously and then render them as a single merged wav file.

If you have access to a keyboard , you can record some notes or instruments to create the wav samples.
