xlsior(Posted 2014) [#4]
Keep in mind that mailto: only works on PC's that have a local email client installed & configured -- something that's no longer true on the majority of computers, where people use hotmail/gmail/yahoo mail/whatever these days...

In which case the mailto: link will result in an error stating that you have no mail program configured. You may want to list a support email explicitly in your program for those people.
