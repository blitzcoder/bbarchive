BLIde Professional Publisher is out!
What does this application do?

-This application will let you Build you BlitzMax Applications, adding them a ICON with a single CLICK!

-It icludes also a Photoshop and PaintShopPro Plug in that will let you 'Save As ICO'

-It also includes the NRV compression algorithm that will let you decrease the size of your final executables. Last test made: a 14MBytes EXE was reduced to 1.21 MBytes

For more information, purchasing or downloading trial, please go to http://www.blide.org/nueva/addons.htm

What do you need to use this application?

-BlitzMax

-BLIde (0.5.0 or greater)

-MinGW

-Photoshop, PaintShopPro or any compatible application if you want to use the ICO Plug In.

All the benefits of the sales of this application will be destined to BLIde Professional Publisher development, and to BLIde development.
