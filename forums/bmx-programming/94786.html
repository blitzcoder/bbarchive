SpaceAce(Posted 2011) [#4]
You have to download MaxGUI separately from BlitzMax. Check the "Account"/"Product updates" area of this website for the MaxGui downloads. Also, check http://sourceforge.net/projects/maxgui/ for the latest-latest MaxGUI files. Make sure the maxgui.mod folder is in your BlitzMax mod folder.

I just compiled imagedrop.bmx on my (Windows 7) machine, and got no errors. I have the latest BlitzMax installation and MaxGUI 1.40.
