To clarify:

BLIde is a free IDE for BlitzMax, It's free and it'll be free for ever and ever. You don't have to pay anything to use it. Then' there's BLIde Professional Publisher. This application, in combination with BLIde, the BlitzMax compiler and the MinGW, enables the integration of icons in your blitzMax application. they're different applications. So on application is free (BLIde) , the other one isn't (BLIde Professional Publisher).

BLIde: Integrated devolpment environment for BlitzMax (FREE APPLICATION) http://www.blide.org
