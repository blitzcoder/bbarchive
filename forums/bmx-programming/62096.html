Mordax_Praetorian(Posted 2006) [#3]
Just use MS Paint and keep at it

Zoom in at 800%, press CTRL+G to activate the grid when you need it, press CTRL+F to get a quick look at your work zoomed out, and any key to go back to normal

Use the Pencil tool for most things, if you need to make things look symmetrical, copy/paste and then right click and Flip/Rotate -> Flip Horizontal/vertical

Keep drawing and colouring people from various angles, it wont be long till you notice yourself improving
