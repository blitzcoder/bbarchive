Brucey(Posted 2007) [#2]
Alpha blending is to do with transparency, rather than anti-aliasing.

As far as I know, DX7 doesn't do line anti-aliasing, but you cen enable it in OpenGL with "glEnable GL_LINE_SMOOTH".
