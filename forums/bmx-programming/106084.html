There are a few things I really don't understand in the world. Hypnosis, Epilepsy, and the Higgs Boson theory.

Epilepsy is certainly an unusual condition. Through a series of flashing lights and sometimes just pictures, people can and have gone into seizures.

I know I was 7-years old. I remember this well. I was with my Mom, the B&W portable TV was on the ground, we were getting ready to put it up on a table to see it better when I looked at it and there a scene of a man getting ready to dive into the water from a diving board.

The camera was facing away from the diver. The diver dove away from the camera and the camera followed him into the water with an inaudible splash (as the sound was turned down).

For some reason my brain just didn't like what it saw, and I had, as my Mom explained to me, a seizure because of it. For a full minute she said I was moaning - like I was angry. finally I returned back to awareness and realized I had drool down my face and my bottom lip was bleeding from biting it.

Nothing else has seemed to trigger this. So - to this day it is a mystery to me.
