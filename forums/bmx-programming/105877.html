As the remarks below say, I entered this in a competition to make the best effect of a fire. I downloaded some of the early entrants to see they were painstakingly working on tedious pixels to create a very slow and small illusion of fire.

While their fire may have looked more believable, it was horrendously lagging on the PC at the time. So I decided to experiment with drawing lines on the screen that were colored like fire.

The result, a much faster and full-screen fire effect and in minimal coding too. No need to use massive calculations involving pixel arrays like the other guys were doing.

Enjoi !
