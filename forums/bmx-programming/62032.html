Hi Guys,

Long time no post :) I hope everyone is well. I'm terribly busy, one of the reasons, is the subject of my post here.

I'm in the process of writing a book dealing with game programming using BlitzMax. It will leverage my experience working with BlitzMax and my experience as a professional game programmer in the Australia and US games industry.

The book is scheduled to be published somewhere between Q4 this year and Q1 next year worldwide. So! Why do I post here? Well there are two reasons:

1) Each of the chapters of my book needs a screenshot from extacly one BliztMax game/application. The best screenshots will be chosen from those submitted to grace each chapter.

2) There is a chapter in my book called "Game Developer Case Studies". This is where BlitzMax developers get to talk about how they went about making their games with BlitzMax.

What's the payoff? This is what you get:

- Your name in the contributors list at the front of the book
- Your game and your website/details in the book being published worldwide
- Glory? I don't know... kudos or something :)

So what can you send me(read carefully below)?

- Screenshot contributors (completed or non-completed game/app in progress)
- One per submitter please
- PNG screenshot of your game
- Your name/Company name
- Your website/Details
- Everything in zip file

OR

- Case study submitters (Only fully finished games/projects please)
- One per submitter please
- PNG screenshot of your game
- Your name/Company name
- Your website/Details
- Everything in zip file
- Questions:
- Are your Indie, Solo or Professional?
- How many people worked on the project?
- How long did it take you to develop your game/app?
- How much money did you spend?
- Who did the design, story, programming, art, sound, music and QA?
- Why BlitzMax?
- If applicable: How was your cross-platform experience with BlitzMax?
- Any previous projects?
- Are you published?
- If you could change anything about the way you developed your game, what would it be?
- One final question: What are quibbles? (Make up an inventive answer)

Email your submissions to: tommybear2@...
