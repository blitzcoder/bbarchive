No really simple answer I am afraid.
For Local x:Int= 1 To 50
	StandardIOStream.WriteString x
	If x =25 StandardIOStream.WriteString "~n"

Next


or
For Local x:Int= 1 To 50
	WriteStdout x
	If x =25 WriteStdout "~n"

Next
