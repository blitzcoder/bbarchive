Now my stupid question

_CameraBackColor(mcamera, $222222)

How does $222222 equate to rgb values.

Or put another way how would I convert, for example, R=100, G=200, B=255 into the above format.


therevills(Posted 2010) [#17]
$222222 is a hex value.

You need to convert your decimal value into hexadecimal.

Red: 100 (decimal) = 64 (hex)

Green: 200 (decimal) = C8 (hex)

Blue: 255 (decimal) = FF (hex)

So in total it will be:
$64C8FF
