No offense intended, but let's look at the priorities. BRL only has a limited amount of man-power. If they are going to set the demo up to syncmod they have to either a) figure out a way in one process to separate the stuff that will be synced on a demo version from a full sync, or b) construct a completely new and separate process to sync demos. Given that many of us are breathlessly awaiting the release of MaxGUI and Max3D, what would you have that limited manpower spent on?
