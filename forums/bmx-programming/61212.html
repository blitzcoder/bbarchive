Pyroplay's Dev Corner releases PPBlitzSpell v1.0.
PPBlitzSpell is a free spell checking and dictionary management module for BlitzMax.

PPBlitzMax features:
- Easy spell checking
- Word suggestions for misspelled words
- Load and save personal dictionarys
- Session dictionary
- and more

The example in the download archive offers a MaxGUI textarea with funtional spell checking and word suggestions via popupmenu.
